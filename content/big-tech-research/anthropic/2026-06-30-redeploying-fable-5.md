**[Redeploying Fable 5](https://www.anthropic.com/news/redeploying-fable-5)** — _Anthropic · Jun 30_

**Main takeaway:** After US export controls on June 12 forced Anthropic to suspend all user access to Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5, the controls were lifted June 30 and Fable 5 returned globally on July 1; the post also outlines a proposed industry-wide jailbreak severity scoring framework co-developed with Amazon, Microsoft, Google, and Glasswing partners, and announces deeper US government collaboration on pre-release testing.

**Main methods:**
- **Why the suspension happened.** On June 12 the US government applied export controls after Amazon researchers reported a method of bypassing Fable 5's safeguards — specific prompting caused the model to identify software vulnerabilities, including one case where it produced code demonstrating how a vulnerability could be exploited.
- **Return timeline.** Export controls lifted June 30; Fable 5 available from July 1 on Claude Platform, Claude.ai, Claude Code, and Claude Cowork, covering up to 50% of weekly usage limits through July 7, then via usage credits. AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Foundry access being restored as quickly as possible. Mythos 5 restored for select US organizations after government approval on June 26.
- **Jailbreak severity framework.** Together with Amazon, Microsoft, Google, and Glasswing partners, Anthropic is developing an industry-wide standard for assessing jailbreak severity — intended to help AI developers triage new findings consistently, launch highly capable models with greater safety, and communicate risk levels coherently to government and industry.
- **Safeguard approach.** Anthropic uses safety classifiers to detect potentially dangerous cybersecurity uses of its models; the post details updates made to these classifiers in response to the bypass report.
- **Deeper government collaboration.** Strengthened post-incident collaboration includes new pre-release testing protocols, information sharing arrangements, and formal research collaboration with the US government.

**[Fable 5 重新上线](https://www.anthropic.com/news/redeploying-fable-5)** — _Anthropic · 6月30日_

**Main takeaway:** 6 月 12 日美国政府对 Claude Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 实施出口管制，Anthropic 随即暂停了所有用户的访问。管制于 6 月 30 日解除，Fable 5 从 7 月 1 日起全球重新上线。这篇文章还公布了 Anthropic 联手 Amazon、Microsoft、Google 和 Glasswing 合作方共同推进的 jailbreak 严重性评分框架，以及与美国政府的深度协作计划。

**Main methods:**
- **为什么会被暂停。** 6 月 12 日，Amazon 研究人员向政府报告了一种绕过 Fable 5 safeguard 的方式——通过特定提示让模型识别软件漏洞，其中一个案例还生成了展示 exploit 的代码。政府随即对两款模型下了出口管制令，Anthropic 因为没有实时核验国籍的办法，只好对所有用户一并暂停访问。
- **重新上线的时间线。** 管制 6 月 30 日解除；Fable 5 从 7 月 1 日起在 Claude Platform、Claude.ai、Claude Code、Claude Cowork 全球恢复，7 月 7 日前每周用量限额内可用 50%，之后转为 usage credits。AWS、Google Cloud、Microsoft Foundry 正在尽快恢复。Mythos 5 则在 6 月 26 日获政府批准后，向部分美国机构恢复访问。
- **行业级 jailbreak 严重性框架。** Anthropic 拉上 Amazon、Microsoft、Google 和 Glasswing 合作方，正在制定一套行业通用标准来评估 jailbreak 的严重程度——目标是帮助各家 AI 开发者快速 triage 新发现、更安全地发布高能力模型，也能与政府和行业伙伴统一沟通风险等级。
- **safeguard 机制。** Anthropic 用 safety classifier 检测潜在的网络安全危险用途；文章详述了他们在收到绕过报告后是如何更新这套 classifier 的。
- **与美国政府的深度协作。** 包括新的发布前测试流程、信息共享机制，以及正式研究合作——这是这次出口管制事件之后 Anthropic 与政府关系的结构性升级。
