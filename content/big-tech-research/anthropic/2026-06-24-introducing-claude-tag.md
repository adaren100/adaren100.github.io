**[Introducing Claude Tag](https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-claude-tag)** — _Anthropic · Jun 22_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic launched Claude Tag, letting teams add @Claude as a member of Slack channels to delegate tasks; internally, 65% of Anthropic's product team's code is now created via the internal version of this feature.

**Main methods:**
- **Slack as the first home.** Claude joins selected Slack channels as a team member, with access to chosen tools, data, and codebases; anyone in the channel can tag @Claude to delegate work.
- **Multiplayer, persistent context.** There's one Claude per channel that everyone shares, so anyone can see ongoing work and pick up where the last person left off, and Claude builds context over time instead of starting from scratch each time.
- **Ambient/proactive behavior.** If enabled, Claude proactively surfaces relevant updates, flags information from connected channels and tools, and follows up on stalled threads or tasks without being asked.
- **Asynchronous and self-scheduling.** Claude can work on tasks over hours or days and schedule its own follow-up tasks, freeing users to focus elsewhere while it works.
- **Direct messages supported.** Users can also DM Claude for private responses using their own personal tools and connectors.
- **Availability.** Live today in beta for Claude Enterprise and Team customers, with plans to expand beyond Slack to other workplace tools.

**[Anthropic 推出 Claude Tag](https://www.anthropic.com/news/introducing-claude-tag)** — _Anthropic · 6月22日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 上线了 Claude Tag，团队可以把 @Claude 拉进 Slack 频道当成员一样用，直接 @它 派活；Anthropic 内部产品团队现在65%的代码都是靠这套内部版本写出来的。

**Main methods:**
- **先落地在 Slack。** Claude 可以加入指定的 Slack 频道，拿到你给它开的工具、数据甚至代码库权限，频道里任何人都能 @Claude 派任务。
- **多人共用、上下文会攒。** 一个频道里只有一个 Claude，大家共享，谁都能看到它在干什么，接着上一个人没说完的话继续聊；Claude 会跟着频道慢慢攒上下文，不用每次从头解释。
- **能主动找你。** 开了"ambient"模式之后，Claude 会主动汇报它觉得你该知道的事，从相关频道和工具里挑出信息，还会去跟进那些没人管又卡住的任务。
- **能异步、自己排期。** 交给它的任务可以跑上几个小时甚至几天，它还能给自己排后续任务，让人腾出手做别的事。
- **也支持私聊。** 用户可以直接给 Claude 发私信，它会用你自己配的工具和连接器私下回复。
- **现在的可用范围。** Claude Enterprise 和 Team 客户已经能用公测版，Anthropic 打算把它从 Slack 扩展到更多办公场景。
