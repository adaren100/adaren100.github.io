**[DXC will integrate Claude into the systems banks, airlines, and other regulated industries rely on](https://www.anthropic.com/news/dxc-anthropic-alliance)** — _Anthropic · Jun 10_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic and DXC Technology announced a multi-year global alliance: DXC will train tens of thousands of Claude-certified forward-deployed engineers and embed them inside the systems DXC runs for major banks, airlines, insurers, manufacturers, and government agencies.

**Main methods:**
- **Forward-deployed engineer (FDE) program.** DXC reskills its own engineers via Anthropic Academy plus a DXC-specific curriculum tied to mission-critical systems, then embeds them at customers.
- **DXC OASIS as the proof point.** DXC's new AI-native orchestration platform was built with Claude writing 95%+ of the code (reviewed by humans), reportedly speeding software development 10×; now serves 50+ customers, with Claude as the default foundation model for its agentic workflows.
- **Four initial focus areas.** Insurance (agentic solutions, core-system modernization), Modernization-as-a-Service (refactoring legacy codebases with Claude), Cybersecurity (always-on security-engineer subagent on Claude Security across DXC SOCs), Application Services.
- **Channel positioning.** DXC joins the Claude Partner Network, giving Anthropic a path into companies where compliance and operational requirements are usually the gating factor.
- **Caveat.** Partnership announcement; quantitative claims (10× dev speedup, 95%+ AI-written code) are DXC self-reported, not externally validated.

**[DXC 把 Claude 集成进银行、航司这些受监管行业的系统里](https://www.anthropic.com/news/dxc-anthropic-alliance)** — _Anthropic · 6月10日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 和 DXC Technology 签了一份多年期全球合作：DXC 要训练上万名 Claude-certified forward-deployed engineer（FDE），让他们直接驻进 DXC 运维的银行、航司、保险、制造、政府客户的系统里。

**Main methods:**
- **FDE 项目。** DXC 把自己的工程师送进 Anthropic Academy，再叠加一层针对关键业务系统的 DXC 内部课程，培训完直接 embed 到客户那边。
- **DXC OASIS 是样板。** DXC 新的 AI-native 运维编排平台 OASIS，95% 以上代码由 Claude 写、工程师 review，软件开发据说快了 10 倍；目前已经服务 50+ 客户，Claude 是其 agentic workflow 的默认底层模型。
- **初期四个方向。** 保险（agentic 方案 + 核心系统现代化）、Modernization-as-a-Service（用 Claude 重构 legacy 代码库）、Cybersecurity（基于 Claude Security 的常驻安全工程师 subagent，部署到各 SOC）、Application Services。
- **渠道布局。** DXC 加入 Claude Partner Network，给 Anthropic 打开了那些合规和运维要求卡得最死的客户。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是合作公告，10× 开发提速、95% AI 写代码这种数字都是 DXC 自报，没有外部验证。
