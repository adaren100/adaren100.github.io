**[Introducing Claude Sonnet 5](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-sonnet-5)** — _Anthropic · Jun 29_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic released Claude Sonnet 5, the most agentic Sonnet to date — performance close to Opus 4.8 at a lower price, now the default model on Free and Pro plans, with introductory API pricing at $2/M input tokens and $10/M output tokens through August 31, 2026.

**Main methods:**
- **Closing the Sonnet-Opus gap.** Sonnet 5 narrows the performance gap to Opus 4.8 on BrowseComp (agentic search) and OSWorld-Verified (computer use), while covering a wider cost-performance range than Opus 4.8 at medium effort levels.
- **Major improvements over Sonnet 4.6.** Substantial gains on reasoning, tool use, coding, and knowledge work; early testers reported it finishes complex multi-step tasks where previous Sonnet models stalled, and checks its own output without being asked.
- **Safety profile.** Sonnet 5 has a lower rate of undesirable behaviors than Sonnet 4.6 and is deemed safer for agentic use; its ability to perform cybersecurity-offensive tasks is significantly lower than current Opus models.
- **Pricing and availability.** Now the default model for Free and Pro plans; available in Max, Team, Enterprise, and Claude Code. API introductory pricing: $2/M input, $10/M output through Aug 31, 2026, then moving to $3/$15.
- **Partner feedback.** Early testers including Lovable described Sonnet 5 finishing two-part automation jobs end-to-end (e.g., updating Salesforce account tiers and sending a launch announcement) that previously stalled halfway through.

**[Anthropic 发布 Claude Sonnet 5](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-sonnet-5)** — _Anthropic · 6月29日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 发布了 Claude Sonnet 5，目前最能自主干活的 Sonnet——性能接近 Opus 4.8，价格更低；现在是 Free 和 Pro 套餐的默认模型，API 介绍期定价 $2/M input token、$10/M output token，优惠至 2026 年 8 月 31 日。

**Main methods:**
- **缩小 Sonnet 和 Opus 之间的差距。** 在 BrowseComp（agentic 搜索）和 OSWorld-Verified（电脑操控）上，Sonnet 5 逼近 Opus 4.8，中等 effort 下成本效率更有优势，覆盖比 Opus 4.8 更宽的价格-性能区间。
- **比 Sonnet 4.6 提升明显。** 推理、tool use、写代码、知识工作都有实质性进步；早期测试方反馈它能把之前 Sonnet 卡住的复杂多步任务跑完，还会主动检查自己的输出不用人提醒。
- **安全评估。** Sonnet 5 的不良行为发生率比 Sonnet 4.6 低，agentic 场景下更安全；在网络安全攻击能力上明显低于当前 Opus 系列。
- **价格和可用范围。** 现在是 Free 和 Pro 套餐的默认模型，Max、Team、Enterprise 和 Claude Code 也可用。API 介绍期：$2/M input、$10/M output，8 月 31 日后调整为 $3/$15。
- **合作方反馈。** Lovable 等早期测试方说 Sonnet 5 能一口气跑完之前会卡在中途的双任务自动化（比如更新 Salesforce 账户等级 + 发上线通知邮件）。
