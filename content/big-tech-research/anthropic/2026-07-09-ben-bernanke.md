**[Ben Bernanke appointed to Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust](https://www.anthropic.com/news/ben-bernanke)** — _Anthropic · Jul 9_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT) has appointed Dr. Ben Bernanke—2022 Nobel laureate in economics and former Federal Reserve Chair—as its newest independent trustee, bringing macroeconomic expertise to the body that oversees Anthropic's public benefit mission.

**Main methods:**
- **What the LTBT does.** The independent Trust holds no equity and shares no profits; it advises Anthropic's board on significant AI risk and societal impact decisions, and has authority to appoint board members.
- **Bernanke's fit.** His academic work on the Great Depression and the role of banks in financial crises, plus eight years leading the Fed through the 2008 crisis, directly maps to the economic disruption questions AI raises.
- **Economic research contribution.** Beyond general governance, Bernanke will specifically contribute to Anthropic's ongoing research on how AI is changing the economy.
- **Trust composition.** He joins Chair Neil Buddy Shah, Richard Fontaine (national security), and Mariano-Florentino Cuéllar (law/technology policy)—trustees chosen for domain breadth, not financial alignment.

**[Ben Bernanke 加入 Anthropic 长期利益信托](https://www.anthropic.com/news/ben-bernanke)** — _Anthropic · 7月9日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 的 Long-Term Benefit Trust（LTBT）新增了一位成员：Ben Bernanke 博士——2022 年诺贝尔经济学奖得主、前美联储主席——为这个负责监督 Anthropic 公共利益使命的独立机构带来宏观经济层面的专业视角。

**Main methods:**
- **LTBT 是干什么的。** 这个独立信托不持有股权、不分享利润，负责就重大的 AI 风险和社会影响决策向 Anthropic 董事会提供建议，并有权任命董事会成员。
- **Bernanke 的契合点。** 他在大萧条和银行在金融危机中扮演角色方面的学术积累，加上带着美联储扛过 2008 年金融危机的八年经历，正好对准了 AI 可能引发的经济冲击议题。
- **经济研究贡献。** 除了日常治理工作，Bernanke 还会专门参与 Anthropic 关于 AI 如何改变经济的研究。
- **信托现有阵容。** 他加入的是：主席 Neil Buddy Shah、Richard Fontaine（国家安全方向）、Mariano-Florentino Cuéllar（法律/技术政策）——成员选拔注重领域多样性而非财务利益绑定。
