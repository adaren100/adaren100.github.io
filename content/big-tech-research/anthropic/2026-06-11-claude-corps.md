**[Introducing Claude Corps](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-corps)** — _Anthropic · Jun 10_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic is committing $150M to Claude Corps, a fellowship that trains 1,000 early-career fellows on Claude and pays them to spend a year full-time, in-person, embedded at US nonprofits — framed as direct investment in workers absorbing AI-driven disruption.

**Main methods:**
- **Program structure.** 12-month fellowship; intensive Claude training up front, 5 hours/week ongoing training, the rest of the time dedicated to the host nonprofit.
- **Three-party partnership.** Anthropic funds and leads strategy; CodePath is the employer of record and runs day-to-day programming; Social Finance handles measurement and builds a financial vehicle to scale the program longer-term.
- **Fellow package.** $85,000 salary plus benefits, a CodePath mentor, an "expansive" Claude token budget, plus office hours with Anthropic for technical questions.
- **Host scale.** At least 400 nonprofits will host fellows — examples include Braven, Code the Dream, Heartland Forward, Montgomery County Food Bank, Team Red White & Blue, Reef Environmental Education Foundation, and SoundOff.
- **Why now.** Announced alongside Anthropic's policy framework on AI's impact on work; explicit framing is that the companies building the disruption should fund the workers absorbing it.
- **Caveat.** Workforce / philanthropy program, not a research result — no model claims involved.

**[Anthropic 推出 Claude Corps](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-corps)** — _Anthropic · 6月10日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 拿出 1.5 亿美元启动 Claude Corps，培训 1000 名职业早期的 fellow 用 Claude，再付他们工资让他们全职、线下到美国各地的 nonprofit 工作一年——明确定位为直接投资被 AI 冲击的劳动者。

**Main methods:**
- **项目结构。** 12 个月的 fellowship，开头有密集的 Claude 培训，之后每周 5 小时持续培训，其他时间都在 host 机构里做事。
- **三方合作。** Anthropic 出钱并主导策略；CodePath 作为正式雇主负责日常运营；Social Finance 负责测量评估，并搭一个长期能扩大规模的金融工具。
- **fellow 的待遇。** 85,000 美元年薪加福利，配 CodePath mentor，开放 Claude token 额度，技术问题可以约 Anthropic 的 office hours。
- **host 规模。** 至少 400 家 nonprofit 接收 fellow——已公布的有 Braven、Code the Dream、Heartland Forward、Montgomery County Food Bank、Team Red White & Blue、Reef Environmental Education Foundation、SoundOff 等。
- **为什么是现在。** 和 Anthropic 关于 AI 对工作影响的政策框架一起发——明确说造出这种冲击的公司有责任直接投资被冲击的人。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是一个 workforce/慈善项目，不是研究成果，没有任何模型层面的 claim。
