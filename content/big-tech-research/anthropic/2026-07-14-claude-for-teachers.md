**[Introducing Claude for Teachers](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-teachers)** — _Anthropic · Jul 14_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic launched Claude for Teachers, giving verified K-12 educators in the US free access to premium Claude capabilities, a library of teaching skills, and a direct connection to evidence-based curricula mapped to academic standards in all 50 states. The stated aim is to close the gap between proven teaching practices and what a teacher's week actually allows.

**Main methods:**
- **Why teachers, not students.** Practices like differentiation, mastery-based learning, and small-group instruction reliably improve achievement, but teachers lack time to implement them — a strain heaviest in under-resourced schools. Anthropic cites early evidence that AI tools for teachers strengthen instruction, while impact of AI tools for students is mixed and implementation-dependent.
- **Learning Commons connector.** Claude gets access to academic standards in all 50 states, plus the smaller learning competencies beneath each standard and the order students typically learn them, so drafted lesson plans come out scaffolded and standards-aligned.
- **Evidence-based curricula built in.** Trusted resources like OpenSciEd and IM v.360 from Illustrative Mathematics are wired in, so lesson drafts draw on widely used curricula rather than generic generation.
- **A K-12 tool ecosystem.** Educators can connect Claude to ASSISTments, Brisk Teaching, Canva Education, Coteach, Diffit, Eedi, MagicSchool, Snorkl, and TeachFX — covering auto-scored math problems, diagnostic questions, classroom-ready designs, and instructional feedback grounded in real classroom talk.
- **Teaching skills co-developed with Learning Commons.** The skills were designed around tasks teachers said mattered most, evaluated for rigor, pedagogical alignment, and classroom usability, and refined through feedback from classroom teachers, including at Prospect Schools in Brooklyn.

**[Claude for Teachers 发布：美国 K-12 老师免费用](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-teachers)** — _Anthropic · 7月14日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 推出 Claude for Teachers，通过认证的美国 K-12 教师可以免费用上 Claude 的高级能力，外加一整套教学 skill 库，还直接接入了对齐全美 50 个州学术标准的循证课程资源。目标说得很直白：好的教学方法早有研究背书，缺的是老师的时间，这个产品就是来补这个缺口的。

**Main methods:**
- **为什么做给老师而不是学生。** 差异化教学、掌握式学习、小组教学这些方法确实能提高学生成绩，但老师没时间落实，资源不足的学校压力最大。Anthropic 引用的早期证据显示，给老师用的 AI 工具能改善教学效果，而给学生用的 AI 工具效果参差不齐，很依赖具体怎么落地。
- **接入 Learning Commons。** Claude 能拿到全美 50 个州的学术标准，还有每条标准下面更细粒度的学习能力点和学生通常的学习顺序，所以生成的教案天然带脚手架、对齐标准。
- **循证课程直接内置。** OpenSciEd、Illustrative Mathematics 的 IM v.360 这些广泛使用的课程资源都接进来了，教案初稿有真实课程打底，不是凭空生成。
- **打通 K-12 工具生态。** 老师可以把 Claude 连到 ASSISTments、Brisk Teaching、Canva Education、Coteach、Diffit、Eedi、MagicSchool、Snorkl、TeachFX，覆盖自动批改的数学题、诊断性问题、课堂设计素材，以及基于真实课堂对话的教学反馈。
- **教学 skill 和 Learning Commons 一起打磨。** 这套 skill 围绕老师反馈里最重要的任务来设计，评估过严谨性、教学法对齐和课堂可用性，还请一线老师（包括布鲁克林的 Prospect Schools）试用后迭代过。
