**[Apply for Anthropic's AI for Science rare disease research grants](https://www.anthropic.com/news/rare-disease-research-grants)** — _Anthropic · Jul 20_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic opened a themed call within its AI for Science program focused specifically on rare genetic diseases, offering accepted applicants up to $50,000 in Claude credits over six months across two tracks: basic research and early-stage biotech.

**Main methods:**
- **Thematic calls replace open-ended applications.** After finding that grantees working on related questions generate more progress when they can compare notes, Anthropic is now organizing AI for Science around focused themes rather than one broad open call.
- **Two funding tracks.** Track one supports scientists doing basic research into rare disease mechanisms; track two backs early-stage biotechs trying to speed up clinical development for rare diseases.
- **Scale of the problem.** An estimated 400 million people live with one of more than 7,000 rare diseases, but small, scattered patient populations make registries, therapeutic targets, and trial design hard to build.
- **Early partner: the Monarch Initiative.** An international consortium building the Mondo Disease Ontology (reconciling disease definitions across OMIM, Orphanet, ICD, and other sources) and the Monarch Knowledge Graph (integrating genotype-phenotype data across species for diagnostics and mechanism discovery).
- **Where AI is expected to help.** Modeling rare genetic diseases and spotting patterns across them, synthesizing findings across large literature corpora, extracting information from limited datasets, and creating shared terminology across isolated disease studies.

**[Anthropic 开放稀有病研究基金申请](https://www.anthropic.com/news/rare-disease-research-grants)** — _Anthropic · 7月20日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 在 AI for Science 项目里开了一个专门针对稀有遗传病的主题基金，入选的申请者六个月内最多能拿到 5 万美元的 Claude credits，分基础研究和早期生物科技两条赛道。

**Main methods:**
- **从开放申请转向主题式征集。** Anthropic 发现如果好几个 grantee 都在研究相关的问题、互相交流心得，项目进展会更快，所以现在把 AI for Science 拆成一个个聚焦主题，而不是笼统地开放申请。
- **两条赛道。** 第一条给做基础研究、研究稀有病机制的科学家；第二条给想加快稀有病临床开发进度的早期生物科技公司。
- **问题的规模有多大。** 全球估计有 4 亿人患有 7,000 多种稀有病中的至少一种，但患者群体分散又小，医生很难建患者登记库、找治疗靶点、设计临床试验。
- **早期合作方：Monarch Initiative。** 这是一个国际联盟，在建 Mondo Disease Ontology（把 OMIM、Orphanet、ICD 等一堆分散的疾病定义对齐到一起）和 Monarch Knowledge Graph（跨物种整合基因型-表现型数据，帮助诊断和找机制）。
- **AI 具体能帮上什么忙。** 建模稀有遗传病、发现病与病之间共通的模式，从大量文献里综合提炼结论，从有限的数据集里快速抽取信息，给研究得很分散的疾病建立统一术语。
