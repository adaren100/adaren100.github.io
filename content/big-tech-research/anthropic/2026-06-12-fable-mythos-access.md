**[Statement on the US government directive to suspend access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access)** — _Anthropic · Jun 11_

**Main takeaway:** The US government issued an export control directive ordering Anthropic to suspend all access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5 — for every foreign national worldwide, including Anthropic's own foreign-national employees — forcing the company to abruptly disable both models for all customers. Anthropic is complying but publicly disagrees with the underlying reasoning.

**Main methods:**
- **The directive.** Delivered the same day at 5:21pm ET, citing national security authorities; the letter did not detail the specific concern. Other Anthropic models are unaffected.
- **Government's apparent concern.** Anthropic's read is the government became aware of a jailbreak method against Fable 5. After reviewing a demo, Anthropic says the technique only surfaces a small number of previously known minor vulnerabilities that other public models can find without any bypass.
- **Defense-in-depth posture.** Anthropic argues no model provider has perfect jailbreak resistance today, so Fable launched with conservative safeguards plus monitoring and 30-day data retention so that successful attacks can be detected and shut down quickly.
- **Anthropic's counter-claim.** Pre-launch red-teaming with the US government, UK AISI, and third parties found no universal jailbreak, and no disclosed jailbreak to date has produced a harmful result with Mythos-specific uplift.
- **Caveat.** Anthropic frames this as a policy disagreement they are complying with, not a security incident — and warns the precedent affects the whole industry.

**[关于美国政府要求暂停 Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 访问的声明](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access)** — _Anthropic · 6月11日_

**Main takeaway:** 美国政府以国家安全为由，发了一份 export control 指令，要求 Anthropic 立刻切断 Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 对所有 foreign national 的访问——包括 Anthropic 自己的外籍员工，于是 Anthropic 不得不对所有用户都把这两个模型关掉。Anthropic 选择合规执行，但公开表示不认同政府的判断依据。

**Main methods:**
- **指令本身。** 美东时间当天下午 5:21 送达，援引国家安全权限，但没说具体担心的是什么。Anthropic 的其他模型不受影响。
- **政府担心的是什么。** Anthropic 推测政府得知了一个针对 Fable 5 的 jailbreak 方法。看过演示之后，他们认为这个技术只挖出几个早就已知的小 vulnerability，其他公开模型不用任何 bypass 也能找到。
- **defense in depth 的姿态。** Anthropic 的看法是：目前没有任何 model provider 能做到完美的 jailbreak resistance，所以 Fable 一开始就配了偏保守的 safeguard，再加上 monitoring 和 30 天数据 retention，方便快速发现并切断成功的攻击。
- **Anthropic 自己的反驳。** 发布前他们和美国政府、英国 AISI 以及多家第三方 red-team 了上千小时，没人找到 universal jailbreak；到目前为止被披露的 jailbreak 也都没有 Mythos 独有的 uplift，没有造成实际危害。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** Anthropic 把这事定性为政策分歧而不是安全事故——他们配合执行，但警告这个先例对整个行业都有影响。
