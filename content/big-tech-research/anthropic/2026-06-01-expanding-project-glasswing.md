**[Expanding Project Glasswing](https://www.anthropic.com/news/expanding-project-glasswing)** — _Anthropic · Jun 1_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic expands Project Glasswing from ~50 initial partners to ~150 new organizations across 15+ countries, adding critical infrastructure sectors (power, water, healthcare, communications, hardware) absent from the first cohort — all sharing the criterion that a major cyberattack on their codebase could affect more than 100 million people.

**Main methods:**
- **Scale-up rationale.** With initial partners finding 10,000+ high/critical vulnerabilities and Mythos-class models expected at competing labs within 6–12 months, Anthropic is accelerating defender readiness before the capability becomes widely available.
- **New sector coverage.** First cohort skewed toward software companies; the expansion targets infrastructure operators in power, water, healthcare, communications, and hardware — entities whose compromise has wide downstream impact.
- **Vendor-multiplier strategy.** Many new partners maintain codebases used by other organizations including governments — securing one vendor protects many downstream dependents.
- **Shifting from finding to fixing.** Anthropic signals a phase change: early focus was detecting vulnerabilities; next phase is disclosure, patching, and deployment — the harder part of the security workflow once AI removes the detection bottleneck.

**[Project Glasswing 扩大规模](https://www.anthropic.com/news/expanding-project-glasswing)** — _Anthropic · 6月1日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 把 Project Glasswing 从最初约 50 家合作方扩展到约 150 家新组织，覆盖 15 个以上国家，新增了第一批没覆盖到的关键基础设施行业（电力、水务、医疗、通信、硬件）——所有合作方的共同标准是：对其代码库的重大攻击可能影响超过 1 亿人。

**Main methods:**
- **扩大规模的理由。** 初始合作方已找到 1 万多个高/严重漏洞，同时 Mythos 级别的模型预计在 6–12 个月内就会出现在竞争对手那里——Anthropic 在此之前加速让防御方做好准备。
- **新增行业覆盖。** 第一批合作方偏向软件公司；新一批重点是电力、水务、医疗、通信、硬件领域的基础设施运营商——这些实体一旦被攻破，下游影响面极广。
- **供应商乘数策略。** 很多新合作方维护着被其他组织（包括政府）依赖的代码库——保护一个供应商就等于保护了大量下游依赖方。
- **从发现漏洞转向修复漏洞。** Anthropic 表示项目重点正在转变：初期聚焦于检测漏洞；下一阶段是披露、修复和部署补丁——当 AI 解决了检测瓶颈之后，安全工作流里更难的那部分。
