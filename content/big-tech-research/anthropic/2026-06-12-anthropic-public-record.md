**[Results from the first Anthropic Public Record](https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-public-record)** — _Anthropic · Jun 11_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic surveyed nearly 52,000 Americans in late 2025 and found broad, bipartisan demand for AI regulation — 70%+ want government to play a role, only 15% trust AI companies to self-govern, and job loss is the top fear in every state (64%).

**Main methods:**
- **Survey design.** Nationally representative online survey via YouGov, n=51,993, weighted to Census benchmarks; state-level samples range from n=232 (Alaska) to n=1,902 (NY).
- **Top hopes.** Curing disease (48%) leads by 12 points, followed by helping people with disabilities (36%), then technological progress and easier life (tied at 23%).
- **Top fears.** Job loss (64%), cognitive dependency (56%), misinformation (52%) — job loss is #1 in every state.
- **Where people want regulation.** Privacy (56%), child safety (52%), and liability for harm (49%); ranked highest-leverage interventions are legal liability for AI companies (47%) and prioritizing safety over growth (44%).
- **Surprisingly low polarization.** Views did not split heavily along partisan, geographic, or educational lines — disagreement mostly showed up as differences in intensity, not direction.
- **Why Anthropic ran it.** Complements existing work (Anthropic Economic Index, qualitative Anthropic Interviewer study of 81,000 Claude users) by reaching non-users of AI — the survey will repeat regularly and eventually expand outside the US.

**[第一期 Anthropic Public Record 调查结果](https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-public-record)** — _Anthropic · 6月11日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 在 2025 年底做了一份覆盖近 52,000 美国人的调查，发现公众对 AI 监管的需求广泛而且跨党派——超过 70% 希望政府介入，只有 15% 信任 AI 公司自我管理，而失业是所有州里排第一的恐惧（64%）。

**Main methods:**
- **调查设计。** 通过 YouGov 做的全国代表性在线调查，n=51,993，按 Census 加权；州一级样本从阿拉斯加 n=232 到纽约 n=1,902 不等。
- **最大的期待。** 治愈疾病（48%）领先第二名 12 个点，其次是帮助残障人士（36%），然后是技术进步和让生活更轻松（并列 23%）。
- **最大的恐惧。** 失业（64%）、认知依赖（56%）、misinformation（52%）——失业在每一个州都是第一。
- **想让政府管什么。** 隐私（56%）、儿童安全（52%）、危害的法律责任（49%）；最有效的干预排序里，让 AI 公司承担法律责任（47%）和把安全放在增长之前（44%）排在最前。
- **意外地不分裂。** 大多数议题上党派、地域、教育程度都没有把人切开——分歧主要体现在态度强度，方向上基本一致。
- **为什么做这个。** 补足 Anthropic Economic Index、Anthropic Interviewer 对 81,000 Claude 用户的访谈这些已有工作，触达非 AI 用户；调查会定期重复，以后会扩展到美国以外。
