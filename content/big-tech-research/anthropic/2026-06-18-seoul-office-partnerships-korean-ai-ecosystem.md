**[Anthropic opens Seoul office and announces new partnerships across the Korean AI ecosystem](https://www.anthropic.com/news/seoul-office-partnerships-korean-ai-ecosystem)** — _Anthropic · Jun 16_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic has opened a Seoul office and is publicizing a broad slate of Claude deployments across Korea — NAVER, Nexon, LG CNS, Hanwha Solutions, Samsung SDS, Channel Corp — plus an academic partnership with the NAIRL consortium and an NGO deployment with Good Neighbors Korea. It's a go-to-market and ecosystem announcement, not a model or research release.

**Main methods:**
- **Developer-tool footprint.** NAVER has rolled out Claude Code across its entire engineering organization (thousands of engineers); Nexon engineering uses Claude Code to write, review, and ship code for live-service games.
- **Conglomerate rollouts.** LG CNS is deploying Claude to thousands of employees and across LG Group; Samsung SDS is deploying Claude (including Claude Cowork and Claude Code) across Samsung Electronics; Hanwha Solutions is delivering Claude via AWS Bedrock to meet in-region data-residency and security requirements.
- **Startup integrations.** Channel Corp uses Claude to power Channel Talk, the customer AI platform used by 230,000+ companies across Korea, Japan, and the United States.
- **Academic partnership.** Anthropic will give Claude access to up to 60 researchers in the National AI Research Lab (NAIRL) — a consortium of KAIST, Korea University, Yonsei, and POSTECH — for work on safety, evaluation, alignment, robustness, and frontier AI research.
- **Nonprofit deployment.** Good Neighbors Korea, a child-rights NGO, is using Claude to analyze program outcomes, navigate social welfare law and internal guidelines, and cut administrative load on frontline social workers.
- **Caveat.** Pure ecosystem and partnership announcement — no new model, eval, or technical result. The framing is enterprise scale and Korean market presence.

**[Anthropic 开首尔办公室，公布一整套韩国 AI 生态合作](https://www.anthropic.com/news/seoul-office-partnerships-korean-ai-ecosystem)** — _Anthropic · 6月16日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 在首尔开了办公室，顺手公布一长串韩国本地的 Claude 部署案例——NAVER、Nexon、LG CNS、Hanwha Solutions、Samsung SDS、Channel Corp 全都在内，还和 NAIRL 学术联盟、NGO Good Neighbors Korea 谈好了合作。这是一份 go-to-market 和生态公告，不是模型或研究发布。

**Main methods:**
- **开发工具铺得最深。** NAVER 把 Claude Code 推到了整个工程部门，几千号工程师在用；Nexon 工程团队用 Claude Code 写、review 并上线那些服务全球玩家的 live-service 游戏代码。
- **大集团整体上车。** LG CNS 给上千员工开了 Claude，还要在 LG 集团内推广；Samsung SDS 把 Claude（包括 Claude Cowork 和 Claude Code）部署到 Samsung Electronics；Hanwha Solutions 走 AWS Bedrock 给全球员工开通，满足严格的本地 data-residency 和安全合规要求。
- **创业公司直接集成。** Channel Corp 用 Claude 驱动它的 Channel Talk 客服 AI 平台，覆盖韩国、日本、美国 23 万多家公司。
- **学术合作。** Anthropic 会给 NAIRL（KAIST、Korea University、Yonsei、POSTECH 组成的联盟）最多 60 位研究员开 Claude，方向是 AI safety、模型 evaluation、alignment、robustness 这些 frontier 议题。
- **NGO 部署。** 儿童权益 NGO Good Neighbors Korea 用 Claude 帮员工分析项目效果、查社会福利法规和内部规章，把一线社工从行政杂事里解放出来。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 纯生态和合作公告，没有新模型、新 eval、新技术结果，重点是企业规模和韩国本地落地。
