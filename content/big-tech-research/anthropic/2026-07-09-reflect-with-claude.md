**[Introducing a way to reflect on how you use Claude](https://www.anthropic.com/news/reflect-with-claude)** — _Anthropic · Jul 9_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic launches a beta reflection dashboard in Claude (web and desktop) that lets users visualize their AI usage patterns—topics, timing, task types—over up to 12 months, and periodically prompts them to think about which tasks they want to keep doing themselves.

**Main methods:**
- **Usage analytics dashboard.** Shows breakdowns of when you use Claude and what you worked on across 1, 3, 6, or 12-month windows; a total time spent view is coming soon.
- **Reflective prompts built in.** The dashboard periodically surfaces questions like "What's one thing you want to keep doing yourself, even if Claude could do it faster?" and invites users to discuss their answers directly with Claude.
- **Quiet hours and usage nudges.** Users can set quiet hours or schedule a reminder to take a break after a set amount of usage—both are soft preferences that can be dismissed.
- **4D AI Fluency Framework.** The reflection is structured around Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence, with personalized summaries of how the user tends to collaborate with Claude and practical improvement suggestions.
- **Privacy-first design.** Incognito chats excluded; source files from connected tools not included; health integration conversations left out entirely. Insights stay within the reflection feature and are not used for any other purpose. Developed with the MIT Media Lab AHA program, Boston Children's Hospital Digital Wellness Lab, and the Family Online Safety Institute.

**[Claude 新出了一个帮你"照镜子"的功能](https://www.anthropic.com/news/reflect-with-claude)** — _Anthropic · 7月9日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 在 Claude 网页版和桌面版上线了 beta 版"使用回顾"dashboard，让用户看清自己的 AI 使用习惯——主题、时间段、任务类型——可以往回看最长 12 个月，并定期提醒你想想哪些事还是想自己来。

**Main methods:**
- **使用分析 dashboard。** 展示你在不同时间段用 Claude 最多是什么时候、在做什么，支持查看过去 1、3、6 或 12 个月；总使用时长视图即将上线。
- **内置反思提示。** Dashboard 会不定期抛出"就算 Claude 能更快做完，有没有什么事你仍然想自己来？"这类问题，并邀请你直接跟 Claude 聊聊想法。
- **安静时段和使用提醒。** 可以设置"免打扰时段"，或者在用了一定时间后收到休息提醒——都是软设置，随时可以关掉。
- **4D AI 流利度框架。** 回顾报告围绕四个维度展开——Delegation（委托）、Description（描述）、Discernment（判断）、Diligence（负责）——给出你的个性化协作风格总结和改进建议。
- **隐私优先设计。** 无痕对话不纳入统计；连接工具的源文件不读取；健康整合相关对话全部排除在外；回顾里的洞察只用于这个功能本身。整个功能与 MIT Media Lab AHA 项目、Boston Children's Hospital Digital Wellness Lab 和 Family Online Safety Institute 合作开发。
