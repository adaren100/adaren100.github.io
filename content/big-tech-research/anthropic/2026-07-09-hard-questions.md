**[Inviting hard questions](https://www.anthropic.com/news/hard-questions)** — _Anthropic · Jul 9_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic is launching a public initiative asking people to submit their hardest questions about AI—covering jobs, society, families, science, and medicine—and committing to publicly track and report concrete actions it takes in response.

**Main methods:**
- **Existing listening work.** Anthropic has already surveyed 52,000 Americans via the Anthropic Public Record and conducted an AI-mediated Anthropic Interviewer survey of 81,000 Claude users across 159 countries in 70 languages.
- **In-person and observational research.** Dozens of focus groups and community sessions have been run alongside analysis of anonymized real-world Claude usage data.
- **Anthropic Institute.** An internal research effort tasked specifically with confronting the most significant societal challenges AI will pose.
- **Long-Term Benefit Trust.** An independent oversight body providing checks on how well Anthropic advances its public benefit mission—active since early in the company's history.
- **Two-way public accountability.** The new initiative is explicitly bidirectional: people submit questions, Anthropic publicly tracks what it does (or fails to do) in response, and will be transparent about where it falls short.

**[Anthropic 征集公众对 AI 最难回答的问题](https://www.anthropic.com/news/hard-questions)** — _Anthropic · 7月9日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 发起公众倡议，邀请所有人提交关于 AI 的"最难问题"——涵盖就业、社会、家庭、科学和医疗等方向——并承诺公开追踪和汇报自己针对这些问题采取的具体行动。

**Main methods:**
- **已有的倾听基础。** Anthropic 已经通过 Anthropic Public Record 调查了 5.2 万名美国人，还用 AI 驱动的 Anthropic Interviewer 在 159 个国家、70 种语言里访谈了 8.1 万名 Claude 用户。
- **线下调研也在跑。** 团队做了几十场焦点小组和社区座谈，同时也在分析匿名化的真实 Claude 使用数据。
- **Anthropic Institute。** 公司内部专门成立的研究机构，任务就是正面应对 AI 对社会影响最深的挑战。
- **Long-Term Benefit Trust（LTBT）。** 独立监督机构，从公司成立早期就在检验 Anthropic 推进公共利益使命的实际效果。
- **双向公开问责。** 新举措建立了双向机制：公众提问，Anthropic 公开记录自己做了什么（或者没做到什么），并对自己承诺做到而实际没做到的地方保持透明。
