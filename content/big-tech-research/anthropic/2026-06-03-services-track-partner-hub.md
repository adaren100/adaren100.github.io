**[Introducing the Services Track and Partner Hub of the Claude Partner Network](https://www.anthropic.com/news/services-track-partner-hub)** — _Anthropic · Jun 2_

**Main takeaway:** Business announcement: Anthropic is rolling out a tiered Services Track and a Partner Hub portal for the Claude Partner Network, which since its March launch has pulled in 40,000+ firms and certified 10,000+ consultants — with the largest consultancies committing six-figure headcounts to Claude practices.

**Main methods:**
- **Three-tier Services Track.** Select (≥10 certified individuals, ≥2 production joint customers, ≥1 public story), Preferred (≥100 / ≥15 / ≥3), and Global Premier (≥1,000 certified, ≥100 customers across three regions, ≥15 stories, plus a joint business plan with named executive sponsors).
- **Same bar for all firm sizes.** A 10-person AI-native shop and a global consultancy are measured against the same numbers; size doesn't raise the tier — only certified bench and deployed joint customers do.
- **Partner Hub portal.** Partners see exactly where they stand against tier requirements; customers use it to find firms qualified for the scope of their project.
- **Scale of the existing network.** Accenture is training 30,000 professionals; Cognizant has rolled Claude to ~350,000 associates; Deloitte to 470,000; KPMG across 276,000+; Infosys is building industry-specific Claude agents; PwC is rolling out Claude Code and Cowork starting in the US.
- **Program economics.** Backed by a $100M Anthropic investment in partner training, technical support, and shared marketing; the ladder also counts adoption and enablement work, so deployment-focused firms can climb early.

**[Anthropic 把 Claude Partner Network 拆出了 Services Track 和 Partner Hub](https://www.anthropic.com/news/services-track-partner-hub)** — _Anthropic · 6月2日_

**Main takeaway:** 一篇商业公告：Anthropic 给 Claude Partner Network 加了两个新东西，一个分层的 Services Track 和一个叫 Partner Hub 的门户。这个 network 是今年 3 月开的，到现在拉了 40,000 多家公司申请、10,000 多人拿到 Claude 认证，几家头部咨询公司直接把六位数的人头压上去做 Claude 实践。

**Main methods:**
- **Services Track 分三档。** Select（至少 10 名认证个人，过去 12 个月里至少 2 个 production 客户，至少 1 个公开案例）；Preferred（100 / 15 / 3）；Global Premier（≥1,000 认证，≥100 个客户且覆盖至少三个区域，≥15 个公开案例，再加一份有明确高管 sponsor 的 joint business plan）。
- **不看公司大小，看实打实的数字。** 不管你是 10 个人的 AI-native 小作坊还是全球咨询巨头，门槛都一样；规模本身不会让你上一档，只有认证人数和上线的 joint customer 才能。
- **Partner Hub 门户。** 合作方能直接看到自己离每一档要求还差多少；客户也能从这里找到适合自己项目体量的 firm。
- **现有 network 的规模。** Accenture 在培训 30,000 名专业人员；Cognizant 已经把 Claude 推给约 350,000 名员工；Deloitte 是 470,000；KPMG 跨 276,000 多人；Infosys 在做行业专属的 Claude agent；PwC 则从美国团队开始铺 Claude Code 和 Cowork。
- **这套项目的投入。** 背后是 Anthropic 1 亿美元的投资，砸在合作方培训、技术支持和联合营销上；这个 ladder 也认 adoption 和 enablement 工作，所以专门做"把 Claude 真正上线给客户"的 firm 能比较早往上爬。
