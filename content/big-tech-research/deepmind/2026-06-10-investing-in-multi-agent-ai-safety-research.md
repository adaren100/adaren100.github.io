**[Investing in multi-agent AI safety research](https://deepmind.google/blog/investing-in-multi-agent-ai-safety-research/)** — _Google DeepMind · Jun 10_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind, Schmidt Sciences, the Cooperative AI Foundation, and ARIA (with Google.org support) are putting up to $10M into a global funding call for research on how large populations of interacting AI agents behave — and how to make those interactions safe and predictable.

**Main methods:**
- **Why it matters now.** Most safety evals look at one model in isolation, but soon millions of agents built by different organizations will communicate, negotiate, and transact across networks — emergent group behaviors that no current eval framework captures.
- **What gets funded.** Sandboxes and testbeds (virtual marketplaces, simulated ecosystems, multi-organization environments), plus work on understanding and mitigating emergent multi-agent risks like sudden phase transitions in behavior.
- **Building on prior work.** Extends DeepMind's 2025 multi-agent safety framework and recent AI Agent Traps work on adversarial environments.
- **Aligned with partner programs.** Plugs into Schmidt Sciences' Science of Trustworthy AI and AI Agents programs, and ARIA's Scaling Trust programme on cyber-physical multi-agent coordination.
- **Open call rationale.** A diverse, independent research community is presented as the way to keep safety standards transparent and robust as systems scale beyond any single lab's reach.

**[10M 美金的 multi-agent AI 安全研究 funding call](https://deepmind.google/blog/investing-in-multi-agent-ai-safety-research/)** — _Google DeepMind · 6月10日_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind 联合 Schmidt Sciences、Cooperative AI Foundation、ARIA（Google.org 支持），拉起 1000 万美元的全球 funding call，研究大量 AI agent 互相交互时的群体行为，以及怎么让这种交互更安全可预测。

**Main methods:**
- **为什么是现在。** 目前的 safety eval 几乎都只看单个模型，但马上就会出现数百万个由不同组织造的 agent 在网络上交流、谈判、交易——这种群体涌现行为现有评测框架根本捕捉不到。
- **funding 重点投什么。** sandbox 和 testbed（虚拟市场、生态模拟、跨组织环境），加上对多 agent 涌现风险（比如行为的突变 phase transition）的理解和缓解。
- **建在已有工作之上。** 延续 DeepMind 2025 年的 multi-agent safety 框架，以及最近关于 AI Agent Traps 的对抗环境研究。
- **和合作方项目对齐。** 接 Schmidt Sciences 的 Science of Trustworthy AI 和 AI Agents 项目、ARIA 的 Scaling Trust 项目（cyber-physical 多 agent 协作）。
- **为什么走 open call。** 一个多元、独立的研究社区被认为是保证 safety standard 透明且 robust 的方式——单个 lab 罩不住整个生态。
