**[Powering the future of robotics in Europe](https://deepmind.google/blog/powering-the-future-of-robotics-in-europe/)** — _Google DeepMind · Jun 9_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind is launching a three-month accelerator for 15 early-stage European robotics startups, giving them access to Google's AI stack, Gemini robotics models, and hands-on mentorship.

**Main methods:**
- **Program shape.** Three months of mentorship, product guidance, and technical support; kicks off in London.
- **Cohort breadth.** 15 startups spanning healthcare, manufacturing, climate, logistics, construction, recycling, and advanced navigation — examples include 3D-Components AS (robotic welding/metal 3D printing QC), Acumino (hardware-agnostic physical AI), and Adapta Robotics (haptic device/software testing).
- **Tech access.** Selected teams get Google's AI stack and Gemini robotics models to integrate into their products.
- **Why Europe.** Frames embodied AI as a frontier where Europe has strong research depth, and the accelerator as a way to convert that research into shipped robotics products.
- **Caveat.** Accelerator program announcement, not a research result — no new model or benchmark.

**[Google DeepMind 在欧洲启动机器人加速器](https://deepmind.google/blog/powering-the-future-of-robotics-in-europe/)** — _Google DeepMind · 6月9日_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind 启动一个三个月的加速器，挑了 15 家欧洲早期机器人创业公司，给他们开放 Google 的 AI 栈、Gemini robotics 模型，再加上手把手的 mentor。

**Main methods:**
- **项目形态。** 三个月 mentor、产品指导、技术支持；本周在伦敦开营。
- **入选 cohort 很杂。** 15 家创业公司，覆盖医疗、制造、气候、物流、建筑、回收、高级导航——比如 3D-Components AS（机器人焊接和金属 3D 打印质控）、Acumino（硬件无关的 physical AI）、Adapta Robotics（用机器人模拟人手触感测试设备）。
- **能拿到什么。** 入选团队可以用 Google 的 AI 栈和 Gemini robotics 模型，集成进自己的产品。
- **为什么是欧洲。** DeepMind 把 embodied AI 定位成欧洲研究底子最强的方向之一，加速器就是把研究变成实际产品的通道。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是加速器项目公告，不是研究成果，没有新模型也没有 benchmark。
