**[Google DeepMind and A24 announce first-of-its-kind research partnership](https://deepmind.google/blog/google-deepmind-and-a24-announce-first-of-its-kind-research-partnership/)** — _Google DeepMind · Jul 4_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind and film studio A24 announced a first-of-its-kind research partnership: a multi-project R&D collaboration where A24's filmmakers work side-by-side with DeepMind researchers to shape AI tools for filmmaking, and Google has also made an investment in A24.

**Main methods:**
- **Research-first framing.** This is pitched as a research collaboration, not a product deal — pairing a frontier research lab with what DeepMind calls the industry's most filmmaker-forward studio, spanning multiple projects over time.
- **Artists in the loop.** The stated design is to anchor DeepMind's innovations directly inside the creative process, so A24's filmmakers guide new workflows and techniques rather than adapting to tools built without them.
- **Feedback flows both ways.** DeepMind frames the hands-on collaboration as a source of invaluable feedback and guidance from leading artists for its own research.
- **Financial stake.** Beyond the research collaboration, Google has made an investment in A24 (no amount disclosed).
- **Deliberately open-ended scope.** The initial focus is bridging cutting-edge technology and next-generation entertainment, but the post says specific goals, technical outputs, and creative milestones will evolve as the two sides test, iterate, and build together.

**[Google DeepMind 拉上 A24 搞了个前所未有的研究合作](https://deepmind.google/blog/google-deepmind-and-a24-announce-first-of-its-kind-research-partnership/)** — _Google DeepMind · 7月4日_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind 和电影公司 A24 宣布了一个号称业内首创的研究合作：不是一锤子买卖，而是横跨多个项目的长期 R&D 协作，让 A24 的电影人和 DeepMind 的研究员一起打磨面向电影制作的 AI 工具。Google 顺手还投资了 A24。

**Main methods:**
- **定位是研究合作，不是卖产品。** 官方口径强调这是 research collaboration：一边是前沿研究实验室，一边是 DeepMind 眼里业内最"以电影人为本"的工作室，合作会跨多个项目长期推进。
- **创作者直接参与工具设计。** 思路是把 DeepMind 的技术直接嵌进创作流程里，让 A24 的电影人来主导新 workflow 和新技术的走向，而不是让他们被动适应别人做好的工具。
- **反馈是双向的。** DeepMind 也把这次合作当成拿一线艺术家反馈的渠道，让顶尖创作者的意见直接喂回自己的研究。
- **真金白银入股。** 除了研究合作，Google 还对 A24 做了投资，金额没有披露。
- **范围刻意留白。** 初期先聚焦打通前沿技术和下一代娱乐内容，但博客明说具体目标、技术产出和创作里程碑都会随着双方一起测试、迭代慢慢演化。
