**[Start building with Nano Banana 2 Lite and Gemini Omni Flash](https://deepmind.google/blog/start-building-with-nano-banana-2-lite-and-gemini-omni-flash/)** — _Google DeepMind · Jun 30_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind released Nano Banana 2 Lite (gemini-3.1-flash-lite-image), the fastest and cheapest model in the Nano Banana image family (4-second generation, $0.034/1K images), and brought Gemini Omni Flash for video generation and conversational editing to the Gemini API and Google AI Studio for the first time.

**Main methods:**
- **Nano Banana 2 Lite speed and cost.** Delivers text-to-image in 4 seconds at $0.034 per 1K images; Google recommends it as a drop-in replacement for the original Nano Banana (gemini-2.5-flash-image), available immediately in Google AI Studio, Gemini API, and Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform.
- **Quality held despite speed focus.** Despite optimizing for latency, Nano Banana 2 Lite retains reliable prompt adherence, strong character consistency, and legible in-image text rendering.
- **Nano Banana family tiers.** Lite is built for ultra-low latency and high-volume pipelines; Nano Banana 2 is the generalist workhorse balancing quality and cost; Nano Banana Pro targets complex professional use cases.
- **Gemini Omni Flash now on the API.** The high-quality, cost-efficient video generation and conversational editing model is now available in Google AI Studio, the Gemini API, and Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform for the first time — previously only in the Gemini app and Google Flow.
- **End-to-end multimedia pipelines.** Together the two models let developers connect rapid image generation with multi-turn video creation and editing in a single pipeline.

**[Google DeepMind 发布 Nano Banana 2 Lite 和 Gemini Omni Flash](https://deepmind.google/blog/start-building-with-nano-banana-2-lite-and-gemini-omni-flash/)** — _Google DeepMind · 6月30日_

**Main takeaway:** Google DeepMind 发布了 Nano Banana 2 Lite（gemini-3.1-flash-lite-image），目前 Nano Banana 系列里最快、最便宜的图像模型，4秒出图、每千张 $0.034；同时把 Gemini Omni Flash 视频生成和对话式编辑模型首次向开发者开放，Gemini API 和 Google AI Studio 现在都可以调。

**Main methods:**
- **Nano Banana 2 Lite 的速度和成本。** 4秒完成文到图生成，每千图 $0.034；Google 官方建议原来用第一代 Nano Banana（gemini-2.5-flash-image）的开发者直接换过来，今天起在 Google AI Studio、Gemini API 和 Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform 都上线了。
- **快但没砍质量。** 虽然主打低延迟，Nano Banana 2 Lite 对 prompt 的理解、角色一致性和图内文字渲染都还撑得住。
- **Nano Banana 家族三档定位。** Lite 走极速高并发；Nano Banana 2 做性价比通才；Nano Banana Pro 对准复杂的专业场景。
- **Gemini Omni Flash 正式上 API。** 视频生成和对话式编辑模型首次进入 Google AI Studio、Gemini API 和 Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform，之前只能在 Gemini app 和 Google Flow 里用。
- **端到端多媒体流水线。** 两个模型配合，开发者能把快速出图和多轮视频生成、编辑接成一条完整流水线。
