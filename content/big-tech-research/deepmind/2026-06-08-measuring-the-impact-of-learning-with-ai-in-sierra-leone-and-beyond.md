**[Measuring the impact of learning with AI in Sierra Leone and beyond](https://deepmind.google/blog/measuring-the-impact-of-learning-with-ai-in-sierra-leone-and-beyond/)** — _Google DeepMind · Jun 8_

**Main takeaway:** A pre-registered RCT of 1,763 junior secondary students in Sierra Leone over 8 weeks found that Gemini's Guided Learning produced math-score gains of +0.258 SD — roughly 1.2–1.7 years of typical learning progress, rising to 1.8–2.5 years for classrooms that hit the 12-hour usage target.

**Main methods:**
- **Trial design.** Pre-registered randomized controlled trial run with Fab AI and the Sierra Leone Ministry of Education, covering 12 schools in Port Loko District over 8 weeks.
- **Guided Learning over answer engine.** Tool was built from LearnLM research to prioritize building understanding over giving answers — analysis of 113,000+ interactions showed 91.4% of conversations were about conceptual understanding, and Gemini posed scaffolding questions in 76% of messages, giving direct solutions in only 2%.
- **Teacher-led.** Teachers designed lessons, set objectives, and led discussions; focus-group reports say teachers used Gemini for lesson prep and shifted from lecturer to facilitator.
- **Engagement.** 69% of students met or exceeded usage targets — far above the ~5% typical for voluntary edtech.
- **Impact magnitudes.** Overall +0.258 SD ≈ 1.2–1.7 years of progress; the higher-dose subgroup (Gemini in roughly half of lessons, 12 hours target) saw 1.8–2.5 years.
- **Caveat.** Single 8-week trial in one district; DeepMind frames this as a contribution to a broader global evidence base on AI in education, not a settled result.

**[在塞拉利昂用 AI 辅助学习的 RCT 结果](https://deepmind.google/blog/measuring-the-impact-of-learning-with-ai-in-sierra-leone-and-beyond/)** — _Google DeepMind · 6月8日_

**Main takeaway:** 一项 8 周的预注册 RCT，覆盖塞拉利昂 1763 名初中生，结果发现用 Gemini 的 Guided Learning 让数学成绩提升 +0.258 个标准差——相当于通常 1.2 到 1.7 年的学习进度；在用得多的班级里，进度更是飙到 1.8 到 2.5 年。

**Main methods:**
- **实验设计。** 预注册 RCT，和 Fab AI、塞拉利昂教育部一起做，覆盖 Port Loko 区 12 所学校，持续 8 周。
- **Guided Learning 不是答案引擎。** 工具是从 LearnLM 一脉做下来的，专门调成先建立理解再给答案——11 万 3 千多次对话里，91.4% 是在搭建概念理解，Gemini 76% 的回复在抛引导性问题，只有 2% 直接给答案。
- **以老师为中心。** 老师负责设计课程、定目标、主持课堂讨论；焦点小组反馈说老师自己也在用 Gemini 备课，角色从讲师变成了 facilitator。
- **参与度。** 69% 的学生达到了使用目标——这种自愿性教育科技通常只有 5% 左右，对比悬殊。
- **效果。** 整体 +0.258 SD 大约相当于 1.2 到 1.7 年的学习进度；用得最多的子组（一半左右的课用 Gemini、目标 12 小时）拉到 1.8 到 2.5 年。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 单个 8 周试验，单个地区；DeepMind 把它定位成对 AI 教育全球证据库的贡献，不是定论。
