**[OpenAI public policy agenda](https://openai.com/index/public-policy-agenda)** — _OpenAI · Jun 3_

**Main takeaway:** Policy op-ed laying out OpenAI's public policy priorities, framed around five principles (democratization, empowerment, universal prosperity, resilience, adaptability) and aimed at shaping how governments respond to frontier AI risks and opportunities.

**Main methods:**
- **Five guiding principles.** OpenAI says its policy work is anchored to democratization (resisting power concentration), empowerment, universal prosperity, resilience to new AI risks, and adaptability as the tech evolves.
- **Frontier model safety as a national-security issue.** Treats CBRN-relevant general-purpose models as a public-safety category; backs US state frameworks including California SB 53, the New York RAISE Act, and Illinois SB 315, with their emphasis on transparency, public reporting of catastrophic-risk evals, whistleblower protections, and enforceable developer accountability.
- **User-base framing.** Cites equal gender split, more under-30 and over-30 users than any other AI platform, and more users earning under $100K than over — pitched as evidence that the access mission is on track.
- **Scope of engagement.** Says OpenAI works with governments and civil society to help policymakers understand the tech, improve government function, and broaden democratic participation in the AI economy.
- **Caveat.** This is a public-policy positioning piece, not a research post — no benchmarks, no new technical results.

**[OpenAI 的公共政策议程](https://openai.com/index/public-policy-agenda)** — _OpenAI · 6月3日_

**Main takeaway:** 一篇 policy op-ed，OpenAI 把自己在公共政策上的优先事项摊开讲，整个框架围绕五条原则（democratization、empowerment、universal prosperity、resilience、adaptability），目的是影响各国政府怎么应对 frontier AI 带来的风险和机会。

**Main methods:**
- **五条指导原则。** OpenAI 说他们的政策工作就建在这五条上：democratization（反对技术权力集中到少数人手里）、empowerment、universal prosperity、面对新 AI 风险的 resilience，以及随着技术发展不断更新立场的 adaptability。
- **把 frontier model safety 当国家安全问题。** 涉及 CBRN 风险的通用 frontier model 被划成 public-safety 范畴；他们明确支持美国几个州的框架，包括加州 SB 53、纽约 RAISE Act、伊利诺伊 SB 315，重点是 transparency、catastrophic-risk eval 的公开披露、whistleblower 保护，以及对没尽到安全义务的开发者要有可执行的问责。
- **关于自己用户构成的叙事。** 文章拿出一组数字：男女用户基本对半，30 岁以下和 30 岁以上用户都比任何其他 AI 平台多，年收入 10 万美元以下的用户也多于以上的，用这套数据说明他们"让 AI 普惠"的 mission 在落地。
- **engagement 的范围。** OpenAI 说他们会跟各国政府和 civil society 合作，帮 policymaker 理解这套技术、提升政府运作效率，并扩大大家参与 AI 经济的渠道。
- **一点需要说在前面的。** 这是一篇 policy positioning，不是研究文章，里面没有 benchmark，也没有新的技术结果。
