**[How Omio is building the future of conversational travel](https://openai.com/index/omio)** — _OpenAI · Jun 23_

**Main takeaway:** Travel platform Omio worked with OpenAI to move from search-based booking to a conversational AI experience, and used the same models to transform internal product development — cutting some build efforts from multiple developers over a quarter to one developer in a month.

**Main methods:**
- **Conversational travel via ChatGPT.** Since 2023, Omio has connected OpenAI models directly to its transportation inventory and booking systems, letting users ask natural-language questions like "Should I take a train or flight from Paris to Barcelona?" and get real, bookable journeys.
- **Live data grounding.** Responses are grounded in real-time transportation pricing and inventory across more than 3,000 transportation providers in 47 countries, rather than static info.
- **Expanded dedicated ChatGPT experience.** Omio built out a dedicated ChatGPT experience tied to its global transportation network, pushing toward what it calls "conversational commerce."
- **Internal AI-native transformation.** Beyond the customer-facing product, Omio is rolling out OpenAI tools across internal workflows to speed up engineering and product development.
- **Headline efficiency claim.** The case study reports a shift from needing multiple developers over a quarter to one developer completing similar work in a month.

**[Omio 怎么用 OpenAI 做对话式旅行](https://openai.com/index/omio)** — _OpenAI · 6月23日_

**Main takeaway:** 旅行平台 Omio 跟 OpenAI 合作，把订票体验从传统搜索式改成对话式，同时把同一套模型用到内部产品开发上，把原来多个开发者干一个季度的活，压缩成一个开发者一个月就能搞定。

**Main methods:**
- **靠 ChatGPT 做对话式订票。** Omio 从2023年起就把 OpenAI 模型接进自己的交通库存和订票系统，用户直接问"巴黎到巴塞罗那该坐火车还是飞机"，就能拿到真实可订的行程。
- **数据是实时的。** 回答背后接的是覆盖47个国家、3000多家交通服务商的实时价格和库存数据，不是死的静态信息。
- **专属 ChatGPT 体验上线。** Omio 又做了一个绑定自己全球交通网络的专属 ChatGPT 体验，往他们说的"对话式商业"方向再推一步。
- **内部也在 AI 化。** 除了对客户的产品，Omio 把 OpenAI 的工具铺到内部工作流里，加速工程和产品开发。
- **效率数字很抢眼。** 案例里提到原来需要多个开发者干一个季度的活，现在一个开发者一个月就能完成。
