**[How Preply combines AI and human tutors to personalize learning](https://openai.com/index/preply)** — _OpenAI · Jun 12_

**Main takeaway:** Language-learning marketplace Preply built on OpenAI models to ship AI-generated lesson summaries, personalised feedback, and practice exercises alongside its human tutors.

**Main methods:**
- **Hybrid product shape.** AI sits next to human tutors rather than replacing them: tutors still teach, AI does the heavy lifting around recap, feedback, and between-session practice.
- **Lesson summaries.** Auto-generated recaps after each session give learners a written artifact of what was covered — historically a manual chore that most tutors skipped.
- **Personalised exercises.** AI generates practice tailored to the learner's recent gaps and the tutor's plan, closing the loop between sessions.
- **Caveat.** This is a customer case study from OpenAI's blog rather than a research result; no eval numbers are reported.

**[Preply 把 AI 和真人 tutor 拼在一起做个性化学习](https://openai.com/index/preply)** — _OpenAI · 6月12日_

**Main takeaway:** 语言学习平台 Preply 用 OpenAI 模型，在真人 tutor 之外加了一层 AI——自动生成课后总结、个性化反馈，还有练习题。

**Main methods:**
- **混合形态。** AI 不是来取代 tutor，而是在旁边搭把手：tutor 继续上课，AI 负责课后回顾、反馈、两次课之间的练习这些累活。
- **课后总结。** 每节课结束自动生成一份文字总结，学生有个东西可以回看；以前这事儿全靠 tutor 手写，多数都省掉了。
- **个性化练习。** AI 根据学生最近的薄弱点和 tutor 的教学计划出题，把两次课中间的空档也利用起来。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是 OpenAI 博客的 customer case study，不是 research，没有任何 eval 数字。
