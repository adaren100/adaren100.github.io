**[How engineers at Nextdoor use Codex to build without limits](https://openai.com/index/nextdoor)** — _OpenAI · Jun 9_

**Main takeaway:** Nextdoor engineers use Codex with GPT-5.5 to chase down hard-to-reproduce bugs, ship across platforms, and spend more time on product outcomes instead of plumbing.

**Main methods:**
- **GPT-5.5 powered Codex.** The team is on the latest Codex model — framing is that capability headroom matters more than the team is large.
- **Hard-to-repro investigations.** Codex is used to dig into flaky bugs that humans struggle to reproduce — a classic on-call pain point.
- **Cross-platform builds.** Engineers ship across multiple platforms with Codex doing more of the per-platform translation work.
- **Product over plumbing.** Reported effect: more time on product outcomes vs. low-level engineering churn.
- **Caveat.** Customer-spotlight blog, no quantified before/after numbers.

**[Nextdoor 的工程师用 Codex 不受限地开工](https://openai.com/index/nextdoor)** — _OpenAI · 6月9日_

**Main takeaway:** Nextdoor 工程师用搭载 GPT-5.5 的 Codex 排查难复现的 bug、跨平台同时发版、把时间放在产品本身而不是底层管线上。

**Main methods:**
- **跑在 GPT-5.5 的 Codex。** 团队用的是最新版 Codex——意思是模型上限比团队大小更重要。
- **难复现 bug 调查。** Codex 用来啃那种人很难复现的 flaky bug，典型 on-call 痛点。
- **跨平台开发。** 工程师同时给多个平台发版，Codex 接手了大部分平台间翻译的活。
- **产品 vs 管线。** 报道的效果是工程师把更多时间放在产品上而不是底层杂活上。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是 customer spotlight，没有任何前后对比数字。
