**[Patch the Planet: a Daybreak initiative to support open source maintainers](https://openai.com/index/patch-the-planet)** — _OpenAI · Jun 22_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI launched Patch the Planet with Trail of Bits, pairing AI-assisted security research using its most cyber-capable models with expert human review to find and actually patch vulnerabilities in critical open-source software, not just discover them.

**Main methods:**
- **Trail of Bits as full-time partner.** Trail of Bits committed its entire security research organization to the initial surge, investigating and validating vulnerabilities, developing patches, and coordinating disclosure directly with maintainers.
- **Human review before maintainers see anything.** Security engineers triage AI-found issues first, so the initiative reduces maintainer workload rather than adding more reports to sort through.
- **Per-project engagement model.** Each collaboration starts with a consultation to figure out what a project actually needs — vulnerability validation, patch development, CI/CD improvements, or longer-term security engineering.
- **Initial cohort.** Nine projects so far: cURL, NATS Server, pyca/cryptography, Sigstore, aiohttp, the Go project, freenginx, Python, and python.org — chosen for their role in networking, cryptography, and supply-chain infrastructure.
- **Tooling provided to participants.** Projects get ChatGPT Pro, conditional access to Codex Security, and API credits, plus Trail of Bits' AI-assisted workflows for deduplication, triage, and patching.
- **Additional partners.** HackerOne and Calif are helping with vulnerability triage, coordinated disclosure, and further discovery work beyond the initial surge.

**[Patch the Planet：Daybreak 旗下帮开源维护者修漏洞的项目](https://openai.com/index/patch-the-planet)** — _OpenAI · 6月22日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 和 Trail of Bits 一起搞了个 Patch the Planet，用他们最强的网络安全模型做 AI 辅助安全研究，再配上专家人工审核，目标不只是找出关键开源软件里的漏洞，而是真正把它们修掉。

**Main methods:**
- **Trail of Bits 全员压上。** Trail of Bits 把整个安全研究团队都投入了这次初期冲刺，直接和维护者一起调查、验证漏洞，开发补丁，并协调披露流程。
- **人工先过一遍再给维护者。** 安全工程师会先 triage AI 找出来的问题，所以这个项目是帮维护者减负，而不是给他们添更多要处理的报告。
- **按项目定制合作方式。** 每次合作都从沟通需求开始，看这个项目最需要的是漏洞验证、补丁开发、CI/CD 改进，还是更长期的安全工程支持。
- **首批九个项目。** cURL、NATS Server、pyca/cryptography、Sigstore、aiohttp、Go 项目、freenginx、Python 和 python.org，都是在网络、加密、供应链基础设施里举足轻重的项目。
- **给参与项目配了工具。** 包括 ChatGPT Pro、有条件开放的 Codex Security access，还有 API credits，外加 Trail of Bits 自己做的去重、triage、打补丁的 AI 辅助工作流。
- **还有别的合作方。** HackerOne 和 Calif 帮着做漏洞 triage、协调披露，以及在初期冲刺之外做更多漏洞挖掘。
