**[David Vélez and Robin Vince join the boards of the OpenAI Foundation and OpenAI Group PBC](https://openai.com/index/david-velez-robin-vince-join-openai-boards)** — _OpenAI · Jul 21_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI appointed Nubank founder/CEO David Vélez and BNY Chairman/CEO Robin Vince to the boards of the OpenAI Foundation and OpenAI Group PBC, adding global financial-services leadership as the company keeps scaling.

**Main methods:**
- **Vélez's background.** Founded Nubank in 2013 after seeing the cost and complexity of traditional Brazilian banking firsthand; Nubank has since grown to 135M+ customers and is expanding into the US after securing conditional approval for a national bank charter.
- **Vince's background.** Chairman and CEO since 2022 of BNY, a 240-year-old global financial services firm, following a career at BlackRock and Goldman Sachs.
- **Rationale from leadership.** Board chair Bret Taylor cited their experience using technology to reshape financial services and expand access at global scale as valuable for OpenAI's growth into serving more businesses and people.
- **Philanthropic ties.** Vélez and his wife Mariel Reyes lead VélezReyes+, a philanthropic platform focused on democratic governance and education across Latin America; both new board members have nonprofit leadership experience in healthcare, education, and the arts.
- **Framing.** Both appointees emphasized governance, trust, and responsible deployment as prerequisites for realizing AI's economic potential.

**[David Vélez 和 Robin Vince 加入 OpenAI Foundation 和 OpenAI Group PBC 董事会](https://openai.com/index/david-velez-robin-vince-join-openai-boards)** — _OpenAI · 7月21日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 把 Nubank 创始人兼 CEO David Vélez，还有 BNY 董事长兼 CEO Robin Vince 拉进了 OpenAI Foundation 和 OpenAI Group PBC 的董事会，给公司持续扩张补上了全球金融服务领域的治理经验。

**Main methods:**
- **Vélez 的背景。** 他在 2013 年创立 Nubank，起因是自己在巴西亲身体会到传统银行又贵又麻烦。Nubank 现在已经有 1.35 亿多客户，拿到有条件批准之后正在美国申请国民银行牌照扩张。
- **Vince 的背景。** 2022 年起出任 BNY 的董事长兼 CEO，这家公司有 240 年历史，此前他在 BlackRock 和 Goldman Sachs 都待过。
- **公司给出的理由。** 董事会主席 Bret Taylor 说，两人在用技术重塑金融服务、把机会扩展到全球规模这件事上很有经验，这对 OpenAI 服务更多企业和个人的下一步很有价值。
- **公益背景。** Vélez 和太太 Mariel Reyes 一起在拉美地区做 VélezReyes+，专注民主治理和教育；两位新董事在医疗、教育、艺术领域都有非营利组织的领导经验。
- **两人的表态。** 两位新董事都强调，治理、信任和负责任的部署，是 AI 经济潜力能兑现的前提。
