**[Helping K–12 educators build practical AI skills](https://openai.com/index/k-12-educators-practical-skills)** — _OpenAI · Jul 8_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI Academy and the Walton Family Foundation are running a flagship "AI Skills Jam" for K–12 educators this summer, bringing hands-on AI training to more than 1,600 teachers, administrators, and district leaders across U.S. cities.

**Main methods:**
- **In-person workshop format.** Modeled on OpenAI's earlier Nonprofit AI Jam and Small Business AI Jam, the Jam pairs teachers and administrators with OpenAI mentors for hands-on sessions on lesson planning, communications, and admin tasks.
- **Confirmed multi-city rollout.** Sessions are running in Jonesboro GA, Fairfax VA, Orlando FL (at the PTA Conference), and Chicago, with local district partners like Clayton County Public Schools and Fairfax County Public Schools.
- **Cited time-savings research.** A Walton Family Foundation/Gallup report ("Teaching for Tomorrow") found teachers using AI tools weekly save an average of 5.9 hours per week — roughly six weeks over a school year.
- **Ongoing platform, not one-off event.** Participants get connected to OpenAI Academy, a free online resource hub, as part of a stated goal to build long-term relationships with educators and school systems rather than a single workshop.

**[OpenAI 联合 Walton 基金会,给 K-12 老师办 AI 技能训练营](https://openai.com/index/k-12-educators-practical-skills)** — _OpenAI · 7月8日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI Academy 和 Walton Family Foundation 今年夏天联手办了一场旗舰级的 "AI Skills Jam",面向 K-12 老师,美国多个城市线下开课,覆盖 1,600 多名教师、校长和学区负责人。

**Main methods:**
- **线下手把手的工作坊形式。** 延续之前 Nonprofit AI Jam 和 Small Business AI Jam 的路子,老师和校长跟 OpenAI 的 mentor 一对一学怎么用 AI 做备课、写沟通材料、处理行政杂事。
- **多个城市已经排上日程。** 目前确认的场次在 Jonesboro (GA)、Fairfax (VA)、Orlando (FL,借着 PTA 大会) 和 Chicago,合作的学区包括 Clayton County 和 Fairfax County 的公立学校系统。
- **背后有数据支撑。** Walton Family Foundation 和 Gallup 做的 "Teaching for Tomorrow" 报告发现,每周用 AI 工具的老师平均一周能省下 5.9 小时,一整个学年下来相当于多出六周时间。
- **不是办一次就完事。** 参加的老师会被接入 OpenAI Academy 这个免费在线平台继续学,OpenAI 说这是想跟教育系统建立长期关系,不是搞一次性活动。
