**[How Deutsche Telekom is rewiring telecommunications with AI](https://openai.com/index/deutsche-telekom)** — _OpenAI · Jul 10_

**Main takeaway:** A customer case study: Deutsche Telekom, serving 300M+ customers and 200,000+ employees, is pushing to become one of the world's first AI-native telcos, with ChatGPT Enterprise and API tooling now at 50,000+ monthly active users and usage up 546% since the start of 2026.

**Main methods:**
- **Employee-first rollout.** Phase one gave employees broad access to ChatGPT Enterprise and encouraged open experimentation, which drove fast organic adoption before any top-down mandate.
- **Customer care as first target.** Customer service was one of the earliest workflows redesigned around AI, with leadership framing AI support as eventually outperforming traditional models in certain scenarios as it accumulates more context per interaction.
- **AI embedded directly in live calls.** Working with OpenAI and other partners, Deutsche Telekom is shipping live translation, in-call assistants, and post-call summaries built into existing phone calls, with no new app required from customers.
- **Real-time network optimization.** AI is used with network partners to dynamically reallocate mobile network resources as demand shifts through the day, from commuter traffic to large sporting events.
- **Reframed as a redesign, not a bolt-on.** Leadership explicitly describes the effort as redesigning how decisions get made and how customer journeys are built, not layering AI onto existing processes.

**[Deutsche Telekom 想把电信业务用 AI 重新搭一遍](https://openai.com/index/deutsche-telekom)** — _OpenAI · 7月10日_

**Main takeaway:** 一篇客户案例：服务超过 3 亿用户、员工超过 20 万的 Deutsche Telekom，正朝着"全球最早一批 AI-native 电信公司"这个目标冲，现在 ChatGPT Enterprise 和 API 工具月活用户已经 5 万多，从 2026 年初到现在用量涨了 546%。

**Main methods:**
- **先放开给员工用。** 第一阶段直接给员工开放 ChatGPT Enterprise，鼓励大家自己去摸索，没有自上而下强推，采用速度反而更快。
- **客服是第一个啃的硬骨头。** 客户服务是最早被重新设计的工作流之一，管理层认为随着系统积累的交互上下文越来越多，AI 客服在某些场景下最终能跑赢传统人工支持模式。
- **AI 直接嵌进通话里。** 跟 OpenAI 等合作伙伴一起，把实时翻译、通话中助手、通话后摘要这些能力直接做进现有电话通话里，用户不用装新 app。
- **网络运营也实时调。** 跟网络合作伙伴一起用 AI 动态调配移动网络资源，早晚高峰通勤、大型体育赛事这些场景下的需求变化都能实时响应。
- **强调是重新设计，不是加个功能。** 管理层明确说这不是在现有流程上加一层 AI，而是从决策方式到客户旅程设计整个重新来一遍。
