**[BBVA puts AI at the core of banking with OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/bbva)** — _OpenAI · Jun 11_

**Main takeaway:** Spanish bank BBVA scaled ChatGPT Enterprise to 100,000 employees and is working with OpenAI to weave AI through its core banking operations globally.

**Main methods:**
- **Full-workforce rollout.** 100,000 employees on ChatGPT Enterprise — one of the larger named enterprise deployments OpenAI has publicized.
- **Core-banking depth, not just productivity.** The partnership goes beyond chat assistance into AI-powered banking workflows worldwide.
- **Anchor customer in a regulated industry.** Frames OpenAI's enterprise pitch to other financial institutions, where compliance and accuracy bar are high.
- **Caveat.** A customer case study with no specific eval numbers or feature breakdown — read as a deployment-scale signal, not a research finding.

**[BBVA 把 AI 放进银行核心业务](https://openai.com/index/bbva)** — _OpenAI · 6月11日_

**Main takeaway:** 西班牙银行 BBVA 把 ChatGPT Enterprise 推到了 10 万员工，并和 OpenAI 一起把 AI 织进全球的核心银行业务里。

**Main methods:**
- **全员铺开。** 10 万人用 ChatGPT Enterprise——这是 OpenAI 公布过的较大企业部署之一。
- **深入核心银行业务。** 合作不只停在聊天助手，而是把 AI 用到全球银行业务流程里。
- **受监管行业的样板。** 给 OpenAI 拉其他金融机构提供了一个参照——这种行业合规和准确度门槛高，签下来不容易。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是 case study，没有具体 eval 数字也没说具体功能，应该当作部署规模的信号而不是研究成果来看。
