**[How ChatGPT adoption has expanded](https://openai.com/index/how-chatgpt-adoption-has-expanded)** — _OpenAI · Jun 30_

**Main takeaway:** New OpenAI Signals data from a 0.1% sample of individual ChatGPT users shows that after 6 months, users send 50% more daily messages and have tried 2× as many distinct task categories compared to sign-up month; the fastest relative growth since July 2023 is in Africa, Asia, and lower-HDI countries.

**Main methods:**
- **Depth and breadth both grow over time.** Six months in, users send 50% more daily messages and have cumulatively tried twice as many distinct capabilities (out of 53 classifier categories) compared to their first 28 days.
- **Sample and methodology.** Based on a 0.1% sample of accounts created 2025-10-15 to 2026-05-01, with activity measured through 2026-05-31; banned users, under-18s, and those who sent no messages in their first 28 days are excluded.
- **Africa and Asia lead relative growth.** Every region's weekly active user count has risen sharply vs. the July 2023 baseline, with Africa and Asia outpacing other regions in percentage terms.
- **Lower-HDI countries catching up fastest.** The same relative growth pattern holds when sliced by 2023 Human Development Index: lower-HDI country cohorts are growing the most quickly.
- **Low-cost access as a driver.** OpenAI attributes part of this growth to continued availability of Free and Go plans as low-barrier entry points.

**[ChatGPT 的全球用户增长报告](https://openai.com/index/how-chatgpt-adoption-has-expanded)** — _OpenAI · 6月30日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 公布了 Signals 数据：抽取 0.1% 的个人用户样本，发现注册 6 个月后，用户每天发的消息多了 50%，用过的不同任务类别翻了一倍；自 2023 年 7 月以来，相对增长最快的是非洲、亚洲和低人类发展指数国家。

**Main methods:**
- **用得越来越多、越来越广。** 注册 6 个月后，用户每天消息量比刚注册时多 50%，累积试过的不同功能类别（共 53 类）翻了一倍。
- **样本与方法。** 取 2025 年 10 月到 2026 年 5 月间创建账号的 0.1% 样本，追踪到 2026 年 5 月底；封号用户、未成年人、注册后 28 天内没发过消息的均排除。
- **非洲和亚洲相对增速最快。** 以 2023 年 7 月为基准，各地区每周活跃用户数都显著上涨，非洲和亚洲的百分比增幅领先。
- **低 HDI 国家跑得更快。** 按 2023 年人类发展指数分组同样能看到这个趋势：低 HDI 国家用户增长相对速度最猛。
- **低价入口支撑增长。** OpenAI 把部分增长归因于持续提供免费版和 Go 套餐，维持了低门槛进入。
