**[Samsung Electronics brings ChatGPT and Codex to employees](https://openai.com/index/samsung-electronics-chatgpt-codex-deployment)** — _OpenAI · Jun 21_

**Main takeaway:** Samsung Electronics is rolling out ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex to all employees in Korea and to all Device eXperience division employees worldwide, marking one of OpenAI's largest enterprise deployments to date.

**Main methods:**
- **Scope of rollout.** All Samsung Electronics employees in Korea, plus all DX division employees worldwide, get access to both ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex.
- **Use across functions, not just engineering.** Samsung plans to use the tools for R&D, manufacturing, marketing, and corporate functions, with Codex extending beyond coding into building internal tools, websites, and automated workflows.
- **Enterprise-grade controls.** ChatGPT Enterprise provides data protection, user/access management, and security controls so employees can use it within Samsung's governance framework.
- **Adoption already accelerating.** Codex weekly active users in Korea have grown nearly 800% since February 1, 2026, and Codex now has more than 5 million weekly active users overall.
- **Builds on an existing infrastructure relationship.** Samsung already supplies advanced memory semiconductors for OpenAI's AI infrastructure; this deployment expands the partnership into workforce-wide AI adoption.

**[三星电子把 ChatGPT 和 Codex 铺给全员用](https://openai.com/index/samsung-electronics-chatgpt-codex-deployment)** — _OpenAI · 6月21日_

**Main takeaway:** 三星电子要给韩国全体员工，以及全球范围内 Device eXperience 部门的所有员工，都开通 ChatGPT Enterprise 和 Codex，这是 OpenAI 目前最大规模的企业部署之一。

**Main methods:**
- **覆盖范围。** 韩国的三星电子全体员工，加上全球范围内 DX 部门的所有员工，都能用上 ChatGPT Enterprise 和 Codex。
- **不只是给工程师用。** 三星打算把这套工具用在研发、制造、市场、企业职能等各个环节，Codex 也不只写代码，还能拿来搭内部工具、网站和自动化工作流。
- **企业级的管控。** ChatGPT Enterprise 自带数据保护、用户和权限管理、安全控制，让员工能在三星的治理框架内放心用。
- **增长已经很明显。** 韩国 Codex 的周活用户从 2026 年 2 月 1 日到现在涨了将近 800%，Codex 整体周活用户也超过了 500 万。
- **接的是之前的基础设施合作。** 三星本来就在给 OpenAI 供应下一代 AI 基础设施需要的高端内存芯片，这次部署把合作从基础设施延伸到了全员级的 AI 应用。
