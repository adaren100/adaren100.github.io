**[A blueprint for democratic governance of frontier AI](https://openai.com/index/frontier-safety-blueprint)** — _OpenAI · Jun 3_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI publishes a blueprint for a US federal framework for frontier-AI governance, built on a three-part strategy: a national framework that borrows from state safety laws, a strengthened CAISI as the lead federal institution, and a broader cross-government resilience plan.

**Main methods:**
- **Three-part strategy.** (1) National framework aligned with the emerging state-law consensus; (2) strengthen CAISI as the US government's primary frontier-AI safety institution; (3) mobilize a resilience plan across government for national-security and public-safety challenges.
- **Building on state momentum.** Names California SB 53, New York's RAISE Act, and Illinois's SB 315 as the converging state baselines the federal layer should harmonize.
- **Federal trigger point.** Cites the White House executive order on Promoting Advanced AI Innovation and Security as the moment to move from state patchwork to a durable federal framework.
- **Framed as a positioning document.** Functions as OpenAI's pitch to US lawmakers rather than a research artifact — the post mostly points to the full blueprint PDF for the substance.
- **Caveat.** No technical content or evals here; this is policy advocacy and pairs with the same-day public-policy agenda post.

**[OpenAI 给 frontier AI 治理画了一张联邦层级的蓝图](https://openai.com/index/frontier-safety-blueprint)** — _OpenAI · 6月3日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 放出一份 blueprint，提议美国搭一套联邦层级的 frontier AI 治理框架，整体思路分三步：把各州现有 safety law 的共识抬到联邦层；把 CAISI 强化成联邦层面主管 frontier AI 安全的机构；再在整个政府层面铺一套 resilience plan。

**Main methods:**
- **三步走的总体策略。** （1）搭一个跟现有州法 consensus 接得上的全国框架；（2）把 CAISI 强化成美国联邦层面主管 frontier AI 安全的核心机构；（3）把 national-security 和 public-safety 相关的应对动员到整个政府层面。
- **借力各州已经跑出来的势头。** 文章点名加州 SB 53、纽约 RAISE Act 和伊利诺伊 SB 315，把这几个州的共识当成联邦层应该去对齐和统一的 baseline。
- **联邦动起来的契机。** 拿白宫那份 Promoting Advanced AI Innovation and Security 的 executive order 作为切入点，说现在正是把零散的州层规定汇成一个稳态联邦框架的时候。
- **这是一份立场文件。** 与其说是研究产出，不如说是 OpenAI 给美国立法机构的一份提案，正文主要在引导你去读完整的 blueprint PDF。
