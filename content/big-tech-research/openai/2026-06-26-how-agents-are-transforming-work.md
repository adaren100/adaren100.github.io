**[How agents are transforming work](https://openai.com/index/how-agents-are-transforming-work)** — _OpenAI · Jun 25_

**Main takeaway:** A new OpenAI economic research paper shows Codex has become OpenAI's dominant internal AI tool, now accounting for 99.8% of weekly output tokens generated company-wide, as both OpenAI staff and external users shift from short chatbot interactions to longer, delegated agentic tasks.

**Main methods:**
- **Internal adoption curve.** Through August 2025 the average OpenAI worker spent under 10% of tokens on Codex; now every department, including non-technical ones like Legal and Recruiting, uses Codex as its primary AI tool, with Codex making up over 85% of an average worker's output tokens.
- **Task horizons keep growing.** By May 2026, 80.6% of sampled individual users made a Codex request estimated to exceed 30 minutes of human-equivalent work, 70.2% exceeded one hour, and 25.6% exceeded eight hours.
- **Non-developer adoption is outpacing developers.** Since August 2025, non-developer usage rose 137x for individual users, 189x for organizational users, and 12x within OpenAI.
- **Codex pulls people outside their job description.** Non-technical staff in Legal, Finance, and Recruiting now regularly use Codex for coding-adjacent work such as automation, data transformation, tooling, and debugging.
- **Nearly a quarter of requests are hour-plus tasks.** The paper frames this as evidence that agentic AI is shifting the basic unit of knowledge work from short, self-contained chatbot interactions to delegated, long-horizon tasks.

**[Codex 怎么把 OpenAI 自己的工作方式改了](https://openai.com/index/how-agents-are-transforming-work)** — _OpenAI · 6月25日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 这篇新的经济研究报告说，Codex 已经成了公司内部用得最多的 AI 工具，现在占到全公司每周输出 token 的 99.8%，不管是内部员工还是外部用户，都在从短对话式交互转向更长、更愿意"委托出去"的 agent 任务。

**Main methods:**
- **内部采用曲线。** 2025 年 8 月之前，OpenAI 员工平均只把不到 10% 的 token 花在 Codex 上；现在包括 Legal、Recruiting 这些非技术部门都把 Codex 当成主力工具，平均每个员工的输出 token 里 Codex 占比超过 85%。
- **任务时长一路往上走。** 到 2026 年 5 月，80.6% 的抽样个人用户至少发过一次预估超过 30 分钟人力工作量的 Codex 请求，70.2% 超过一小时，25.6% 超过八小时。
- **非开发者的采用速度反超开发者。** 2025 年 8 月以来，非开发者用户量在个人用户里涨了 137 倍，组织用户里涨了 189 倍，OpenAI 内部涨了 12 倍。
- **Codex 让人干起了"不属于自己岗位"的活。** Legal、Finance、Recruiting 这些非技术员工现在经常用 Codex 做自动化、数据处理、工具搭建、debug 这类技术执行工作。
- **将近四分之一的请求都是一小时起步的任务。** 报告认为这说明 agentic AI 正在把知识工作的基本单位，从一次次短暂独立的对话，变成委托出去的长周期任务。
