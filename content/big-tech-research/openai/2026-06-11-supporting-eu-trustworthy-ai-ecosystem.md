**[Supporting Europe's work in ensuring a trustworthy AI ecosystem](https://openai.com/index/supporting-eu-trustworthy-ai-ecosystem)** — _OpenAI · Jun 11_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI is publicly backing the EU Code of Practice on AI content transparency and committing to advance provenance standards and tooling so people can tell what content is AI-generated.

**Main methods:**
- **Code of Practice endorsement.** OpenAI signs on to the EU's voluntary transparency code as a policy signal alongside the AI Act rollout.
- **Provenance standards.** Continued investment in content credentials / watermarking-style approaches so generated media carries verifiable origin metadata.
- **Tooling for end-users.** A push to make provenance information surface-able in products people actually use, not just in metadata fields no one reads.
- **Caveat.** Policy/Global Affairs post — no technical details on which standards or what's shipping, just positioning.

**[OpenAI 表态支持欧洲建可信 AI 生态](https://openai.com/index/supporting-eu-trustworthy-ai-ecosystem)** — _OpenAI · 6月11日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 公开支持 EU Code of Practice 里关于 AI 内容透明度的部分，承诺继续推动 provenance 标准和工具落地，让大家能看出哪些内容是 AI 生成的。

**Main methods:**
- **签 Code of Practice。** 在 EU AI Act 落地的节点上，签欧盟自愿性的透明度准则算一个政策表态。
- **provenance 标准。** 继续投入 content credentials 这类做法，给生成内容打上可验证的来源元数据。
- **面向用户的工具。** 把 provenance 信息做到普通用户能看到的产品里，而不是埋在没人看的元数据字段里。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是政策/Global Affairs 性质的发文，没有技术细节，只表明立场。
