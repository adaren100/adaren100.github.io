**[How sales teams use ChatGPT Work](https://openai.com/academy/codex-for-work/how-sales-teams-use-codex)** — _OpenAI · Jul 14_

**Main takeaway:** An OpenAI Academy on-demand webinar on how sales teams use ChatGPT Work to turn account context, customer conversations, and deal signals into pipeline briefs, meeting prep packets, forecast reviews, account plans, and stalled-deal diagnoses.

**Main methods:**
- **Pulls together scattered sales context.** Sales work lives across CRM fields, call notes, email threads, Slack discussions, decks, customer docs, and account signals; ChatGPT Work assembles that context into the first usable version of the artifact.
- **Five headline artifacts.** Prioritized account briefs, meeting prep packets, forecast risk reviews, account strategy packs, and stalled-deal diagnoses — with sellers and managers still owning relationship strategy and judgment.
- **A dedicated sales plugin.** The ChatGPT Work sales plugin surfaces high-priority accounts and signals, preps customer meetings, completes follow-ups, updates records, builds close plans, and reviews at-risk deals, using tools like Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Outreach, Clay, Rox, and Actively.
- **Formerly the Codex app.** The webinar was recorded when these workflows lived in the former Codex app; they now run in ChatGPT Work at chatgpt.com or in the desktop app.

**[销售团队怎么用 ChatGPT Work](https://openai.com/academy/codex-for-work/how-sales-teams-use-codex)** — _OpenAI · 7月14日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI Academy 的一期点播 webinar，讲销售团队怎么用 ChatGPT Work 把客户背景、客户对话和成交信号变成 pipeline 简报、会前准备包、forecast 复盘、客户经营计划，还能诊断卡住不动的 deal。

**Main methods:**
- **把散落各处的销售上下文拉到一起。** 销售信息分散在 CRM 字段、通话记录、邮件、Slack 讨论、售前材料和客户文档里，ChatGPT Work 负责把这些拼成第一版能用的产物。
- **五类核心产出。** 按优先级排好的客户简报、会前准备包、forecast 风险复盘、客户策略包，以及卡壳 deal 的诊断。客户关系策略和判断还是销售自己拿主意。
- **专门的销售 plugin。** ChatGPT Work 的 sales plugin 能找出高优先级客户和信号、准备客户会议、跟进待办、更新客户记录、制定 close plan、复盘有风险的 deal，对接 Salesforce、HubSpot、Slack、Outreach、Clay、Rox、Actively 这些工具。
- **前身是 Codex app。** webinar 录制时这些 workflow 还在原来的 Codex app 里，现在都搬到了 ChatGPT Work，网页版和桌面版都能用。
