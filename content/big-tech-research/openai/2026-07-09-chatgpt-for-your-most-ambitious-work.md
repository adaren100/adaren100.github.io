**[ChatGPT is now a partner for your most ambitious work](https://openai.com/index/chatgpt-for-your-most-ambitious-work)** — _OpenAI · Jul 9_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI launches ChatGPT Work, a Codex-powered agent that takes on multi-step professional workflows—creating slides, sheets, docs, and web apps—and can stay with complex projects for hours while coordinating across apps, files, and third-party tools.

**Main methods:**
- **Codex engine built in.** More than 5 million people use Codex weekly; over 1 million now use it for non-software tasks, showing the breadth of what the underlying agent capability can handle.
- **Powered by GPT-5.6.** The new frontier model makes ChatGPT state of the art at reasoning through multi-step tasks and producing outputs that follow user templates and reference files.
- **End-to-end workflow chaining.** A single prompt can kick off research → campaign brief → marketing assets → market adaptations, with context carried through every step automatically.
- **Scheduled Tasks.** ChatGPT Work can run independently while users are offline, turning Teams and Slack updates into docs or slides and notifying the team of key changes.
- **Early enterprise results.** Zapier used it to review thousands of leads monthly by tracing touchpoints across CRM, email, and other tools, surfacing a weekly exec dashboard that revealed seven figures in missed pipeline.

**[ChatGPT 来接手你最雄心勃勃的工作了](https://openai.com/index/chatgpt-for-your-most-ambitious-work)** — _OpenAI · 7月9日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 推出 ChatGPT Work，一个基于 Codex 技术的 agent，能接手跨 app、跨文件的多步骤专业工作流，生成幻灯片、表格、文档和 web app，还能独立撑着复杂项目跑好几个小时。

**Main methods:**
- **底层跑 Codex。** Codex 每周有超过 500 万用户在用，其中 100 多万人已经把它用在软件开发以外的工作上，说明这个 agent 能力的覆盖面相当宽。
- **模型换成 GPT-5.6。** 这个最新旗舰把 ChatGPT 推到了多步任务推理和按模板生成内容的新高度。
- **端到端工作流串联。** 一句话可以触发整条链路：用户调研 → 营销简报 → 创意素材 → 各市场本地化，每一步的上下文都自动带着走。
- **Scheduled Tasks 离线继续跑。** 用户不在电脑前的时候，ChatGPT Work 照样能把 Teams 和 Slack 的新消息整理成更新后的文档或幻灯片，顺手把关键变化告知团队。
- **企业早期实测。** Zapier 拿它搭了一套每月复盘数千条线索的系统，追踪 CRM、邮件等各平台的用户触点，每周生成一份高管 dashboard，直接揭出了潜在 pipeline 里漏掉的七位数销售机会。
