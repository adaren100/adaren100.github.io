**[New OpenAI Academy courses for the next era of work](https://openai.com/index/academy-courses-applying-ai-at-work)** — _OpenAI · Jun 12_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI is launching three new Academy courses aimed at helping workers build practical AI skills, package what they do into repeatable workflows, and start using agents in everyday tasks.

**Main methods:**
- **Three-course bundle.** The new tracks cover applying AI at work day-to-day, turning ad-hoc prompting into reusable workflows, and getting started with agents — pitched at the general workforce rather than developers.
- **Workflow focus.** Material emphasizes converting one-off ChatGPT sessions into repeatable processes a team can share, which is the gap most non-technical adopters hit after the novelty wears off.
- **Agent on-ramp.** A dedicated course introduces using agents in everyday work — OpenAI's read on where mainstream productivity is heading next.
- **Caveat.** This is an education/adoption announcement rather than a research drop; no new model or benchmark is involved.

**[OpenAI Academy 出了三门新课](https://openai.com/index/academy-courses-applying-ai-at-work)** — _OpenAI · 6月12日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 新开三门 Academy 课程，重点教普通职场人怎么把 AI 用起来、怎么把零散的 prompting 整理成可复用的 workflow，以及怎么在日常工作里开始用 agent。

**Main methods:**
- **三门一套。** 三个 track 分别讲日常工作里怎么用 AI、怎么把临时性的 ChatGPT 操作整理成团队能复用的流程，以及 agent 入门，面向普通员工而不是开发者。
- **重点在 workflow。** 内容强调把一次性的对话变成可重复的流程，这正是大多数非技术用户尝鲜过后卡住的地方。
- **agent 入门。** 单独一门课带大家上手在工作里用 agent，能看出 OpenAI 觉得下一波生产力提升会从这里来。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是教育推广性质的发布，不是 research drop，没有新模型也没有新 benchmark。
