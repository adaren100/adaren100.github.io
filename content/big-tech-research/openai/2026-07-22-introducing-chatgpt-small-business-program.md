**[Introducing the ChatGPT for small business program](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-small-business-program)** — _OpenAI · Jul 22_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI launched a ChatGPT for small businesses program to help entrepreneurs adopt AI, building on last year's Small Business AI Jams where 78% of participants built a working AI workflow in a single day and 42% saved 5+ hours a week.

**Main methods:**
- **Hands-on virtual training.** Product-specific webinars showing day-to-day use cases for ChatGPT Work, with prompts to try and workflows across accounting, marketing, and ecommerce, plus partner webinars and Q&As.
- **In-person AI academies.** In-person events across the US run with the OpenAI Academy team, pairing guided instruction and hands-on exercises with peer support from other small business owners.
- **New starter content.** Customer stories, interactive guides that can be uploaded directly into ChatGPT Work, and short-form videos with specific prompts and examples for getting started in minutes.
- **Partner ecosystem for common workflows.** Plugins, skills, and offers from partners including Dropbox, Shopify, Intuit, Slack, Atlassian, and Wix, built for common small-business workflows.
- **Feedback loop into the roadmap.** OpenAI is collecting input via webinar Q&As, local events, and surveys to shape future product decisions for this segment.

**[OpenAI 推出面向小企业的 ChatGPT 计划](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-small-business-program)** — _OpenAI · 7月22日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 上线了一个专门面向小企业的 ChatGPT 项目，帮企业主用起 AI。去年的 Small Business AI Jams 活动里，78% 的参与者一天内就搭出了能用的 AI workflow，42% 每周省出 5 个多小时。

**Main methods:**
- **线上实操培训。** 针对具体产品的 webinar，展示 ChatGPT Work 在日常场景里怎么用，会给到可以直接抄的 prompt，覆盖记账、营销、电商等workflow，还有合作伙伴的 webinar 和 Q&A。
- **线下 AI 学院。** OpenAI Academy 团队在美国各地办线下活动，把手把手教学、实操练习和其他小企业主之间的经验分享拉到一起。
- **新手上路内容。** 客户案例、可以直接传进 ChatGPT Work 的交互式指南，加上短视频，都配好了具体 prompt 和示例，几分钟就能上手。
- **合作伙伴生态。** Dropbox、Shopify、Intuit、Slack、Atlassian、Wix 等合作方提供的插件、skills 和专属优惠，专门针对小企业常见的 workflow 打磨。
- **反馈直接进产品路线图。** OpenAI 通过 webinar Q&A、线下活动和问卷收集反馈，用来决定这条产品线接下来怎么做。
