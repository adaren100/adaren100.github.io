**[GPT-5.6: Frontier intelligence that scales with your ambition](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-6)** — _OpenAI · Jul 9_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI launches the GPT-5.6 family (Sol, Terra, Luna), designed for better intelligence per token and per dollar; Sol sets a new high of 53.6 on Agents' Last Exam—13.1 points above Claude Fable 5—while Terra and Luna match Fable 5 capability at roughly one-sixteenth the estimated cost.

**Main methods:**
- **Three-tier family.** Sol is the flagship (highest capability), Terra is the balanced everyday model, Luna is the most cost-efficient—all generally available following a limited preview.
- **Efficiency as the headline claim.** GPT-5.6 Sol beats Fable 5 by 11.4 points on Agents' Last Exam even at medium reasoning at roughly one-quarter the estimated cost. On the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index it comes within one point of Fable 5 while finishing tasks 61% faster at approximately half the cost.
- **"Ultra" compute mode.** A new on-demand setting coordinates multiple agents across parallel workstreams to accelerate the most demanding long-horizon tasks.
- **Stronger computer use and design judgment.** Sol can inspect, refine, and deliver polished ready-to-use results, positioning it as a capable end-to-end knowledge-work collaborator.
- **Safeguards at launch.** The most extensive evaluation yet—human red-teaming plus large-scale automated testing during preview—with layered real-time checks, monitoring, and trust-calibrated access in production.

**[GPT-5.6 发布：越跑越省，越省越强](https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-6)** — _OpenAI · 7月9日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 正式发布 GPT-5.6 系列三款模型（Sol、Terra、Luna），主打每个 token 和每块钱都更有用；Sol 在 Agents' Last Exam 上拿了 53.6 的新高，比 Claude Fable 5 高出 13.1 分，而 Terra 和 Luna 能在 Fable 5 性能附近跑到大约十六分之一的成本。

**Main methods:**
- **三档模型家族。** Sol 是旗舰（能力最强）、Terra 主打日常平衡、Luna 主打极低成本，三款均在小范围预览后正式开放。
- **效率是核心卖点。** GPT-5.6 Sol 用中档 reasoning 就能比 Fable 5 在 Agents' Last Exam 上高出 11.4 分，成本大约是四分之一。Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index 上它跟 Fable 5 相差不到 1 分，但完成任务快了 61%，成本约减半。
- **新增 "ultra" 算力模式。** 按需开启，协调多个 agent 并行跑工作流，专门加速最重的长链任务。
- **计算机使用和设计判断力提升。** Sol 能帮你检查、打磨并交付完整可用的成果，更像一个能端到端接手知识型工作的合作伙伴。
- **发布时的安全防护。** 正式上线前经历了迄今最密集的评估——人工 red-teaming 加大规模自动化测试，上线后叠加实时检查、监控和按信任程度分级的访问控制。
