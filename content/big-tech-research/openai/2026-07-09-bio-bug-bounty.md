**[GPT-5.5 Bio Bug Bounty](https://openai.com/index/bio-bug-bounty)** — _OpenAI · Jul 9_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI is evolving its one-off GPT-5.5 Bio Bug Bounty into a permanent private program—the OpenAI Bio Bounty Program—focused on finding universal jailbreaks for biosafety guardrails on frontier models, with the reward raised from $25,000 to $50,000.

**Main methods:**
- **Ongoing private program structure.** Rather than a time-limited campaign, OpenAI runs a rolling invite-based program with a continuous application process; past GPT-5.5 applicants don't need to reapply.
- **Scope transition.** The GPT-5.5 Bio Bounty testing window closes July 27, 2026; from that point only GPT-5.6 (and future frontier models) will be in scope.
- **Universal jailbreaks as the target.** The program hunts specifically for jailbreaks that defeat a predefined biosafety challenge end-to-end—not isolated bypasses. Partial wins may receive smaller discretionary awards.
- **Reward doubled.** The prize for a confirmed universal jailbreak is now $50,000 for both GPT-5.5 and GPT-5.6, up from $25,000.
- **NDA and ChatGPT account required.** Accepted participants sign a non-disclosure agreement and must already have a ChatGPT account before onboarding to the bounty platform.

**[OpenAI Bio Bug Bounty 升级为常设计划](https://openai.com/index/bio-bug-bounty)** — _OpenAI · 7月9日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 把原来的 GPT-5.5 生物安全 bug bounty 升级成了长期私有计划——OpenAI Bio Bounty Program，专门盯着能绕过前沿模型生物安全 guardrail 的 universal jailbreak，奖金从 2.5 万美元翻倍到 5 万美元。

**Main methods:**
- **转为常设私有项目。** 不再是限时活动，改成滚动邀请制，持续接受申请；之前报名过 GPT-5.5 Bio Bounty 的研究者不需要重新申请。
- **范围切换时间表。** GPT-5.5 的测试窗口 2026 年 7 月 27 日关闭，之后只有 GPT-5.6（以及后续前沿模型）在范围内。
- **目标锁定 universal jailbreak。** 计划专门追的是能把预设生物安全挑战从头到尾跑通的 jailbreak，不是零碎的单点绕过；部分突破可能酌情拿到较小奖励。
- **奖金翻倍。** 确认有效的 universal jailbreak，GPT-5.5 和 GPT-5.6 各自的奖励都从 2.5 万提到了 5 万美元。
- **NDA + ChatGPT 账号是前提。** 通过审核的参与者需要签保密协议，且必须已有 ChatGPT 账号才能加入 bounty 平台。
