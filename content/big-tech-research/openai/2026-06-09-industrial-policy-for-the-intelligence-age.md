**[Industrial policy for the Intelligence Age](https://openai.com/index/industrial-policy-for-the-intelligence-age)** — _OpenAI · Jun 9_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI lays out a "people-first" industrial policy agenda for the AI era, framed around expanding opportunity, sharing prosperity, and building resilient institutions as advanced intelligence evolves.

**Main methods:**
- **People-first framing.** Policy proposals are pitched around workers and broad-based prosperity, not just industrial competitiveness.
- **Resilient institutions.** A through-line is that institutions need updating to absorb AI-driven shifts in labor and capital.
- **Shared prosperity.** Mechanisms for spreading AI's economic gains beyond the firms building the technology.
- **Caveat.** This is a Global Affairs op-ed style piece, not research — the body wasn't scraped, so specifics live in the linked post.

**[OpenAI 提出 Intelligence Age 的产业政策](https://openai.com/index/industrial-policy-for-the-intelligence-age)** — _OpenAI · 6月9日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 提出一套面向 AI 时代的"以人为本"产业政策主张，主线是扩大机会、共享繁荣，并在 AI 演进的过程中重建有韧性的制度。

**Main methods:**
- **以人为本的定位。** 政策建议围绕劳动者和广泛繁荣展开，不只是产业竞争力的话题。
- **有韧性的制度。** 一条主线是制度本身要更新，才能吸收 AI 带来的劳动和资本变化。
- **共享繁荣。** 提出让 AI 的经济收益扩散到造模型的公司之外的机制。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是 Global Affairs 性质的政策文章，不是研究；正文没抓到，具体主张请看链接。
