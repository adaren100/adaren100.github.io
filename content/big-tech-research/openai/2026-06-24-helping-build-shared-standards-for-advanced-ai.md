**[Helping build shared standards for advanced AI](https://openai.com/index/helping-build-shared-standards-for-advanced-ai)** — _OpenAI · Jun 23_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI helped found the Appia Foundation, hosted by the Linux Foundation, to build open specifications that translate international AI standards into practical, cross-organization assessment criteria.

**Main methods:**
- **Appia Foundation's mandate.** Develops open, modular specs that turn international standards and frameworks into practical assessment criteria across the AI value chain, creating a trust layer for third-party conformity checks.
- **Builds on a governance blueprint.** OpenAI's recent blueprint for democratic governance of frontier AI calls for a durable U.S. framework, a strengthened Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI), and a broader government resilience strategy.
- **International cooperation as a complement to national capacity.** Argues strong national institutions like CAISI should pair with a network of institutions sharing methods and trusted evidence across borders.
- **Disclosure playbook for evaluations.** OpenAI's shared playbook for trustworthy third-party evaluations specifies what frontier assessments should disclose: system tested, tool access, evaluation harness, elicitation methods, resources, and validation checks.
- **Testing partnerships already running.** Partnerships with US CAISI and UK AISI on frontier capability assessments and biological-misuse safeguards have already led to concrete safety improvements in OpenAI's systems.
- **Fits into broader safety infrastructure.** Connects to OpenAI's Preparedness Framework (internal risk management) and Frontier Governance Framework (public-facing regulatory document).

**[OpenAI 牵头搭建前沿 AI 通用标准](https://openai.com/index/helping-build-shared-standards-for-advanced-ai)** — _OpenAI · 6月23日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 联合发起了由 Linux Foundation 主持的 Appia Foundation，要做一套开放规范，把国际标准转化成各家机构都能用的实际评估标准。

**Main methods:**
- **Appia 的定位。** 做开放、模块化的 spec，把国际标准和现有框架转成 AI 全链条上可操作的评估标准，给第三方做 conformity check 提供一个信任层。
- **承接此前的治理蓝图。** OpenAI 之前那份前沿 AI 民主治理蓝图里提到要有稳固的美国框架、加强版的 CAISI（Center for AI Standards and Innovation），以及政府层面更全面的 resilience 策略。
- **国家能力和国际合作要互相补强。** 像 CAISI 这样的机构负责攒技术能力、评估前沿系统；同时一批这样的机构联网，互认评估方法和证据，才能让各国政府形成共同的技术判断。
- **第三方评估的披露标准。** OpenAI 那份关于可信第三方评估的 playbook 里写清楚了前沿评估该披露什么：测的是哪个系统、有没有工具访问权限、evaluation harness 是什么、用了什么方法去 elicit 能力、有多少资源、又怎么验证结果。
- **已经在跑的测试合作。** 跟美国 CAISI 和英国 AISI 的合作，在前沿能力评估和生物安全相关的 safeguard 上，已经实打实推动了 OpenAI 系统的改进。
- **和现有 safety 体系衔接。** 这套思路跟 OpenAI 的 Preparedness Framework（内部风险管理）以及 Frontier Governance Framework（对外的监管文件）是一脉相承的。
