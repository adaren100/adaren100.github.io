**[Introducing the OpenAI Economic Research Exchange](https://openai.com/index/economic-research-exchange)** — _OpenAI · Jun 8_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI is launching the Economic Research Exchange to fund and surface independent research on AI's impact on jobs, productivity, and the economy. Applications are open for selected projects.

**Main methods:**
- **Open application.** Outside researchers can submit project proposals to study AI's economic effects.
- **Scope.** Jobs, productivity, and broader economic impact — meant to build empirical evidence rather than rely on anecdote.
- **Why now.** Reads as OpenAI's bet that the AI-and-work debate needs more rigorous, independent data inputs.
- **Caveat.** Program announcement; no list of funded projects, budget, or methodology disclosed in the blog summary.

**[OpenAI 启动 Economic Research Exchange](https://openai.com/index/economic-research-exchange)** — _OpenAI · 6月8日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 启动 Economic Research Exchange，资助并展示关于 AI 对就业、生产力和经济影响的独立研究，开放项目申请。

**Main methods:**
- **公开申请。** 外部研究者可以提交项目，研究 AI 在经济层面的影响。
- **范围。** 就业、生产力、更广的经济影响——目的是用实证数据替代轶事。
- **为什么是现在。** 看上去是 OpenAI 押注：AI 与工作的讨论需要更严谨、更独立的数据输入。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是项目公告，没有公布资助的项目、预算和方法论。
