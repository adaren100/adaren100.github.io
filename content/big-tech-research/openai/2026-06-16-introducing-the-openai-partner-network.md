**[Introducing the OpenAI Partner Network](https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-partner-network)** — _OpenAI · Jun 14_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI is launching the OpenAI Partner Network, a $150M program to spin up a global ecosystem of systems integrators, management consultancies, technology firms, and data partners who help enterprises actually turn frontier models into deployed workflows. The company also aims to train and certify 300,000 consultants by the end of 2026.

**Main methods:**
- **Reframing the bottleneck.** OpenAI argues capability is no longer the limiting factor for enterprise AI value; the gap is in repeatedly identifying use cases, redesigning workflows, integrating with existing systems, and managing change at scale.
- **$150M ecosystem investment.** Funding goes toward enabling partners to build, sell, and deliver AI solutions on top of OpenAI models, with launch partners drawn from systems integration, consulting, technology, and data verticals.
- **300,000 certified consultants by end of 2026.** A training-and-certification track aims to scale a pool of practitioners who can deliver OpenAI-based solutions inside enterprises.
- **Joint customer case studies.** Highlighted collaborations include Agilent with BCG (deploying AI across instruments, software, and services), eBay with Artium (next-gen AI customer service blending human and agent workflows), and a payroll-platform engagement with Bain.
- **Positioning.** The post frames partners as essential for secure integration, governance, responsible deployment, and adoption — i.e. the non-model parts of enterprise AI rollouts.
- **What it is, honestly.** This is a go-to-market and ecosystem announcement, not a research or model release; no new capabilities, evals, or technical artifacts are introduced.

**[OpenAI Partner Network 上线，砸 1.5 亿美元拉合作方一起做企业 AI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-openai-partner-network)** — _OpenAI · 6月14日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 正式推出 OpenAI Partner Network，拿出 1.5 亿美元拉一批做系统集成、管理咨询、技术和数据的全球合作方，帮企业把 frontier 模型真正落到业务里。他们还定了个目标：到 2026 年底培训并认证 30 万名顾问。

**Main methods:**
- **重新定义瓶颈。** OpenAI 直接说现在企业用 AI 的卡点已经不是模型能力，而是怎么反复找到合适的 use case、改造 workflow、跟现有系统打通、再把人推动起来用。
- **1.5 亿美元砸生态。** 这笔钱用来支持合作方基于 OpenAI 模型一起 build、sell、deliver 解决方案，首批合作方覆盖系统集成、咨询、技术和数据几个方向。
- **30 万认证顾问。** 配套搞了一套培训和认证体系，目标是 2026 年底前培养出一大批能在企业里交付 OpenAI 方案的人。
- **客户联合案例。** 文章里点名了几个：Agilent 跟 BCG 一起把 AI 推到仪器、软件和服务里；eBay 拉上 Artium 做下一代 AI 客服，让人和 agent 一起处理工单；还有跟 Bain 合作把一个 payroll 关键工作流改成了 production 级 AI 方案。
- **合作方的定位。** OpenAI 把 partner 摆在安全集成、治理、负责任部署、change management 这些"非模型"环节上，意思是模型他们出，落地这一公里得靠生态。
- **本质上是什么。** 这是一个 go-to-market 和生态公告，不是研究成果或模型发布，没有新能力、新 eval，也没有新技术产物。
