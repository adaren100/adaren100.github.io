**[From data to decisions: how LSEG is scaling trusted AI](https://openai.com/index/lseg)** — _OpenAI · Jun 10_

**Main takeaway:** London Stock Exchange Group is scaling OpenAI-based AI across its global business — empowering ~4,000 employees, accelerating insights, and shrinking release cycles.

**Main methods:**
- **Workforce reach.** Around 4,000 employees enabled with OpenAI tooling across LSEG's global operation.
- **Faster release cycles.** AI is folded into the build-and-ship loop, not just analyst chat — LSEG flags shorter release cycles as a measurable effect.
- **Insight acceleration.** Use cases center on getting from raw market and reference data to decisions faster.
- **Caveat.** Customer-story blog with no specific eval numbers, model versions, or before/after metrics.

**[LSEG 在规模化用可信 AI](https://openai.com/index/lseg)** — _OpenAI · 6月10日_

**Main takeaway:** 伦敦证券交易所集团（LSEG）在全球业务里规模化使用 OpenAI 模型——覆盖大约 4000 名员工、加速洞察生成、缩短发布周期。

**Main methods:**
- **覆盖人数。** OpenAI 工具铺到 LSEG 全球大约 4000 个员工。
- **发布周期变短。** AI 不只是给分析师聊天用，而是嵌进了 build 和 ship 的环路里——LSEG 把发布周期变短当作一个可衡量的效果。
- **从数据到决策更快。** 用例集中在把原始市场数据和参考数据更快变成决策。
- **需要注意的 caveat。** 这是 customer story，没有具体的 eval 数字、模型版本、前后对比。
