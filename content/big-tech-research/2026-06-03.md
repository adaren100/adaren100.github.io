### OpenAI

**[A shared playbook for trustworthy third party evaluations](https://openai.com/index/trustworthy-third-party-evaluations-foundations)** — _OpenAI · May 29_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI publishes a methodology guide for evaluating frontier AI models, arguing that modern models must be tested as tool-using, multi-step agents — not as chatbots — because performance depends heavily on the "harness" (execution environment and workflow setup) surrounding the model.

**Main methods:**
- **Beyond prompt-and-answer evaluation.** Today's frontier models use tools, maintain state across steps, and act within larger workflows; evaluation setups that prompt the model as a user miss critical capability signals.
- **Harness design as a first-class concern.** The harness — the scaffolding managing tool calls, context, and orchestration — can significantly alter benchmark scores, so OpenAI recommends evaluators publish full harness specs alongside results.
- **Capability vs. safety mitigations.** The guide distinguishes evaluating raw capabilities (what can the model do?) from evaluating mitigations (does the safeguard actually prevent misuse?), recommending different methodologies for each.
- **Ecological validity.** Tasks should reflect realistic attacker or user conditions, not idealized lab setups; the guide flags common validity-undermining mistakes like giving too many hints or using atypically clean environments.
- **Toward shared norms.** OpenAI frames the playbook as a contribution toward shared standards for third-party auditors — intended as input to the broader eval community, not an internal policy.

**[前沿模型第三方评估手册](https://openai.com/index/trustworthy-third-party-evaluations-foundations)** — _OpenAI · 5月29日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 发了一篇方法论指南，专门讲怎么做前沿 AI 模型的第三方评估。核心论点：现在的模型是工具调用、多步推理的 agent，不是聊天机器人——用老方法评估会漏掉关键信号，因为模型表现严重依赖它运行的 "harness"（执行环境和工作流框架）。

**Main methods:**
- **超越"问答式"评估。** 今天的前沿模型会用工具、跨多步维护状态、嵌入更大的工作流——用"给它发消息看回答"这种方式会系统性地误估它的真实能力。
- **Harness 设计是一等公民。** Harness（管理工具调用、上下文、编排的脚手架）会显著影响 benchmark 分数，评估者应该把完整的 harness 规格跟结果一起公开发布。
- **能力评估 vs. 缓解措施评估。** 指南区分了两类：模型能做什么（能力上限）vs. 安全缓解措施是否真的挡住了滥用——两类的方法论不同，不能混用。
- **生态效度。** 任务设计要贴近真实的攻击者/用户条件，而不是理想化的实验室设置；指南列了常见的效度失效模式，比如给了太多提示、用了过于干净的环境。
- **迈向行业标准。** OpenAI 把这份 playbook 定位为对更广泛评估社区的贡献，推动第三方审计员之间形成共同规范。

**[Strengthening societal resilience with Rosalind Biodefense](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-societal-resilience-with-rosalind-biodefense)** — _OpenAI · May 29_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI launches Rosalind Biodefense, a restricted-access developer platform for building biodefense and pandemic preparedness tools, while expanding access to its GPT-Rosalind model for vetted U.S. government and allied-nation partners.

**Main methods:**
- **Trusted-access architecture.** Rosalind Biodefense is available only to developers who apply and are vetted — OpenAI frames this as "defensive acceleration" that puts frontier bio-AI capabilities exclusively in defenders' hands.
- **Two-track rollout.** Track 1: a developer platform for building new biodefense applications. Track 2: expanded GPT-Rosalind access for US government and allied partners focused on public health and biodefense missions.
- **Defenders-first rationale.** OpenAI argues that as AI accelerates biological discovery, the same capabilities that advance science could lower barriers to misuse; responsible deployment structures are the mechanism to advantage defenders over bad actors.
- **No new capability claims.** The announcement does not disclose technical specs for GPT-Rosalind or benchmark it against other models — it is primarily a deployment and access-policy announcement.

**[Rosalind Biodefense：加强社会韧性](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-societal-resilience-with-rosalind-biodefense)** — _OpenAI · 5月29日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 推出 Rosalind Biodefense——一个只对审核通过的开发者开放的平台，用于构建生物防御和疫情防范工具；同时扩大 GPT-Rosalind 模型对美国政府及盟友相关机构的访问权限。

**Main methods:**
- **受信任访问架构。** Rosalind Biodefense 只开放给申请并通过审核的开发者——OpenAI 把这种模式称为"防御性加速"，核心是把前沿生物 AI 能力只交到防御方手里。
- **双轨推进。** 第一轨：面向开发者的平台，用于构建新的生物防御应用。第二轨：为专注公共卫生和生物防御任务的美国政府及盟友机构扩大 GPT-Rosalind 的访问权限。
- **为什么要严格把关。** OpenAI 认为 AI 加速生物科学发现的同时也可能降低滥用门槛；负责任的部署结构是让防御方而非恶意行为者占优势的关键机制。
- **没有新能力披露。** 这篇公告没有公布 GPT-Rosalind 的技术规格或 benchmark 数据——主要是一个部署与访问政策层面的通告。

**[Building self-improving tax agents with Codex](https://openai.com/index/building-self-improving-tax-agents-with-codex)** — _OpenAI · May 27_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI and Thrive Holdings describe a six-month co-development effort to build a self-improving tax agent for Crete accountants, where Codex-driven eval infrastructure automates the feedback loop from production failures to prompt and code improvements — replacing the manual engineer-in-the-loop update cycle.

**Main methods:**
- **Self-improvement via eval infrastructure.** The core insight: with good eval harnesses and access to real practitioner environments, you can build agents that detect their own production failures and improve without manual engineer intervention each cycle.
- **Practitioner-in-the-loop data generation.** Tax domain expertise was embedded by pairing engineers with Crete's accountants to generate realistic edge cases and ground-truth annotations that pure synthetic data misses.
- **Codex as meta-level optimizer.** Codex was used not just as the agent itself but as the optimizer — rewriting prompts and code based on eval failures, closing the loop between production behavior and model instructions.
- **Forward deployment model.** OpenAI engineers embedded directly with the customer team for six months, enabling rapid iteration that a remote API relationship wouldn't support.

**[用 Codex 构建自我改进的税务 agent](https://openai.com/index/building-self-improving-tax-agents-with-codex)** — _OpenAI · 5月27日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 和 Thrive Holdings 花六个月为 Crete 税务会计师联合开发了一个自我改进税务 agent，核心方法是用 Codex 驱动的 eval 基础设施，把"生产环境报错→改进提示词和代码"这个反馈循环自动化，替代了之前靠工程师手动推进的瓶颈。

**Main methods:**
- **靠 eval 基础设施实现自我改进。** 关键洞察：有了完善的 eval harness 和真实从业者环境的访问权限，可以构建能自动检测生产故障并自我改进的 agent，不需要工程师手动介入每次迭代。
- **从业者参与生成数据。** 把税务领域专业知识嵌进来的方式：让工程师和 Crete 的会计师配对工作，生成纯合成数据覆盖不到的真实边缘案例和 ground-truth 标注。
- **Codex 同时是 agent 本身和元层面优化器。** Codex 不只是跑任务的 agent，还被用来根据 eval 失败重写提示词和代码——把生产行为和模型指令之间的反馈环闭合。
- **前置部署模式。** OpenAI 工程师直接嵌入客户团队工作了六个月，这种深度配合支撑了快速迭代，远程 API 关系做不到。

### Anthropic

**[Introducing Claude Opus 4.8](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-8)** — _Anthropic · May 27_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic upgrades Claude Opus to version 4.8 — same price as 4.7, stronger across coding, agentic tasks, reasoning, and knowledge work benchmarks, with three new platform features: user-controlled effort levels, Claude Code dynamic workflows for large-scale problems, and a 3× price cut on fast mode (2.5× speed).

**Main methods:**
- **Across-the-board benchmark gains.** Opus 4.8 beats 4.7 on coding, agentic, reasoning, and professional knowledge work evals; full comparison tables and a System Card published alongside.
- **Better agentic judgment.** Early testers report Opus 4.8 is more reliable for long-running tasks — asks clarifying questions at the right moments, catches its own mistakes mid-task, pushes back on unsound plans, and builds confidence before making large-scale changes.
- **User effort control.** claude.ai users can now dial how much effort Claude puts into a response, trading speed for depth on demand.
- **Dynamic workflows in Claude Code.** New feature lets Claude Code tackle very large-scale problems by dynamically composing sub-tasks and workflows rather than single-shot code generation.
- **Fast mode price cut.** Fast mode (2.5× speed) is now 3× cheaper than for Opus 4.7, lowering the cost of latency-sensitive agentic deployments.

**[Claude Opus 4.8 发布](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-8)** — _Anthropic · 5月27日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 把 Claude Opus 升级到 4.8 版本——价格跟 4.7 一样，但在 coding、agentic 任务、推理和知识工作的 benchmark 上全面提升；同时推出三个平台新功能：用户可控的努力程度、Claude Code 的 dynamic workflows（处理超大规模问题）、以及快速模式 3 倍降价（2.5× 速度）。

**Main methods:**
- **全线 benchmark 提升。** Opus 4.8 在 coding、agentic、推理、专业知识工作的评测上都超过了 Opus 4.7；完整对比表和 System Card 一并发布。
- **更好的 agentic 判断力。** 早期测试者反馈 Opus 4.8 在长时间运行任务中更可靠——会在合适时机提问、在中途自己发现错误、在计划不合理时推回去、在做大范围改动前先建立信心。
- **用户可控努力程度。** claude.ai 用户现在可以调节 Claude 在一次回答上投入多少精力——按需在速度和深度之间取舍。
- **Claude Code dynamic workflows。** 新功能让 Claude Code 能处理超大规模问题，方式是动态组合子任务和工作流，而不只是单次代码生成。
- **快速模式大幅降价。** 快速模式（2.5× 速度）现在比 Opus 4.7 时代便宜 3 倍，降低了对延迟敏感的 agentic 部署的成本。

**[Expanding Project Glasswing](https://www.anthropic.com/news/expanding-project-glasswing)** — _Anthropic · Jun 1_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic expands Project Glasswing from ~50 initial partners to ~150 new organizations across 15+ countries, adding critical infrastructure sectors (power, water, healthcare, communications, hardware) that were absent from the first cohort — all sharing the criterion that a major cyberattack on their codebase could affect more than 100 million people.

**Main methods:**
- **Scale-up rationale.** With initial partners finding 10,000+ high/critical vulnerabilities and Mythos-class models expected at competing labs within 6–12 months, Anthropic is accelerating defender readiness before the capability becomes widely available.
- **New sector coverage.** First cohort skewed toward software companies; the expansion targets infrastructure operators in power, water, healthcare, communications, and hardware — entities whose compromise has wide downstream impact.
- **Vendor-multiplier strategy.** Many new partners maintain codebases used by other organizations including governments — securing one vendor protects many downstream dependents.
- **Shifting from finding to fixing.** Anthropic signals a phase change: early focus was detecting vulnerabilities; next phase is disclosure, patching, and deployment — the harder part of the security workflow once AI removes the detection bottleneck.

**[Project Glasswing 扩大规模](https://www.anthropic.com/news/expanding-project-glasswing)** — _Anthropic · 6月1日_

**Main takeaway:** Anthropic 把 Project Glasswing 从最初约 50 家合作方扩展到约 150 家新组织，覆盖 15 个以上国家，新增了第一批没覆盖到的关键基础设施行业（电力、水务、医疗、通信、硬件）——所有合作方的共同标准是：对其代码库的重大攻击可能影响超过 1 亿人。

**Main methods:**
- **扩大规模的理由。** 初始合作方已找到 1 万多个高/严重漏洞，同时 Mythos 级别的模型预计在 6–12 个月内就会出现在竞争对手那里——Anthropic 在此之前加速让防御方做好准备。
- **新增行业覆盖。** 第一批合作方偏向软件公司；新一批重点是电力、水务、医疗、通信、硬件领域的基础设施运营商——这些实体一旦被攻破，下游影响面极广。
- **供应商乘数策略。** 很多新合作方维护着被其他组织（包括政府）依赖的代码库——保护一个供应商就等于保护了大量下游依赖方。
- **从发现漏洞转向修复漏洞。** Anthropic 表示项目重点正在转变：初期聚焦于检测漏洞；下一阶段是披露、修复和部署补丁——当 AI 解决了检测瓶颈之后，安全工作流里更难的那部分。
