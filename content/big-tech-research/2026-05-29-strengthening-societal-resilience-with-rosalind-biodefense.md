**[Strengthening societal resilience with Rosalind Biodefense](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-societal-resilience-with-rosalind-biodefense)** — _OpenAI · May 29_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI launches Rosalind Biodefense, a restricted-access developer platform for building biodefense and pandemic preparedness tools, while expanding access to its GPT-Rosalind model for vetted U.S. government and allied-nation partners.

**Main methods:**
- **Trusted-access architecture.** Rosalind Biodefense is available only to developers who apply and are vetted — OpenAI frames this as "defensive acceleration" that puts frontier bio-AI capabilities exclusively in defenders' hands.
- **Two-track rollout.** Track 1: a developer platform for building new biodefense applications. Track 2: expanded GPT-Rosalind access for US government and allied partners focused on public health and biodefense missions.
- **Defenders-first rationale.** OpenAI argues that as AI accelerates biological discovery, the same capabilities that advance science could lower barriers to misuse; responsible deployment structures are the mechanism to advantage defenders over bad actors.
- **No new capability claims.** The announcement does not disclose technical specs for GPT-Rosalind or benchmark it against other models — it is primarily a deployment and access-policy announcement.

**[Rosalind Biodefense：加强社会韧性](https://openai.com/index/strengthening-societal-resilience-with-rosalind-biodefense)** — _OpenAI · 5月29日_

**Main takeaway:** OpenAI 推出 Rosalind Biodefense——一个只对审核通过的开发者开放的平台，用于构建生物防御和疫情防范工具；同时扩大 GPT-Rosalind 模型对美国政府及盟友相关机构的访问权限。

**Main methods:**
- **受信任访问架构。** Rosalind Biodefense 只开放给申请并通过审核的开发者——OpenAI 把这种模式称为"防御性加速"，核心是把前沿生物 AI 能力只交到防御方手里。
- **双轨推进。** 第一轨：面向开发者的平台，用于构建新的生物防御应用。第二轨：为专注公共卫生和生物防御任务的美国政府及盟友机构扩大 GPT-Rosalind 的访问权限。
- **为什么要严格把关。** OpenAI 认为 AI 加速生物科学发现的同时也可能降低滥用门槛；负责任的部署结构是让防御方而非恶意行为者占优势的关键机制。
- **没有新能力披露。** 这篇公告没有公布 GPT-Rosalind 的技术规格或 benchmark 数据——主要是一个部署与访问政策层面的通告。
