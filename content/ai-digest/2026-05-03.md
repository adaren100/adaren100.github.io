### Latent Space's Swyx

Swyx put out a request for a Chrome extension that augments every image input box on the web, letting you either generate a simple text-based image (no AI), draw something with tldraw, or use words/sketches to produce an image of the required proportions, and pinged Devin to build it. He also riffed that Codex is now a better Slack AI search than Slack's own AI search, and noted how that comparison "really puts the efficiency into focus." (https://x.com/swyx/status/2050460622706626740, https://x.com/swyx/status/2050432398161264664, https://x.com/swyx/status/2050396374282408358)

Swyx 发了一条产品需求：希望有个 Chrome 扩展能改造网页上所有的图片输入框，要么用纯文字生成一张简单图（不走 AI），要么用 tldraw 直接画，要么用文字加草图生成符合尺寸要求的图，并 @ 了 Devin 让它来做。他还顺手吐槽，Codex 已经比 Slack 自家的 AI 搜索更好用了，这种对比"真的把效率差距摆在了眼前"。

### Roblox Product Lead Peter Yang

Peter Yang shared a satisfying use case: turning Codex or Claude Code loose on his local files and Google Drive to "marie kondo" them. He gives the agents full computer access plus the gws (Google Workspace CLI), then prompts things like "Tell me what apps load on bootup and give me a plan to clean this up" or "Look at my downloads folder and give me a plan to organize it" — always asking for a plan first, since these are semi-dangerous operations. His verdict: files and Drive now spark joy. (https://x.com/petergyang/status/2050623358488997917)

Roblox 产品负责人 Peter Yang 分享了一个非常解压的玩法：让 Codex 或 Claude Code 给本地文件和 Google Drive 来一次"近藤麻理惠"式整理。他给 agent 开了完整的电脑权限和 gws（Google Workspace CLI），然后丢出类似"告诉我开机启动了哪些 app，给我一个清理方案"或"看看我的 Downloads 文件夹，给个整理计划"这样的 prompt。他强调一定要先让 agent 给出计划再动手，因为这类操作风险不小。最终结果是，文件和 Drive 都"令人怦然心动"。

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Aaron Levie made the most analytical case of the day for why AI creates jobs rather than destroying them. His thought experiment: a life sciences company that 10 years ago couldn't compete with tech for engineers and had to pare down its automation goals. Today, that same company has access to the *exact same* output tokens as the best tech companies — its engineers are 2x to 5x more capable. The rational response isn't to lay people off, it's to hire more, because each engineer can now finally tackle the long backlog of unmet needs. Extrapolate that across every bank, manufacturer, retailer, SMB, and across marketing/legal/finance/design, and you get a story of expansion, not contraction. In a separate post, he argued there's a finite limit to automation: accountability. Agents can compress job boundaries, but each role exists because it owns specialized context — a PM talking to customers, an engineer thinking about system design and security — and merging that context too aggressively eventually produces worse output from both sides. Specialization wasn't an accident, and agents will be force multipliers for it as much as they erase boundaries elsewhere. (https://x.com/levie/status/2050684160151617603, https://x.com/levie/status/2050645685104746686)

Box CEO Aaron Levie 今天给出了关于"AI 是否抢工作"最有分析力度的一段论证。他的思想实验是这样的：十年前一家生命科学公司想搞实验室自动化和大规模数据处理，但抢不过科技公司挖工程师，只能把目标砍小。如今同一家公司能用到和顶级科技公司*一模一样的*输出 token，自家工程师战力翻了 2 到 5 倍。理性反应不是裁员，而是招更多人，因为每个工程师现在终于有能力去啃那堆长期积压的需求。把这个逻辑外推到每一家银行、制造商、零售商、SMB，再扩展到市场、法务、财务、设计等岗位，你看到的是扩张而不是收缩。他在另一条推文里提出了自动化的一个真实边界：问责。agent 确实能压缩岗位边界，但每个岗位之所以存在，是因为它承载了一套专门的上下文——PM 要和客户聊、研究市场、想设计；工程师要做系统设计、关注安全和线上问题。把这些上下文过度合并，最终两头都会变差。岗位分工不是凑出来的，agent 在抹平某些边界的同时，也会成为另一些专业领域的放大器。

### OpenClaw's Peter Steinberger

Peter Steinberger shipped Crabbox 0.3.0 with a meaningful feature pack: remote Linux runs for dirty worktrees, GitHub browser login, Blacksmith Testbox wrap, `crabbox attach` for live run replay, durable run events, AWS image creation, and Cloudflare Access — install via `brew upgrade openclaw/tap/crabbox`. He also pushed an OpenClaw update that fixes the dependency bloat and slowness some users hit when installing via npm; almost everything moved into extensions, making the core package much leaner. (https://x.com/steipete/status/2050490163810230579, https://x.com/steipete/status/2050735979477008412)

OpenClaw 的 Peter Steinberger 发布了 Crabbox 0.3.0，更新内容相当扎实：dirty worktree 的远程 Linux 运行、GitHub 浏览器登录、Blacksmith Testbox 封装、可以实时回放运行的 `crabbox attach`、持久化运行事件、AWS 镜像创建以及 Cloudflare Access，安装命令是 `brew upgrade openclaw/tap/crabbox`。他同时推送了一版 OpenClaw 更新，解决了部分用户通过 npm 安装时遇到的依赖膨胀和速度慢问题，几乎所有功能都被搬到了 extensions 里，主包瘦身明显。

### Every CEO Dan Shipper

Dan Shipper laid out a confident UX prediction: "agent running continuously on the left, application that you + the agent use on the right" is how we'll be doing most of our work for the next 10 years. He also pointed people to Proof, a Codex-native writing app from Every, as a concrete example of the pattern. (https://x.com/danshipper/status/2050583747041640608, https://x.com/danshipper/status/2050608311888941301)

Every CEO Dan Shipper 给出了一个相当笃定的 UX 预测：未来 10 年我们大部分工作的形态就是"左边一个持续运行的 agent，右边是你和 agent 共同使用的应用"。他同时安利了 Every 出品的 Codex-native 写作应用 Proof，作为这种范式的一个具体例子。

He also said GPT 5.5 xhigh in fast mode is "really good" and that he thinks he got "psyoped by twitter on medium for a bit." (https://x.com/sama/status/2050671161915371998, https://x.com/sama/status/2050658558174437701)

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 给出了一个反直觉的自我修正：他总以为自己更想要更便宜、更快的模型，而不是更聪明的模型，但最终发现"更聪明"依然是最重要的事。他还说 GPT 5.5 xhigh 在 fast 模式下"真的很好用"，并坦言自己"被 Twitter 上对 medium 的吹捧 PUA 了一阵"。
