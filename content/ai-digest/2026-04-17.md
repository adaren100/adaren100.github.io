### Anthropic (claudeai on X)

Claude Opus 4.7 launched today across claude.ai, the Claude Platform, and all major cloud platforms. The new model brings a `/ultrareview` command to Claude Code that runs a dedicated review session flagging what a careful reviewer would catch. On the API, a new `xhigh` effort level — sitting between `high` and `max` — gives developers finer control over reasoning depth and latency on hard problems. Task budgets (beta) help Claude prioritize work and manage costs across longer runs.
[https://x.com/claudeai/status/2044785272617939282](https://x.com/claudeai/status/2044785272617939282) | [https://x.com/claudeai/status/2044785266590622185](https://x.com/claudeai/status/2044785266590622185) | [https://x.com/claudeai/status/2044785264313221470](https://x.com/claudeai/status/2044785264313221470)

Anthropic 今日正式发布 Claude Opus 4.7，可在 claude.ai、Claude Platform 及各大云平台使用。新模型为 Claude Code 带来 `/ultrareview` 指令，能像资深 reviewer 一样全面审查代码改动。API 新增 `xhigh` effort 级别（介于 `high` 与 `max` 之间），在复杂问题上提供更精细的推理与延迟控制。Task budgets（beta）功能则帮助 Claude 在长任务中优先排序并管理成本。

### Guillermo Rauch — Vercel CEO (rauchg on X)

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch championed the launch of Workflow SDK, calling it a solution to the hardest problem in agent backends: durability. LLMs go down, services rate-limit, databases slow unexpectedly — Workflow SDK handles all of this without requiring the complexity of SQS/Kafka/microservices architectures, and comes with a strong self-hosting and multi-cloud story from day one. He drew a direct comparison to what Next.js did for the frontend. Separately, he highlighted the Vercel AI Gateway as the right tool for capturing the upside of AI's relentless pace — letting teams swap between Anthropic, xAI, OpenAI, and Google without vendor lock-in.
[https://x.com/rauchg/status/2044858872842826102](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2044858872842826102) | [https://x.com/rauchg/status/2044808819273052663](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2044808819273052663)

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 力推 Workflow SDK 的发布，称其解决了 agent 后端最难的问题：持久性。LLM 会宕机，服务会限速，数据库会意外变慢——Workflow SDK 全部处理，无需 SQS/Kafka/微服务架构的复杂性，且从第一天起就支持强大的自托管和多云方案。他将其类比为 Next.js 对前端的意义。他还单独推荐了 Vercel AI Gateway，认为这是在 AI 快速迭代中捕获红利的正确工具，让团队可以在 Anthropic、xAI、OpenAI 和 Google 之间自由切换而不被厂商绑定。

### Aaron Levie — Box CEO (levie on X)

Box CEO Aaron Levie made a nuanced case for why AI will net-create jobs across many industries: automating one bottleneck simply exposes the next one that still requires humans. More legal AI queries leads to more lawyers; more code leads to more security researchers; automated patient referrals hit a bottleneck of not enough doctors. His take: the "AI replaces all work" narrative ignores that the world is dynamic and competitive — rivals using AI to do more will force you to match them or fall behind. On the product front, he highlighted the new Box plugin inside Codex as enabling enterprise content automation at scale.
[https://x.com/levie/status/2044621545348481285](https://x.com/levie/status/2044621545348481285) | [https://x.com/levie/status/2044855448722100720](https://x.com/levie/status/2044855448722100720)

Box CEO Aaron Levie 对"AI 会净增就业"提出了细致的论证：自动化一个瓶颈，只会暴露下一个仍需人类的瓶颈。更多法律 AI 咨询意味着更多律师被呼唤；更多代码意味着更多安全研究员；自动化的患者转诊遭遇的是医生不足的瓶颈。他的观点："AI 取代一切工作"的叙事忽视了世界是动态且竞争性的——对手用 AI 做得更多，你就必须跟上，否则落后。产品层面，他重点介绍了 Codex 内置的 Box 插件，可实现企业内容的规模化自动化。
