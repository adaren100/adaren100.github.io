### OpenAI, AI research company

OpenAI introduced GPT-Red, an automated red-teaming model aimed at prompt injection vulnerabilities. GPT-Red learns through adversarial self-play, trying to prompt-inject a variety of challenging defender models; every successful attack it finds is used to improve those defenders, which in turn pushes GPT-Red to discover broader and more complex failures. OpenAI frames it as a response to a scaling problem: red-teaming is essential as model capabilities grow, but today's manual approaches are a critical bottleneck. [how it works](https://x.com/OpenAI/status/2077446721161093124) · [why it matters](https://x.com/OpenAI/status/2077446719990796505)

OpenAI发布了GPT-Red,一个专门针对prompt injection漏洞的自动化红队模型。GPT-Red通过对抗性自我博弈来学习,目标是对多种高难度的防御模型实施prompt injection攻击;它每找到一次成功的攻击,都会被用来强化这些防御模型,反过来又逼着GPT-Red去发现更广泛、更复杂的失效模式。OpenAI将其定位为对规模化难题的回应:随着模型能力增长,红队测试必不可少,但如今的人工方式已经成为关键瓶颈。
