### Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch

The biggest builder drop of the day: Guillermo Rauch open-sourced `deepsec` (`npx deepsec`), an agent orchestrator for deep security reviews. Built for Vercel's internal use, then road-tested against major OSS projects before release. The pitch: coding agents can now find critical vulnerabilities in minutes that would take human teams months — if they can spot them at all. Because `deepsec` is optimized for Vercel Sandbox, you can effectively run thousands of agents scrutinizing a codebase in parallel. Rauch is also offering to sponsor runs for OSS maintainers via DM. (https://x.com/rauchg/status/2051386798899888539)

今天最重磅的 builder 发布来自 Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch：他开源了 `deepsec`（`npx deepsec`），一个用来做深度安全审计的 agent 编排器。这个工具最早是 Vercel 内部用的，后来拿去跑了几个大的开源项目，跑出底气之后才决定开源。卖点很直接：coding agent 现在可以在几分钟内找出一些原本人类团队要花几个月、甚至根本发现不了的关键漏洞。由于 `deepsec` 针对 Vercel Sandbox 做了优化，你可以让上千个 agent 并行去审你的代码库。Rauch 还说如果是 OSS 项目，欢迎私信他来 sponsor 一次跑动。(https://x.com/rauchg/status/2051386798899888539)

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Aaron Levie's read on the day: both Anthropic and OpenAI just launched new initiatives to help enterprises deploy AI agents inside their organizations, and this trend is "early but going to get very big fast." His core point is that there's no shortcut from raw model capability to a stable enterprise process — and the long tail of work that follows (upgrading IT systems, getting agents the context they need, modernizing workflows for human-agent collaboration, driving adoption, change management) is creating a massive market for new jobs, new firms, and now first-party programs from the labs themselves. (https://x.com/levie/status/2051344780328858040)

Box CEO Aaron Levie 今天对一件事的判断很明确：Anthropic 和 OpenAI 都刚刚推出了帮助企业在组织内部署 AI agent 的新计划，而这个趋势"刚刚开始，但会非常快地变得很大"。他的核心观点是：模型再强，也没有捷径能让这种能力直接、稳定地嵌入到企业的业务流程里——后面那一长串需要做的工作（升级 IT 系统、给 agent 喂正确的 context、把流程改造成适合人和 agent 协作、推动落地、做 change management）正在催生一个庞大的市场，孕育出大量新的岗位、新的公司，连大模型公司自己也在亲自下场做这些事。(https://x.com/levie/status/2051344780328858040)

### Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan

Garry Tan shipped GStack v1.26.3 with native GBrain integration — your coding agent now has substantially more context than it did before. In a separate post he sketched his vision of Man-Computer Symbiosis: "spend more time thinking and building and less time just getting into the position to do that," with everyone doing more to serve each other and less just to get through the work day. He frames that as "a blessing nobody is talking about re: AI." (https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051339045410885726, https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051327120383889906)

Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan 发布了 GStack v1.26.3，新版本和 GBrain 直接打通——意味着你的 coding agent 现在能拿到的 context 比之前多得多。另外一条推文里他描述了对 Man-Computer Symbiosis 的设想："多花时间去思考和动手做事，少花时间在为了开始做事而做的准备工作上"，每个人会有更多精力为别人提供价值，而不是只为了挨过工作日。他说这是"AI 带来的一种福利，但现在几乎没人在谈"。(https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051339045410885726, https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051327120383889906)

### FPV Ventures Partner Nikunj Kothari

Nikunj Kothari ran two contrarian takes on the day. First, the 2023-2025 startup vintage is "slowly realizing" that fancy launch videos and a pure distribution focus might land you VC funding but result in money being lit on fire if you didn't spend equal or longer on retention — "momentum (in lieu of anything else) has ceased to be a moat. It never was in the first place." He's expecting acquihires to happen at a much faster clip than we've seen before, and the seed-to-Series-A gap data is starting to confirm it. Second, he flagged Gemini Flash as "criminally cheap and good" — 1M context windows and structured outputs — and his most-used model in production workloads, with a side note that Google's new live voice model is "mindblowingly good." (https://x.com/nikunj/status/2051349526171287930, https://x.com/nikunj/status/2051321911741972900)

FPV Ventures 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 今天发了两条非主流观点。第一，他说 2023-2025 这一代创业公司"正在慢慢回过味儿来"——华丽的 launch video 加上只押 distribution 的玩法，确实容易拿到 VC 钱，但如果没在 retention 上花同样多甚至更多的时间，钱基本是直接烧掉的，"势能（在没有别的东西支撑时）已经不是护城河了，其实从来都不是。"他预期接下来会看到比以往明显更密集的 acquihire，而种子到 A 轮之间的转化率数据已经开始印证这件事。第二，他直言 Gemini Flash"便宜得离谱、好用得离谱"——支持 1M context window 和 structured output——是他在生产环境里用得最多的模型；顺便夸了一句 Google 新的 live voice model"好到让人震惊"。(https://x.com/nikunj/status/2051349526171287930, https://x.com/nikunj/status/2051321911741972900)

South Park Commons 合伙人 Aditya Agarwal 否定了把 SPC 类比成 accelerator 的说法："我们从来没想做一个 accelerator。在没有确定北极星的前提下，单纯追求速度并不让人觉得有意思。"(https://x.com/adityaag/status/2051330205902581842)
