### Peter Yang, AI Educator & Podcast Host

AI educator Peter Yang shared 5 use cases worth trying before Fable 5 leaves Claude subscriptions at midnight, each with a copy-paste prompt: have Fable find your top 5 "Fable-worthy" tasks, get life and business advice from your plan doc and connected tools, hunt down every real bug before shipping a project, plan your next big project so a cheaper model can execute it step by step, and refactor your whole AI skill system ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2074206798631071796)). He also posted a snippet from Codex PM Rohan: in large enterprises only the top 5-10% most "AI-pilled" engineers are maximally leveraging Codex, while a long tail is still pair programming and delegating less — "almost everything you need to do professionally should almost always just immediately have Codex start doing it" ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2074165284852941041)).

AI 教育者 Peter Yang 分享了在 Fable 5 于今晚午夜退出 Claude 订阅之前值得一试的 5 个用法，每个都附上了可以直接复制的 prompt：让 Fable 找出最值得交给它的 5 项任务、基于你的计划文档和已连接工具给出人生与商业建议、在项目上线前揪出所有真实 bug、把下一个大项目规划到便宜模型也能逐步执行的程度，以及重构你的整套 AI skill 系统（[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2074206798631071796)）。他还发了采访 Codex PM Rohan 的片段：在大型企业里，只有最"AI 上头"的前 5-10% 工程师在充分利用 Codex，剩下一大批人还停留在结对编程、很少做任务委派。Rohan 的原话是"你工作中几乎所有事情，几乎都应该立刻先让 Codex 去做"（[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2074165284852941041)）。

### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch offered his answer to "the ultimate test for coding AI": his own agency and autonomy have radically increased, personal software is real (he has a stack of web tools and macOS apps written just for himself), Vercel's CTO is bringing genuinely useful software to life with swaths of agents, and PMs, junior engineers, and interns are shipping quality software at scale. The ultimate jury, he says, is whether people love the products and expand their usage — and no, there has never been a proclamation to "tokenmaxxx" ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2074222247548735996)). He also highlighted that the eve agent framework ships with built-in evals — "eve evals itself with eve eval" — arguing that while web frameworks left testing to the ecosystem, evals are essential for agents and belong out of the box ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2074287795028512773)).

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 给出了他对"coding AI 终极检验标准"的回答：他本人的自主性和掌控力大幅提升，个人软件已经成为现实（他给自己写了一堆网页工具和 macOS 应用），Vercel 的 CTO 正带着成群的 agent 打造真正有用的软件，产品经理、初级工程师和实习生也都在规模化地交付高质量软件。他说，最终的裁判是用户是否热爱产品、是否持续加大使用，另外他澄清公司从来没有过什么"tokenmaxxx"的号令（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2074222247548735996)）。他还强调 eve agent 框架自带 eval 能力，"eve 用 eve eval 来评估自己"。他认为 Web 框架时代把测试留给了生态系统，比如 React 就没有内置测试方案，但对 agent 来说 eval 是刚需，理应开箱即用（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2074287795028512773)）。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie laid out how to think about open source AI and the applied AI layer: frontier intelligence will remain at the forefront of solving brand-new use cases and orchestrating complex workflows, while mature, predictable enterprise use cases can peel off tokens to cheaper open or task-tuned models. Doing that too early doesn't make sense because you don't yet know what you're optimizing for — and since this process can run forever, spend and token volume for both approaches will keep rising for years. The dynamic only works, he notes, because the applied AI layer can eval workflows in a specific domain, mix models, and eventually train its own ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2074163686990913576)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 阐述了该如何看待开源 AI 与应用层 AI 的关系：前沿智能会始终站在解决全新场景和编排复杂工作流的最前线，而当企业场景成熟、变得可预测之后，就可以把一部分 token 分流给更便宜的开源模型或者专门微调过的模型。但过早这么做没有意义，因为你还不知道自己在优化什么。由于这个过程可以永远持续下去，两条路线的支出和 token 用量在未来多年都会持续攀升。他指出，这种格局之所以成立，正是因为应用层 AI 能在特定领域对工作流做 eval、混合调用多个模型，并最终训练自己的模型（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2074163686990913576)）。

### Nikunj Kothari, FPV Ventures Partner

FPV Ventures partner Nikunj Kothari poked at the contradiction in venture capital: the job is investing in outliers, yet VCs tout "revolutionary sourcing algorithms" built on proprietary data signals. The job involves enormous serendipity and luck, he admits, though years of correct bets mean you aren't simply lucky — returns are the only alpha, but they take time, so VCs resort to random metrics to differentiate themselves. His own unscientific approach: massively expand his surface area of luck and keep a prepared mind in 3-4 areas that are 6-12 months early, meeting every entrepreneur there ([link](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2074141483356340475)).

FPV Ventures 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 调侃了风险投资行业的自相矛盾：这份工作的本质是投资异类，但 VC 们却总在宣传自己"革命性的项目挖掘算法"，号称基于专有数据信号打造。他坦言这一行充满了机缘和运气，不过如果你多年的下注都押对了，那就不只是运气。回报是唯一的 alpha，但回报需要时间验证，所以 VC 们只好拿各种花哨指标来证明自己与众不同。他自己的"不科学"打法是：大幅扩展运气的接触面，同时在 3-4 个领先市场 6-12 个月的方向上保持准备，见遍那里的每一位创业者（[链接](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2074141483356340475)）。

### Anthropic, Official Account

Anthropic published new interpretability research on the "J-space," which lets researchers read, audit, and shape what Claude is actively thinking about — tools for keeping models trustworthy as they grow more capable, with what the team calls surprising parallels between language models and our own minds ([link](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2074185387577094398)). They partnered with Neuronpedia on an interactive demo of the methods on open-weights models ([link](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2074185390060110138)), and invited experts in neuroscience, philosophy, and interpretability to share commentary on the work ([link](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2074185384792109380)).

Anthropic 发布了关于 "J-space" 的可解释性新研究，让研究者能够读取、审计并塑造 Claude 当下正在思考的内容。这是在模型能力不断增强的背景下保持其可信可控的工具，团队还表示这项研究揭示了语言模型与人类心智之间令人意外的相似之处（[链接](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2074185387577094398)）。他们与 Neuronpedia 合作，在开放权重模型上做了一个交互式方法演示（[链接](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2074185390060110138)），并邀请了神经科学、哲学和可解释性领域的专家对这项工作发表评论（[链接](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2074185384792109380)）。
