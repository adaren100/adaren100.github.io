### Roblox PM Peter Yang

Roblox PM and AI newsletter writer Peter Yang broke down Moritz Kremb's short-video production system, which saves him 10 hours a week: cron jobs scrape ideas from X and YouTube, AI drafts rough notes, Moritz iterates on the script with AI, then reads it into his phone (no AI voice), and Postiz CLI auto-posts to YouTube, Instagram and TikTok at once. His framing: the best AI builders set up systems to ship faster, not just tools. (https://x.com/petergyang/status/2053535647819047167)

Roblox PM、AI newsletter 作者 Peter Yang 拆解了 Moritz Kremb 的短视频生产流程，每周帮他省下 10 个小时：cron job 在 X 和 YouTube 上扒选题，AI 先写一版粗稿，Moritz 在 AI 的草稿上反复改，然后亲自对着手机把稿子读出来（语音不交给 AI），最后用 Postiz CLI 一键同步发到 YouTube、Instagram 和 TikTok。他的总结是：真正强的 AI builder 不只是堆工具，而是搭出一整套能让自己更快 ship 的系统。(https://x.com/petergyang/status/2053535647819047167)

### OpenClaw's Peter Steinberger

OpenClaw's Peter Steinberger had a heavy shipping day. He released CodexBar 0.25, adding new model providers (Manus, MiMo, Qwen, Doubao, Venice and more), quota warning notifications, stacked Codex account switching, and faster cost history. He also pushed gogcli 0.16.0, a Google Workspace admin CLI that's now grown up enough to handle user create/delete, aliases, temp passwords, org units, Meet, Sites, YouTube, GA4/Search Console, and Drive changes — "a lot more Google API from one boring binary." His most-shared post of the day, though, was a side-by-side of "slop one-shot websites, 2025 vs 2026" showing just how far AI-generated UI has come in 12 months. (https://x.com/steipete/status/2053617492325523737, https://x.com/steipete/status/2053505486184411342, https://x.com/steipete/status/2053456429420265662)

OpenClaw 的 Peter Steinberger 今天发版发得很猛。CodexBar 0.25 上线，新增了 Manus、MiMo、Qwen、Doubao、Venice 等一票 provider，外加 quota 告警、多个 Codex 账号叠层切换，以及更快的费用历史查询。他同时还把 gogcli 0.16.0 推了出去——这是一款 Google Workspace 管理用的 CLI，现在功能已经长大成熟，能搞定用户增删、alias、临时密码、组织单元、Meet、Sites、YouTube、GA4/Search Console，还有 Drive 的变更和活动记录，他自己的形容是"一个不起眼的二进制文件，把更多 Google API 都吃下来了"。不过他今天传播最广的一条，反而是一张对比图："slop 风格的 one-shot 网站，2025 vs 2026"——一眼能看出来 AI 生成 UI 这 12 个月跨了多大一步。(https://x.com/steipete/status/2053617492325523737, https://x.com/steipete/status/2053505486184411342, https://x.com/steipete/status/2053456429420265662)
