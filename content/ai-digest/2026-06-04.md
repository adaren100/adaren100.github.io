### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie made two related points about the enterprise AI market. First, even with employer caps, spend on AI tokens dramatically exceeds historical software spend — companies that used to pay $10–50 per seat per month for software licenses are now spending hundreds or thousands per employee on tokens, which tells you how big the TAM for intelligence really is ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2062280745889222937)). Second, as token budgets balloon, model routing becomes inevitable and one of the biggest differentiators for the applied AI layer: most use cases still need frontier models today, but enterprises won't be able to route at scale themselves, so products that intelligently send each workflow to the right tier of model will aggregate the demand ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2061974298760495132)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 讲了两件相关的事。第一，即便公司给员工的 AI 用量设了上限，token 上的花费仍然远远超过任何历史上的软件支出——以前一个 license 一个月 10–50 美元就能买，现在每个员工一个月可能要花上百甚至上千美元在 token 上，由此可见智能这个市场的 TAM 有多大（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2062280745889222937)）。第二，随着 token 预算占比越来越高，model routing 是必然趋势，也是应用层 AI 最大的差异化之一：目前大多数场景还是需要 frontier 模型，但企业自己很难在规模上做好这件事，所以那些能根据任务自动把请求路由到合适层级模型的产品，未来会聚合大量需求（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2061974298760495132)）。

### Nikunj Kothari, FPV Ventures partner

### Dan Shipper, Every CEO

Every CEO Dan Shipper recorded an AI & I episode with Figma's director of product management for developers, Matt Colyer, to make the case for a SaaS resurgence and explain why chat is the wrong interface for design. They cover why running your own agents makes you more willing to pay for SaaS rather than less; Figma's MCP server, which lets you reconstruct a live web page on the Figma canvas or hand a design to an agent that opens a pull request; why design agents need personalization because every problem is a context problem (with Apple and Google as the reigning kings of context); and why review is the new bottleneck as teams scale evaluations ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2062202908306030915)).

Every CEO Dan Shipper 录了一期 AI & I，请来 Figma 负责开发者产品的 director Matt Colyer，论证 SaaS 正在复苏，并解释为什么 chat 不是做设计的好界面。他们聊了：为什么自己跑 agent 反而让人更愿意付费给 SaaS；Figma 的 MCP server——你可以把一个在线网页在 Figma 画布上重建出来，或者把设计交给 agent 让它直接开 PR；为什么 design agent 需要个性化，因为每个问题本质都是 context 问题（Apple 和 Google 才是 context 的王）；以及随着团队做评估的规模变大，review 成为新的瓶颈（[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2062202908306030915)）。
