AI Builders Digest — July 12, 2026

### AI Engineer & Latent Space Host Swyx

If you understood Jevons paradox only in the narrow context of software demand in the age of agentic engineering, you may have missed its bigger implication, says Swyx. As coding agents get better and human "AI engineers" learn to wield them, and as those agents break out of coding into other knowledge work, the efficiency gain doesn't shrink total demand, it grows it, because the cost per unit of knowledge work falls while demand for more and better output rises. What happened to software isn't the exception, he argues; it's the preview of what's coming to every other knowledge field. ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2076155833428431012))

如果你只是在"敏捷工程时代的软件需求"这个狭窄语境下理解杰文斯悖论,可能就错过了它更大的含义,Swyx 这样说。随着coding agent越来越强,懂得驾驭它们的"AI工程师"越来越多,而这些agent又不断突破边界,进入编程以外的知识工作领域,效率的提升并不会缩小总需求,反而会放大它,因为知识工作的单位成本下降了,人们对更多、更好产出的需求反而上升了。他认为,发生在软件行业的这一切并不是特例,而是其他所有知识领域即将上演的预告片。([原文](https://x.com/swyx/status/2076155833428431012))

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Aaron Levie points to software job postings outpacing other fields as evidence that lowering the cost of production increases total demand rather than eliminating jobs, the same dynamic seen repeatedly in industrial history, now showing up in knowledge work. Software isn't fully automated, he argues, so people are still needed to decide what to build, maintain the code, and run it long-term; "agents will cause more abundance than replacement." Separately, he highlighted a post on applied enterprise AI, emphasizing that real ROI comes from agents redesigning underlying workflows and business processes, not just being rolled out as tools individual employees choose to adopt, which requires deep domain expertise, forward-deployed engineering support, and comprehensive evals to get right. ([link 1](https://x.com/levie/status/2076116544980214164), [link 2](https://x.com/levie/status/2075963779599466894))

Aaron Levie指出,软件类岗位招聘增速反而超过了其他领域,这恰恰证明了降低生产成本会扩大总需求,而不是消灭工作岗位,这一规律在工业史上反复出现,如今终于在知识工作领域显现。他认为,软件行业并没有被完全自动化,所以仍然需要人来决定做什么、维护代码、长期运营系统,"agent带来的将是更多的富足,而不是取代"。另外,他还转发了一篇关于企业级AI应用的文章,强调真正的ROI来自agent重塑底层工作流和业务流程,而不只是作为工具推给员工自行选择使用,这需要深厚的行业know-how、前线工程支持(FDE)以及完善的评估体系才能做好。([链接1](https://x.com/levie/status/2076116544980214164),[链接2](https://x.com/levie/status/2075963779599466894))

### YC President & CEO Garry Tan

In a riff on Y Combinator's classic "make something people want," Garry Tan posted simply: "Make something agents want," a nod to a growing builder thesis that AI agents themselves are becoming a customer segment worth designing for. ([link](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2075975184293765564))

Garry Tan化用了Y Combinator那句经典口号"做人们想要的东西",发了一条简单的推文:"做agent想要的东西"。这呼应了创业者圈子里正在兴起的一种观点:AI agent本身正在成为一个值得专门设计产品去服务的客户群体。([原文](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2075975184293765564))

Nikunj Kothari分享说,Fable和Sol让他"几个小时内就做出了一个完全能用的iOS应用",直接基于已有的后端和网页应用构建而成,他感叹"我们生活在一个疯狂的世界"。这是一个具体的例证,说明AI编程工具如今能有多快地把一个网页产品变成原生移动应用。([原文](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2076022807365079342))

### AI Developer Peter Steinberger

Peter Steinberger, creator of OpenClaw, posted a terse line capturing how AI agents are starting to work through problems: "Let me verify a couple of load-bearing facts before I give you the architecture assessment," a nod to agents that check their assumptions before committing to a technical judgment. ([link](https://x.com/steipete/status/2076013212043182375))

OpenClaw的创造者Peter Steinberger发了一句简短的话,生动展现了AI agent处理问题的方式:"让我先核实几个关键事实,再给你架构评估意见。"这体现出agent在做出技术判断前,会先检验自己的假设是否成立。([原文](https://x.com/steipete/status/2076013212043182375))

### South Park Commons General Partner Aditya Agarwal

Aditya Agarwal laid out his wishlist for where agentic infrastructure needs to go: run all agents in the cloud, freely choose any model (frontier, open-source, Chinese, American) and any harness, get full tracing, and have recursive improvement loops, and wondered aloud whether it can arrive even faster than the breakneck pace AI is already moving at. ([link](https://x.com/adityaag/status/2076047290083733539))

Aditya Agarwal列出了他对agent基础设施的愿望清单:所有agent都能在云端运行、可以自由选择任意模型(前沿模型、开源模型、中国模型、美国模型都行)和任意harness、拥有完整的tracing能力、还要有递归式的自我改进循环。他还感叹,AI发展本已快得惊人,这一切能不能来得再快一点。([原文](https://x.com/adityaag/status/2076047290083733539))

### OpenAI CEO Sam Altman

Sam Altman said that, contrary to his own (relatively optimistic) expectations, AI so far appears to be net job-creating rather than job-destroying, and floated that this trend could continue. He also pointed to a study finding that physicians identified fewer flaws in GPT-5.6's responses than in physician-written responses, and joked that GPT-5.6 "Sol" tops many benchmarks, though the most reliable signal, he quipped, is that a rival is "obsessed with me again." ([link 1](https://x.com/sama/status/2076036901824532530), [link 2](https://x.com/sama/status/2075985056846451123), [link 3](https://x.com/sama/status/2075983427019612242))

Sam Altman表示,和他自己(相对乐观)的预期相反,目前来看AI呈现出的是净创造就业而非消灭就业的效果,他还大胆猜测这个趋势也许会持续下去。他还提到一项研究发现,医生在评审时,认为GPT-5.6给出的回答中的错误反而比医生自己写的回答更少。他还开玩笑说GPT-5.6"Sol"在很多基准测试中都拿下高分,不过他调侃道,最可靠的信号其实是"马斯克又开始对我念念不忘了"。([链接1](https://x.com/sama/status/2076036901824532530),[链接2](https://x.com/sama/status/2075985056846451123),[链接3](https://x.com/sama/status/2075983427019612242))

### ML Research Engineer & Author Sebastian Raschka

Sebastian Raschka updated his cost-vs-performance comparison of coding-agent models to include Grok 4.5 and Meta's Muse Spark 1.1, finding Grok 4.5 sits at the Pareto frontier, strong capability for the price, and added harness details to the chart, sourced from the Artificial Analysis Coding Agent Index. He also issued a correction to an earlier version of the plot where labels had shifted (clarifying that "Ultra" maps to "Max," which reportedly runs Max with four subagents for speed), while noting the relative comparisons between models still hold. ([link 1](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2075982283509571666), [link 2](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2075961865683825141))

Sebastian Raschka更新了他关于coding agent模型性价比对比图,加入了Grok 4.5和Meta的Muse Spark 1.1,发现Grok 4.5正好处于帕累托前沿,也就是花的钱和获得的能力性价比很高,他还在图表中补充了harness信息,数据来源是Artificial Analysis Coding Agent Index。此外,他还对早前一版图表中的标签错位做了更正(说明"Ultra"实际对应的是"Max",据称Max是用4个subagent并行运行以提升速度),并强调各模型之间的相对比较结论依然成立。([链接1](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2075982283509571666),[链接2](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2075961865683825141))
