### Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch

Rauch posted a follow-up on the ongoing Vercel security incident with an important clarification: deleting an environment variable or resource on Vercel does not invalidate the associated key with the underlying provider. He urged all potentially affected customers to rotate credentials — get a new key from the vendor and invalidate the old one — rather than just deleting the variable on Vercel's side. ([tweet](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2046305710120829374))

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 就持续中的安全事件发表跟进声明，重点澄清一个常见误区：在 Vercel 侧删除环境变量或资源，并不等于在服务提供商那边使相关密钥失效。他强调，用户必须"轮换（rotate）"凭据——即从供应商处获取新密钥、使旧密钥失效——而不只是在 Vercel 端删除变量。([推文](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2046305710120829374))

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Levie argued in a widely-shared post that AI will make most roles more complex, not simpler. As AI handles routine work, the baseline expectation for every job rises: engineers will tackle harder problems, paralegals won't just review contracts, financial analysts won't just compile basic research. Expertise still differentiates — those who grow their skills will keep pulling ahead, while the prior definition of each role becomes obsolete. ([tweet](https://x.com/levie/status/2046067263326028108))

Box CEO Aaron Levie 发布了一条广泛传播的观点：AI 不会让大多数职业变得更简单，而是让它们的复杂度持续提升。随着 AI 承担日常任务，每个岗位的基准要求也随之提高：工程师要攻克更难的问题，律师助理不再只是审合同，金融分析师不再只是汇编基础研究。专业能力依然是核心竞争力——不断成长的人会持续拉开差距，而此前对每个岗位的定义将逐渐变得过时。([推文](https://x.com/levie/status/2046067263326028108))

### FirstMark Capital VC Matt Turck

Turck pushed back on the oft-repeated claim that AI's TAM equals the global human labor market dollar-for-dollar. Like every labor automation technology in history, he argues, AI services will ultimately be priced at marginal cost plus a normal margin — not at the full dollar value of the labor they replace. ([tweet](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2046284478151086178))

FirstMark Capital 风险投资人 Matt Turck 驳斥了一个被反复引用的观点：AI 的 TAM（总可寻址市场）等于全球人力劳动市场的规模。他认为，与历史上所有劳动自动化技术一样，AI 服务的定价最终会收敛到边际成本加正常利润——而非以其所替代劳动力的全额价值来定价。([推文](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2046284478151086178))
