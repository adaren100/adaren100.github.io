### Latent Space's Swyx, AI Engineer organizer

Latent Space's Swyx flagged Microsoft and NVIDIA putting Grace + Blackwell chips in a laptop as a serious shot at Apple Silicon's six-year dominance ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2061567877879369953)). He also pushed a Latent Space episode with an xAI Videogen researcher (Ethan), calling out several bold takes: he's quitting Videogen for LLMs because video models get most of their intelligence from LLMs rather than from scaling video data; the next frontier is "video agent models" that orchestrate video generation; deterministic compression like MP4 is the wrong target vs VAE compression; and if you believe in a "Moore's law" for genmedia, video models become the final UI of everything ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2061479719980425437)).

Latent Space 的 Swyx 注意到 Microsoft 和 NVIDIA 把 Grace + Blackwell 塞进笔记本，认为这是对 Apple Silicon 过去六年统治地位的一次正面挑战 ([链接](https://x.com/swyx/status/2061567877879369953))。他还推了一期和 xAI Videogen 研究员 Ethan 的播客，里面有几条挺猛的判断：他自己正在从 Videogen 转去做 LLM，因为视频模型的大部分智能其实来自 LLM 而不是堆视频数据；下一个 frontier 是"video agent models"，也就是用 agent 去编排视频模型；像 MP4 那种确定性压缩根本是错的目标，应该看 VAE 压缩；而如果你真信 genmedia 有"摩尔定律"，那视频模型最终会变成一切东西的终极 UI ([链接](https://x.com/swyx/status/2061479719980425437))。

### Roblox Product lead Peter Yang

Roblox Product lead Peter Yang shared six takeaways from solo builder Josh Pigford on shipping multiple products with AI agents: ship even though the fear of embarrassment never goes away; charge from day one and kill products that can't pay for themselves; build features in parallel using separate git worktrees (Pigford uses Conductor to manage them); have GPT review Claude's work and vice versa, because each model catches three to five bugs the other misses; build a `/learnings` skill that distills "that didn't work" moments into rules so the agent stops repeating mistakes; and remember that AI lets anyone ship, but 25 years of pre-AI building is what lets Pigford move this fast ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061452068792287622)).

Roblox 的产品负责人 Peter Yang 整理了独立开发者 Josh Pigford 关于"一个人用 AI agent 同时做多个产品"的六条要点：哪怕"丢脸恐惧"永远不会消失，也得继续发版；从第一天就开始收费，养不活自己的产品就直接砍掉并退款；用独立的 git worktree 并行开发不同 feature（Pigford 自己用 Conductor 管这些 worktree）；让 GPT 去 review Claude 写的代码，反之亦然——不同模型互相能挑出三到五个对方漏掉的 bug；做一个 `/learnings` skill，每个阶段结束时把"这条路走不通"的经验沉淀成规则，避免 agent 重复犯错；最后他强调 AI 让所有人都能发版，但 Pigford 自己之所以能跑这么快，靠的是 AI 之前那 25 年的积累 ([链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061452068792287622))。

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Box CEO Aaron Levie argues context is the number one problem for enterprise AI agents: as we move from agentic coding (where the code base carries most of the context) to knowledge-work agents, the gap gets much worse. Existing digital knowledge is fragmented across legacy systems with access controls that don't map to real workflows, and a lot of the critical decisions, processes, and tribal knowledge agents need has never even been digitized. He frames this as a massive point of leverage for applied AI companies and FDE/system-integrator plays — the firms that solve context will get the most out of AI going forward ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2061247380897579500)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 认为 context 才是企业 AI agent 的头号问题：从 agentic coding（代码库本身就携带大部分上下文）走到 knowledge work agent，这个鸿沟会被进一步放大。已有的数字化知识散落在各种 legacy 系统里，access control 和真实工作流也对不上；更麻烦的是，agent 真正需要的那些决策、流程、组织里的 tribal knowledge，很多压根就没被数字化过。他的判断是：这恰恰是 applied AI 公司和 FDE / 系统集成商最大的杠杆点——谁能解决 context 问题，谁就能从 AI 里拿到最多收益 ([链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2061247380897579500))。

### AI evangelist Andrew Ng

AI evangelist Andrew Ng weighed in on the buzz around the AI Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) role — engineers embedded inside customer organizations to customize agentic workflows, a model Palantir pioneered two decades ago and that OpenAI/Anthropic are now reviving. His contrarian take: yes, FDEs are real and growing (and yes, this is one more refutation of the "AI jobpocalypse"), but the number of AI Engineer jobs will dwarf FDE jobs. Most companies want their own employees on their projects, and they don't want a vendor's FDEs to tightly bind them to one stack — in a moment when nobody knows which AI vendor will win, optionality is hugely valuable. He expects the generalist AI Engineer role to eventually fragment into specializations like LLMOps, Evals, Harness, and AI Data engineering, much like Software Engineer fragmented decades ago ([link](https://x.com/AndrewYNg/status/2061477558693384395)).

AI 布道者 Andrew Ng 谈了一下最近很火的 AI Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) ——也就是被派驻进客户公司、专门帮客户定制 agentic 工作流的工程师，这个模式 20 年前 Palantir 就在做，现在 OpenAI 和 Anthropic 把它捡了回来。他的反共识判断是：FDE 确实在增长（这也再次说明所谓"AI 让所有人失业"的叙事是错的），但 AI Engineer 的岗位数量会远远大于 FDE。原因有两个：大多数公司其实更希望自己的员工来做自己家的项目；而且在没人知道哪家 AI 厂商最终会赢的当下，企业非常看重 optionality，不愿意让某家供应商的 FDE 把自己绑死在一套 stack 上。他预测，现在还以"通才"形式存在的 AI Engineer，未来会像几十年前的 Software Engineer 一样裂变出 LLMOps、Evals、Harness、AI Data 等更细分的角色 ([链接](https://x.com/AndrewYNg/status/2061477558693384395))。
