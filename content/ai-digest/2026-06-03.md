### Peter Yang, Roblox Product / AI newsletter

Roblox product lead and AI newsletter writer Peter Yang argued that simple, narrow SaaS is getting squeezed: AI skills can solve the same problems more flexibly, AI-native agents like Codex and Claude Code carry the user's personal context, and consumers anchor SaaS pricing to their Claude/ChatGPT subscription instead of paying for one more $20/month tool. Larger enterprise SaaS that do many jobs (he names Figma) will be fine ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061846283263103274)). He also gave the Devin/Windsurf team flowers for staying disciplined through the cycle ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061936952400814392)) and called Granola's pre-meeting briefs a model for more automated AI features ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061833015257612593)).

Roblox 产品负责人、AI newsletter 作者 Peter Yang 认为窄场景的简单 SaaS 正在被挤压：AI skills 能更灵活地解决同样的问题，Codex、Claude Code 这类 AI-native agent 自带用户的个人上下文，而消费者会拿 SaaS 定价跟自己已经付的 Claude/ChatGPT 订阅比，不愿再多花一份 20 美元。能干多件事的大型企业 SaaS（他举了 Figma）还是没问题（[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061846283263103274)）。他还夸了 Devin/Windsurf 团队在起伏里保持定力（[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061936952400814392)），并把 Granola 的会前简报当成"自动化 AI 功能"的范本（[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2061833015257612593)）。

### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch reframed the post-no-code era as "yes-code": no-code was built on the assumption that code is expensive and scarce, but coding agents made code cheap and abundant, so Vercel's mission is to be the easiest cloud for agents you never graduate from ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2061934154732974376)). He also argued language itself is the new API to the world — much like an SDK's function definitions — and AI finally lets non-engineers go direct from English to tangible artifacts without translating through machine instructions ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2061862134469062850)). He's powering Conductor's Sandbox and called Conductor an "ADE" — an IDE born for coding agents — predicting agents will take remote dev mainstream ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2061809689973944724)).

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 把后 no-code 时代重新框定成"yes-code"：no-code 的前提是代码贵又稀缺，但 coding agent 让代码变得便宜又充足，所以 Vercel 要做"agent 最顺手、永远不会毕业的云"（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2061934154732974376)）。他还提出，语言本身就是新的 world API，像 SDK 的函数签名一样——AI 终于让非工程师可以从英文直接走到成品，不必再经过机器指令的翻译（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2061862134469062850)）。他还宣布为 Conductor 的 Sandbox 提供动力，并称 Conductor 是为 coding agent 而生的"ADE"，认为 agent 将把 remote dev 推向主流（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2061809689973944724)）。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie posed the defining question of the agent era: when your competitor has access to the same models, where does competitive advantage come from? His answer: institutional knowledge, proprietary data assets, and domain-specific workflows wired into AI — captured in a way that protects the long-term value of unique data and expertise, and increasingly something he sees Box customers demanding ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2061662386680127688)). He also weighed in on the AWS–OpenAI partnership, noting AWS's enterprise contracts open distribution for OpenAI and likely lift token consumption across all model providers ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2061612625574944804)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 抛出了 agent 时代的核心问题：当对手用的是同样的模型，差异化从哪来？他的答案是机构知识、自有数据资产和领域工作流——把它们和 AI 接起来，并以能长期保护这部分独特价值的方式留存下来。他说在 Box 越来越多客户都在主动要求这件事（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2061662386680127688)）。他还评论了 AWS 和 OpenAI 的合作：AWS 的企业大单等于给 OpenAI 模型打开分发，同时大概率推高整个模型市场的 token 消费（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2061612625574944804)）。

### Dan Shipper, Every CEO

Every CEO Dan Shipper asked readers for their take on Opus 4.8 almost a week post-launch — internally they were extremely bullish in testing, but the response felt more tepid in the wild. His theory: Opus 4.8 pushes on your frame more, producing high-variance behavior that sometimes feels amazing and sometimes feels obviously wrong, and he wants community feedback to tune Every's evaluations ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2061817375519809665)). He also flagged something Every is internally freaking out about as worth paying attention to ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2061908190040645707)).

Every CEO Dan Shipper 在 Opus 4.8 发布近一周后向读者征询反馈：他们内部测试时非常看好，但外面用户的反应明显更冷淡。他的猜测是：Opus 4.8 更愿意"挑战"你的 frame，结果方差很高——有时候表现惊艳，有时候又错得很明显。他想要社区反馈来校准 Every 的评测体系（[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2061817375519809665)）。他还提醒大家注意一件 Every 内部正在"freaking out"的事（[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2061908190040645707)）。

### Google DeepMind, AI Lab

Google DeepMind is opening up Co-Scientist to individual researchers via Hypothesis Generation, part of a broader Gemini for Science push exploring AI-powered scientific discovery ([link](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2061857553076920643)). Over the past year, expert collaborators have used it to identify new liver-fibrosis targets, surface fresh approaches to ALS, and propose novel genetic leads for reversing aging by digesting decades of literature ([link](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2061857550438392094)). Under the hood, Co-Scientist generates thousands of hypotheses, then ranks them through a "tournament of ideas" with scientific debates, verifying claims against literature and data and pulling in fresh knowledge via web search and specialized models ([link](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2061857547095597517)).

Google DeepMind 把 Co-Scientist 通过 Hypothesis Generation 开放给个人研究者，这是 Gemini for Science 这一更大计划的一部分，主题是用 AI 推进科学发现（[链接](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2061857553076920643)）。过去一年里，外部专家用它找到了新的肝纤维化靶点、ALS 的新思路，还在消化几十年文献后提出了用于"逆转衰老"的新基因线索（[链接](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2061857550438392094)）。它的工作方式是：先生成上千条假说，再通过一场"想法锦标赛"——让这些假说之间展开科学辩论——来排序，同时对照文献和数据做核查，并借助 web search 和专用模型补充新知识（[链接](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2061857547095597517)）。
