### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch announced AI Gateway Rules, framing AI Gateway as a "Token Delivery Network" — like a CDN for AI models. When Fable was suddenly retired, Vercel worried about production workloads depending on it, and its data shows plenty of production traffic still running on older model versions. The new rules let you rewrite model routes on the fly without redeployment, e.g. rewriting `anthropic/claude-fable-5` to `anthropic/claude-opus-5` from the CLI ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2072741369848746315)). He also highlighted private connectivity between services on Vercel: register bindings in `vercel.json` with one line of code, then read the internal URL from an environment variable — works for Node, Python, and Dockerfile-based backends ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2072715658157027375)).

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 发布了 AI Gateway Rules，并把 AI Gateway 定位为"Token 分发网络"，类似于 AI 模型界的 CDN。Fable 突然下线时，Vercel 曾担心依赖它的生产负载受影响，而其数据显示大量生产流量仍在使用旧版本模型。新的规则功能允许在不重新部署的情况下动态改写模型路由，例如通过 CLI 将 `anthropic/claude-fable-5` 改写为 `anthropic/claude-opus-5`（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2072741369848746315)）。他还介绍了 Vercel 上服务间的私有连接：在 `vercel.json` 中用一行代码注册 bindings，然后从环境变量读取内部 URL，支持 Node、Python 以及基于 Dockerfile 的后端（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2072715658157027375)）。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie made the case for why we will need 100x more AI inference, pointing to Devin's "agentic mapreduce" pattern: swarms of agents that map relevant signals across a repo, fan out focused agents over bounded shards, reduce their findings into one report, then verify serious vulnerabilities in isolated sandboxes. The example is code security, but he sees the same pattern in knowledge work — Box customers want to process millions of documents for risk, insights, and relationships, and this will play out in pharma, banking, and beyond. He added that these capabilities are only possible when you can deploy a variety of models, both frontier and lower cost, given the sheer token volumes involved — a major value proposition for the applied AI layer ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2072519377371459836)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 阐述了为什么未来我们需要 100 倍的 AI 推理算力，并以 Devin 的"agentic mapreduce"模式为例：agent 集群先在代码仓库中映射相关信号，再将专注的 agent 分派到有边界的分片上，把发现汇总成一份报告，最后在隔离沙箱中验证严重漏洞。这个例子是代码安全，但他认为同样的模式适用于知识工作，Box 的客户希望处理数百万份文档来分析风险、洞察和关联，这一趋势还将在制药、银行等众多行业展开。他补充说，考虑到这类用例消耗的巨量 token，只有能够灵活部署多种模型（前沿模型与低成本模型并用）才能实现这些能力，这将是应用层 AI 的重要价值主张（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2072519377371459836)）。

### Dan Shipper, Every CEO

Every CEO Dan Shipper voiced a growing pain of working with long-running agents: Fable can go off for hours at a time and then come back with a two-paragraph explanation of what it did. His takeaway — "we need better ways for AI to tell us stories" about the work it performs ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2072805884376301737)).

Every CEO Dan Shipper 说出了与长时间运行的 agent 协作的一个日益明显的痛点：Fable 可以独立工作数小时，回来却只用两段话交代它做了什么。他的结论是："我们需要更好的方式，让 AI 把它做的事情讲成故事"（[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2072805884376301737)）。

### Matt Turck, FirstMark VC & MAD Podcast Host

FirstMark VC Matt Turck released a deep conversation with Bryan Catanzaro of NVIDIA about Nemotron and NVIDIA's AI lab: why a chip company puts hundreds of researchers on building AI models and gives them away for free. The episode covers whether open source is catching the frontier, the Nemotron family (Nano, Super, Ultra), training a 550B model in 4 bits, the hybrid Mamba-Transformer architecture, multi-token prediction, multi-teacher distillation, how NVIDIA decides who gets the GPUs, and the case that open AI is safer than closed ([link](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2072723410975629364), [also on Spotify/Apple/YouTube](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2072723415870411232)).

FirstMark 风投人 Matt Turck 发布了与 NVIDIA 的 Bryan Catanzaro 关于 Nemotron 和 NVIDIA AI 实验室的深度对话：为什么一家芯片公司要投入数百名研究员构建 AI 模型，然后免费开放。节目内容包括开源模型是否正在追上前沿水平、Nemotron 系列（Nano、Super、Ultra）、如何用 4-bit 精度训练 550B 参数模型、Mamba-Transformer 混合架构、多 token 预测、多教师蒸馏、NVIDIA 内部如何决定 GPU 分配，以及"开放的 AI 比封闭的更安全"这一有争议的观点（[链接](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2072723410975629364)，[Spotify/Apple/YouTube 收听](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2072723415870411232)）。
