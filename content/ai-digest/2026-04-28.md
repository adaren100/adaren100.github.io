### Roblox PM and AI creator Peter Yang

Peter Yang shared his top five takeaways from a long conversation with solo AI founder Tibo (Tibo_maker), who is now doing $1M+ MRR. The headline lessons: charge money on day one (free signups masquerade as traction); follow the signal when users surprise you (Tibo bought Typeframe at $2K MRR, noticed users hacking it to stitch AI clips, pivoted to Revid and is now at $600K+ MRR); price AI SaaS at $50–100/month to filter out tire-kickers; keep monthly churn below 20% before scaling acquisition; and build dedicated tool pages targeting long-tail Google searches like "turn audio into video." ([tweet](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2048771729821229091)) He also flagged a list of products that desperately need APIs/MCPs — Substack, Riverside, most video tools, every non-Mercury bank, government and healthcare portals. ([tweet](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2048918016642154698))

Roblox 产品负责人、AI 内容创作者 Peter Yang 分享了与独立 AI 创业者 Tibo（Tibo_maker，目前 MRR 已突破 100 万美元）深聊后总结的五条要点：第一天就开始收费，免费注册量很容易被误当作 traction；当用户做出意料之外的使用方式时要顺势而为——Tibo 当年以 2K MRR 收购了 Typeframe，发现用户把它当作把 AI 短片拼接成长视频的工具，于是干脆转型为 Revid，现在 MRR 已超过 60 万美元；AI SaaS 定价建议放在 $50–100/月，既能避免销售环节，又能过滤掉只想薅羊毛的用户；月流失率压到 20% 以下再去放量获客；以及为长尾 Google 搜索词（如 "turn audio into video"）建大量独立工具页面来获取流量。([推文](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2048771729821229091)) 他还顺手列了一份"急需 API/MCP 的产品名单"——Substack、Riverside、大多数视频剪辑工具、Mercury 之外的每一家银行、几乎所有政府和医疗类网站。([推文](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2048918016642154698))

### Replit CEO Amjad Masad

Amjad Masad announced a Replit-led builder cohort: 20 builders, 8 weeks, one goal — earn the first dollar of revenue. ([tweet](https://x.com/amasad/status/2048912698583453933)) In a separate quote-tweet, he argued Replit "speedran" the lessons others are now learning about agent runtime safety more than a year ago, and is the only platform with proper agent production isolation. Replit CEO Amjad Masad 宣布了一个由 Replit 主导的创始人计划：20 位 builder、8 周时间、一个目标——拿到第一美元的收入。([推文](https://x.com/amasad/status/2048912698583453933)) 在另一条引用推文里，他强调 Replit 早在一年多前就把"agent 运行时安全"这一课走完了，是目前唯一具备正经 agent production isolation 的平台。他的判断很直接：垂直整合平台的红利只会越滚越大。([推文](https://x.com/amasad/status/2048911624061522228))

### Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan

Garry Tan shared more details on GStack: it now ships with a Chromium build that has Claude Code as the CLI, and a `/pair-agent` command lets you bridge your local Claude Code session with a remote OpenClaw/Hermes browser agent. The setup gives an agent a string to paste in and then control your browser remotely — log it into things, scrape what you need. ([tweet](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2049035231903301771)) He also dropped a one-liner that resonated widely: "The artist is closer to reality than the CEO." ([tweet](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2049043739168129419))

YC 总裁 Garry Tan 进一步介绍了 GStack 的细节：现在它内置了一个集成 Claude Code CLI 的 Chromium 浏览器，输入 `/pair-agent` 可以把本地 Claude Code 与远程 OpenClaw/Hermes 浏览器 agent 打通——agent 会给你一段字符串粘贴进去，然后就能远程操控你的浏览器，帮你登录、抓取数据等等。([推文](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2049035231903301771)) 他还发了一句广为传播的金句："艺术家比 CEO 更接近现实（The artist is closer to reality than the CEO）。"([推文](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2049043739168129419))

### FirstMark Capital VC Matt Turck

Matt Turck called out a gap he says he's never seen this wide: in Silicon Valley, the consensus is that AI is self-accelerating, agents run everything, and skeptics are out of touch. In the Global 2000, the lived reality is "we spent a fortune on your AI chatbot two years ago and got zero productivity, our engineers like the coding tools but no one else cares, and agents are scary." ([tweet](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2048896617424527499))

FirstMark Capital VC Matt Turck 抛出了一个他形容"从没见过这么大"的认知鸿沟：硅谷内部的共识是 AI 正在自我加速、agent 已经接管一切、不信的人都是脑子没跟上；而 Global 2000 这一头的真实感受却是——"两年前我们花了大把钱上你们的 AI 聊天产品，没看到任何生产力提升，工程师确实喜欢 coding AI，但其他岗位都不感兴趣，agent 听起来还挺吓人的。"([推文](https://x.com/mattturck/status/2048896617424527499))

OpenClaw 联合创始人 Peter Steinberger 连发几个生动的"Codex 真的当 agent 用"的例子。一次他在 GitHub 上被 rate limit 卡住，Codex 直接打开浏览器，绕过 API 限制，在 GitHub 网页上点点点完成任务——包括在评论框输入文字、关闭 issue。([推文](https://x.com/steipete/status/2049035044702843053)) ([推文](https://x.com/steipete/status/2049035851493953750)) 还有一次 Codex 抱怨某个 API key 权限不够，他让它"那你自己去搞一个新的"，Codex 真的打开 Cloudflare 后台，自己创建了一个新的 API key。([推文](https://x.com/steipete/status/2049046713357254796))
