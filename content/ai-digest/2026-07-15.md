### Swyx, Latent Space podcast host

Latent Space podcast host Swyx shared a practical trick for comparing frontier models: give Fable and Sol the same task prompt, then feed both plans back to each model and ask them to judge. Fable reliably says Sol's plan is better, and he agrees. He also reacted to the GPT 5.6 launch, calling it possibly OpenAI's most successful model since ChatGPT and "IPO altering stuff". [model comparison](https://x.com/swyx/status/2077178561354735962) · [GPT 5.6 reaction](https://x.com/swyx/status/2077162040108748830) · [ainews writeup](https://x.com/swyx/status/2077179995521487317)

Latent Space播客主理人Swyx分享了一个对比前沿模型的实用技巧:把同一个任务prompt分别交给Fable和Sol,再把两份规划喂回给两个模型互相评判。结果Fable总是承认Sol的方案更好,他自己也这么认为。他还对GPT 5.6的发布发表了看法,称这可能是OpenAI自ChatGPT以来最成功的一次模型发布,直言"这是能影响IPO的大事"。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie endorsed a proposal for an AI standards body, distinct from a regulatory agency, arguing it would allow much faster iteration on standards with industry, and warning that if AI progress moves at classic government speed, America likely loses the AI race. The remaining challenge: even industry insiders don't agree on AI safety risks. In a longer post he laid out his structural view of AI: frontier labs keep pushing capability while competition drives per-task pricing down, open weights rapidly absorb frontier breakthroughs and act as a counterbalance, the applied AI layer wins by combining frontier and cheap models with domain evals and enterprise trust, and most enterprises should focus on feeding AI systems the right data rather than training their own models. Net net, he sees opportunity at every layer rather than a zero-sum game. [standards body](https://x.com/levie/status/2077043523703243070) · [structural view](https://x.com/levie/status/2076882332821373381)

Box首席执行官Aaron Levie转发支持了一份关于设立AI标准机构的提案。这个机构不同于监管部门,能与产业界更快地迭代标准。他警告说,如果AI进展变成政府的传统节奏,美国很可能会输掉AI竞赛;剩下的难点在于,连业内人士对AI安全风险都尚未达成共识。在另一篇长文中,他给出了对AI产业结构的判断:前沿实验室会持续推高能力,竞争压低单任务价格;开放权重模型快速吸收前沿突破,形成健康的制衡;应用层AI通过组合前沿模型与低成本模型、领域评测和企业信任来实现最优性价比;而大多数企业应该专注于把正确的数据喂给AI系统,而不是自己训练模型。总的来说,他认为每一层都有巨大机会,并非零和博弈。

### Ryo Lu, Cursor design lead

Cursor design lead Ryo Lu wrote a reflective essay, "when the dream becomes the job", about what happens when your hobby becomes your career and then machines get good at the parts that felt closest to the heart: writing, coding, designing, taste-like decisions. His conclusion: AI can make output faster and raise the floor of the craft, but it cannot want on your behalf or decide what is worth loving, so the real discipline is keeping a relationship with the source, the original love that made you care before the work became useful. He's also assembling a design team at Cursor and looking for design engineers. [essay](https://x.com/ryolu_/status/2077162119506833627) · [hiring](https://x.com/ryolu_/status/2077108336844210352)

Cursor设计负责人Ryo Lu写了一篇题为"when the dream becomes the job"的长文,探讨当爱好变成职业、而机器又开始擅长那些最贴近内心的部分(写作、编程、设计、品味式的决策)时会发生什么。他的结论是:AI可以让产出更快、抬高手艺的下限,但它无法替你产生渴望,也无法替你决定什么值得热爱。所以真正的修行,是与"源头"保持联系,回到工作变得有用之前、让你开始在乎这件事的那份最初的热爱。他还在Cursor组建设计团队,正在招聘design engineer。

### Claude, Anthropic's AI assistant

Anthropic's official Claude account introduced Claude for Teachers, built for K-12 privacy: Anthropic never trains models on these conversations, and student information is protected by a data processing agreement written to comply with FERPA. Ask for a lesson plan and Claude starts from your state standards and high-quality curricula by connecting through Learning Commons, then drafts a plan and student-facing materials you can revise and take into class. [lesson plans](https://x.com/claudeai/status/2077047279689535705) · [privacy](https://x.com/claudeai/status/2077047280767488218) · [learn more](https://x.com/claudeai/status/2077047282109714488)

Anthropic官方Claude账号介绍了Claude for Teachers,一款为K-12隐私要求而设计的产品:Anthropic绝不会用这些对话训练模型,学生信息也受到按FERPA标准起草的数据处理协议保护。老师只需提出备课需求,Claude就会通过Learning Commons对接所在州的课程标准和优质教材,然后起草教案和面向学生的材料,老师可以修改后直接带进课堂。
