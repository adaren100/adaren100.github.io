### AI at Meta

Meta AI released the full training code for Brain2Qwerty v1 and v2, a brain-to-text system using MEG devices and end-to-end deep learning to decode typed sentences from raw neural signals. Trained on ~22,000 sentences across nine volunteers, the system reaches 61% average word accuracy, 78% for the top performer, and performance scales log-linearly with more data — the v1 dataset is also being released by their research partner ([link](https://x.com/AIatMeta/status/2071566927420588162), [link](https://x.com/AIatMeta/status/2071566934571954326)).

Meta AI 发布了 Brain2Qwerty v1 和 v2 的完整训练代码，这是一套利用 MEG 设备和端到端深度学习从原始脑信号解码打字内容的脑机文字系统。该系统基于 9 名志愿者约 22,000 个句子的数据训练，平均词准确率为 61%，表现最佳的参与者达到 78%，且准确率随数据量呈对数线性提升——合作研究机构同步开放了 v1 数据集（[链接](https://x.com/AIatMeta/status/2071566927420588162)，[链接](https://x.com/AIatMeta/status/2071566934571954326)）。
