### AI Creator Peter Yang

AI creator Peter Yang offered a detailed assessment of OpenAI's latest launches. On GPT-5.6 Sol, he said its best quality is that it "has that dog in it" — it never gives up on complex tasks and is very reliable thanks to browser use and other integrations, though it seems to burn more tokens than GPT-5.5. On GPT Live, he called it arguably more important than the day's product updates for the masses, noting that humans have used voice for 100,000+ years while keyboards only arrived in the 1950s-60s. He also offered constructive criticism: the ChatGPT Work vs. Codex split is confusing ("so developers don't work?"), the Sol/Terra/Luna choice with five effort levels is overwhelming for non-power users, and separating chat history from tasks creates unnecessary friction. [Read more](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2075345016437039600)

AI创作者Peter Yang对OpenAI近期发布的产品给出了全面评价。谈到GPT-5.6 Sol，他说它最大的优点是"有股不服输的劲儿"——无论任务多复杂都不放弃，配合浏览器调用等功能可靠性很强，不过似乎比GPT-5.5消耗更多token。关于GPT Live，他认为就覆盖受众而言，这可能比当天其他发布内容更重要，并指出人类使用语音交流已有十万年历史，而键盘直到1950-60年代才出现。他也给出了建设性批评：ChatGPT Work与Codex的区分令人费解（"那开发者不算在工作？"）；Sol/Terra/Luna搭配五档努力等级对普通用户来说选择负担过重；聊天记录与任务分离也增加了不必要的使用门槛。[查看原文](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2075345016437039600)

### Every CEO Dan Shipper

Every CEO Dan Shipper published a day-zero vibe check of GPT-5.6 Sol and the merged ChatGPT Codex desktop app after a month of internal testing. His key findings: Sol is an A-tier coder but not at Fable's level — it scored 56/100 on Every's Senior Engineer benchmark versus 91 for Fable; it's the best writer among frontier models, clearer and more concise than Fable or Opus 4.8; design taste is improved but still below Fable and Opus 4.8; and the "real leap" is knowledge work, where Sol is the first model he's trusted to run whole autonomous loops, from processing email to scanning Facebook Marketplace for furniture. His pro tip: use GPT-5.6 as Fable's subagent for what he calls "the most goated combo in AI coding." [Read more](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2075263920613277809) He also called out the irony in OpenAI naming their general product "ChatGPT Work," since it implies developers don't do work. [Read more](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2075330044289802584)

Every首席执行官Dan Shipper在内测一个月后，发布了GPT-5.6 Sol与合并版ChatGPT Codex桌面应用的第一天实测评价。核心发现：Sol是A级编程助手，但尚未达到Fable的水准——在Every的高级工程师基准测试中Sol得56分，Fable得91分；它是前沿模型中写作能力最强的，比Fable和Opus 4.8更清晰简洁；设计品味有所提升，但仍不及Fable和Opus 4.8；而"真正的飞跃"在于知识工作——Sol是他用过的第一个可以被信任自主完成整套工作流的模型，从处理邮件到在Facebook Marketplace上淘家具一键搞定。他的专家建议：将GPT-5.6作为Fable的子agent使用，两者组合堪称"AI编程最强搭档"。[查看原文](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2075263920613277809)
