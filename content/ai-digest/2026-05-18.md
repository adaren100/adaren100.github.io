### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Box CEO Aaron Levie pushed back hard on the "AI means you don't need to learn the fundamentals anymore" narrative. His argument: the expert with AI will always outrun the novice with AI. The seasoned engineer driving agents will out-ship the vibe coder, the trained designer will out-design the prompt jockey, the analyst who actually understands financial models will out-leverage everyone else. His advice to students and colleges: do not bail on going deep in your craft, despite what's fashionable to say in the Valley right now ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2056051851439857933)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 很直接地反驳了那种"有了 AI 就不用打基础"的说法。他的逻辑是：用 AI 的专家永远会跑赢用 AI 的新手。会用 agent 的资深工程师，产出会甩开只会 vibe coding 的人；受过训练的设计师，做出来的东西会比只会写 prompt 的人好得多；真正懂财务模型的分析师，借助 agent 能撬动的事情也完全不在一个量级。他对学生和大学的建议是：不管硅谷现在流行说什么，都不要放弃在自己专业里往深处钻 ([链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2056051851439857933))。

Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan took a swing at Ken Griffin's cost-cutting framing of AI, arguing the ceiling just got raised and the real winners will be small "cracked AI-human-computer-symbiosis" teams that supersede entire legacy operations. His line: boil the ocean, don't cut your costs. On the tooling side, he said ZeroEntropy now beats OpenAI and Voyage AI for personal AI against his 120k-markdown brain, and that GBrain now ships with ZeroEntropy as the default embedding and re-ranking option ([link](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2056123737544757733), [link](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2056122508550738223), [link](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2056119107133870149)).

Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan 隔空怼了一下 Ken Griffin 把 AI 当作降本工具的思路。他的判断是：天花板刚刚被抬高，真正笑到最后的会是那种"AI–人–计算机共生"的小而精团队，他们会直接把一整套老旧业务给取代掉。他原话是：去 boil the ocean，别去 cut your costs。工具层面他也丢了几个明确信号：在他那个 12 万字 markdown 的个人知识库上，ZeroEntropy 现在已经压过 OpenAI 和 Voyage AI 成为他的第一选择；GBrain 也已经把 ZeroEntropy 设为默认的 embedding 和 re-ranking 方案 ([链接](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2056123737544757733)，[链接](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2056122508550738223)，[链接](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2056119107133870149))。

### FPV Ventures Partner Nikunj Kothari

FPV Ventures partner Nikunj Kothari laid out a "triple whammy" hitting employees joining freshly minted unicorns. First, tranched valuations are now standard, so the strike price at the headline valuation is often more than 2x the lead preferred price. Second, that means a high strike and a high 409a, making early exercise expensive and the wait for growth long. Third, comp packages look great on paper but are inflated by that same tranched valuation, which is also why companies chase higher headline numbers (it lets them grant less equity per dollar). His advice: still join for the learning, mission, and team, but use Claude or ChatGPT to actually model the exit math instead of staring at the sticker comp number ([link](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2056125669466333672)).

FPV Ventures 的合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 给现在打算加入新晋独角兽的员工列了一份"三重暴击"清单。第一，分档估值（tranched valuation）现在很常见，意味着你拿到的行权价往往是 lead preferred 价格的两倍以上。第二，由此带来的就是高行权价 + 高 409a，想提前行权要掏不少钱，不行权就只能慢慢等公司真正长到那个估值。第三，纸面 comp 看着很漂亮，其实就是被这种分档估值撑高的；公司也愿意去冲更高的 headline 估值，因为这样每一块钱可以少给一点股权。他的建议是：你可以为了学习、使命、团队加入这种公司，但一定要用 Claude 或 ChatGPT 老老实实把 exit math 算一遍，别只盯着那个挂出来的 comp 数字 ([链接](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2056125669466333672))。

His takeaway: he is not worried we'll run out of meaning and purpose ([link](https://x.com/adityaag/status/2056015684145127612)).

South Park Commons 的 GP Aditya Agarwal 用一句很朴素的话回应了"AI 把我们的一切都抢走了"那种焦虑：周五，他用 AI 写了一大堆代码；周六，他去看了 FA Cup 决赛，又去看了自己孩子的舞蹈汇演。他的结论很简单：他完全不担心我们会因此失去意义感和目标感 ([链接](https://x.com/adityaag/status/2056015684145127612))。
