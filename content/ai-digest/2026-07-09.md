### Former Google Product Leader Madhu Guru

Former Google product leader for Gemini, Veo, and Nano Banana, Madhu Guru pushed back on the idea that data and evals are low-skill work you can just buy and bolt onto a model. He laid out the real model lifecycle, model strategy, evals, pre/post-training and RL aligned to those evals, then go-to-market, and argued the hard part is staying focused on target eval sets while navigating architecture changes, regressions, competing data, and rival launches, then choosing between checkpoints that trade off target evals, regression evals, and early customer feedback. 曾负责谷歌Gemini、Veo和Nano Banana的产品负责人Madhu Guru反驳了"数据和评测是可以直接买来套用的低技能苦力活"这种说法。他梳理了真正的模型开发流程:模型策略、评测体系、围绕评测展开的预训练/后训练与强化学习,最后才是市场推广;他认为真正的难点在于,在架构变化、效果回退、数据来源竞争和对手发布新品的干扰下,始终聚焦在目标评测集上,并在多个checkpoint之间权衡目标评测、回归评测与早期客户反馈做出取舍。[查看原文](https://x.com/realmadhuguru/status/2074734468854899191)

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Box CEO Aaron Levie recapped themes from a couple dozen meetings with enterprise IT leaders on AI agents. He said companies increasingly need to solve an organizational operating-model problem, since agents work best tied to processes that cut across historically siloed teams, and that persistent data fragmentation keeps agents from giving accurate, on-policy answers. He argued that as everyone gets access to roughly the same frontier models, proprietary context becomes the new data moat, that token counts are the wrong adoption metric compared to business outcomes, that enterprises are heading toward a multi-model world with routing layers, that AI talent has to be trained internally due to a shortage, and that the best use cases fundamentally change how work gets done rather than just making an existing process faster. [Read more](https://x.com/levie/status/2074719479377109312)

Box首席执行官Aaron Levie总结了他与数十位企业IT负责人围绕AI智能体展开的一系列会谈中反复出现的主题。他指出,企业越来越需要解决组织运营模式的问题,因为智能体只有绑定跨越历史孤岛部门的业务流程才能发挥最大价值;而持续存在的数据碎片化问题,则让智能体难以给出准确且符合企业规范的答案。他认为,当所有人都能获得大致相同水平的前沿模型时,专有上下文(context)将成为企业新的数据护城河;相比token用量,业务成果才是衡量AI采用效果的正确指标;企业正在走向一个需要路由层的多模型世界;由于外部人才稀缺,AI落地人才必须靠企业内部培养;而最好的应用场景,往往是从根本上改变工作方式,而不只是让现有流程变得更高效。[查看原文](https://x.com/levie/status/2074719479377109312)
