### Box CEO Aaron Levie (levie on X)

Aaron Levie laid out one of the more compelling theses circulating right now: agents will use enterprise software 100X more than humans ever could, and that's actually a boon for platform companies. Because human usage is capped by working hours and cognitive bandwidth, most software is chronically underutilized. Agents work 24/7, run in parallel, and chain workflows across systems — unlocking use-cases that seat-based pricing could never capture. His take: expect a shift toward consumption-based revenue alongside traditional seats. "Lots of upside here for the companies that embrace this trend."
[https://x.com/levie/status/2045355693050655048](https://x.com/levie/status/2045355693050655048) | [https://x.com/levie/status/2045137708620906573](https://x.com/levie/status/2045137708620906573)

Box CEO Aaron Levie 提出了一个很有说服力的论点：未来 agents 使用企业软件的频次将是人类的 100 倍，而这对平台公司来说其实是巨大利好。由于人类的使用受限于工作时间和认知带宽，大多数软件长期处于利用不足的状态。而 agents 可以 24/7 运转、并行执行、跨系统串联工作流，解锁了 seat 定价模式下根本无法触达的使用场景。他的判断是：消费计费将与传统 seat 收费并驾齐驱。"拥抱这一趋势的公司，上行空间巨大。"

### Kevin Weil — VP Science at OpenAI (kevinweil on X)

Kevin Weil, formerly VP Science and Chief Product Officer at OpenAI, announced his departure: today was his last day. OpenAI for Science — the initiative he launched — is being dissolved and folded back into other research teams. He signed off warmly, calling out Sam Altman and other leaders by name and saying he's "rooting for" the team. A notable exit from one of OpenAI's most senior product-to-research crossover leaders.
[https://x.com/kevinweil/status/2045230426210648348](https://x.com/kevinweil/status/2045230426210648348)

Kevin Weil 正式宣告离职，他曾担任 OpenAI 的 VP Science 及首席产品官。他发起的 OpenAI for Science 项目将被解散并并入其他研究团队。他以温暖的语气道别，点名感谢 Sam Altman 等领导，并表示会"一直为团队加油"。这是 OpenAI 近期最重要的高管离职之一。

### Google Labs (GoogleLabs on X)

Google Labs officially launched **Google Flow Music** (formerly ProducerAI) — a standalone site for creating, sharing, and remixing original music via natural language prompts. It joins the existing Flow tool (images and videos), rounding out a creative AI suite: Flow for visual media, Flow Music for audio. Josh Woodward, VP at Google Labs, personally highlighted the new remix feature and the mission to "push the creative boundaries."
[https://x.com/GoogleLabs/status/2045250788864495874](https://x.com/GoogleLabs/status/2045250788864495874) | [https://x.com/joshwoodward/status/2045305262362530043](https://x.com/joshwoodward/status/2045305262362530043)

Google Labs 正式推出了 **Google Flow Music**（原名 ProducerAI），这是一个独立网站，支持通过自然语言生成、分享和 remix 原创音乐。它与已有的 Flow 工具（图像和视频）并列，构成一套完整的 AI 创作套件：Flow 负责视觉内容，Flow Music 负责音频。Google Labs VP Josh Woodward 亲自强调了新的 remix 功能与"突破创作边界"的使命。
