### Sam Altman, OpenAI CEO

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman said he now talks to ChatGPT more than he types to it, crediting the new voice model with "really crossing a threshold". In a more reflective post, he admitted OpenAI "did not have our best last 12 months ever, which is mostly my fault", but promised the next 12 months will be the company's best yet — framing the mission as giving people more freedom, agency, and wealth: "we want to do the right thing, but we do not want to scare people into doing our thing." [voice threshold](https://x.com/sama/status/2077842579232895286) · [best 12 months ahead](https://x.com/sama/status/2077817060068057493)

OpenAI首席执行官Sam Altman说,他现在和ChatGPT说话比打字还多,并称新语音模型"真正跨过了一个门槛"。在另一条更坦诚的帖子里,他承认OpenAI"过去12个月不是我们的最佳状态,主要是我的责任",但承诺接下来的12个月会是公司史上最好的一年。他把使命归结为让更多人获得自由、主动权和财富:"我们想做正确的事,但不想靠吓唬人来让大家用我们的东西。"

### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch reported that Kimi K3 is now the best performing model on Vercel's comprehensive web engineering benchmark, ahead of Fable and reaching a comparable success rate in less time — the first time an open model has led all proprietary ones on this eval, though he cautions benchmarks don't tell the full story and no model has yet hit 100% (the top performer peaks at 92%, or 96% "with help"). He also announced two major hires: React pioneer Pete Hunt will run Frameworks and lead Next.js, while GraphQL co-inventor Nick Schrock will work on Agentic Developer Experience. [Kimi K3 benchmark](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2077900518404321759) · [Pete Hunt & Nick Schrock join](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2077870043833229692)

Vercel首席执行官Guillermo Rauch宣布,Kimi K3成为Vercel综合性web工程benchmark上表现最好的模型,超过了Fable,并且用更短的时间达到了相当的成功率。这是开源模型首次在这项评测中领先所有闭源模型,不过他也提醒benchmark不代表全部,目前还没有模型达到100%(最高纪录是92%,"有辅助"时96%)。他还宣布了两位重磅加盟:React先驱Pete Hunt将负责Frameworks并领导Next.js,GraphQL共同发明人Nick Schrock将负责Agentic开发者体验方向。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie called the performance of open models "truly wild" and congratulated the Kimi team, arguing that every drop in the cost of frontier intelligence unlocks enterprise workflows previously gated by token costs — and that the mix of open and closed labs lets the applied AI layer tune and route models to their workflows. He also announced that Box now works with Databricks, letting enterprises extract structured data from contracts, financial documents, and supply chain content and query it alongside ERP, CRM, or product analytics data without moving or reprocessing the content. [open models take](https://x.com/levie/status/2077857617859535112) · [Box + Databricks](https://x.com/levie/status/2077782120232350205)

Box首席执行官Aaron Levie形容开源模型的表现"简直疯狂",并向Kimi团队表示祝贺。他认为前沿智能的成本每降一次,那些原本被token成本卡住的企业工作流就会被解锁一批,而开源与闭源实验室的共同突破,让应用层可以按自己的工作流去调优和路由模型。他还宣布Box与Databricks达成合作:企业可以从合同、财务文件、供应链内容中提取结构化数据,无需搬迁或重新处理内容,就能与ERP、CRM或产品分析数据一起查询。

### Dan Shipper, Every CEO

Every CEO Dan Shipper said his team will vibe-check Kimi K3 but that he is "extraordinarily skeptical" of claims it's as good as Fable. He also laid out a detailed history of how OpenAI turned Codex around: after GPT-5 in summer 2025 missed the agentic coding wave, a small team broke off to build a separate Codex model line free from ChatGPT's constraints, shipped a clearly superior desktop app in February, then merged it back in seamlessly — "most companies try to disrupt themselves and fail." [Kimi skepticism](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2077839678636732809) · [OpenAI Codex story](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2077825318992429286)

Every首席执行官Dan Shipper表示,团队会对Kimi K3做一轮vibe check,但他对"它和Fable一样好"的说法"极度怀疑"。他还详细复盘了OpenAI如何靠Codex翻盘:2025年夏天GPT-5错过了agentic编程浪潮之后,一个小团队独立出来打造单独的Codex模型线,摆脱了ChatGPT庞大用户群的束缚,2月推出了明显更强的桌面应用,之后又无缝地并回主产品。他感叹:"大多数公司试图自我颠覆都失败了。"

[distillation](https://x.com/amasad/status/2077908318975332417) · [chess engine](https://x.com/amasad/status/2077908032944779732) · [designers shipping](https://x.com/amasad/status/2077856392611074364)

Replit首席执行官Amjad Masad饶有兴致地指出,蒸馏模型居然可以超过教师模型。他还分享了自己正在开发的国际象棋引擎:先用200万个stockfish标注的棋局做fine-tuning,再跑一轮短的GRPO强化学习,目前下棋水平似乎已经超过前沿大模型,相关文档、实验记录和带注释的代码都已公开。他另外感叹:"设计师现在的交付速度,是以前认为工程师都不可能达到的。"

### Madhu Guru, Sr Director of AI at Meta

Meta Senior Director of AI Madhu Guru argued that open-weight models like Kimi and GLM will force a complete rethink of the enterprise AI stack, and that enterprises need to maximize model optionality. His three prescriptions: build rigorous evals (both regression evals for tablestakes features and aspirational hill-climbing evals) because "eval velocity is a competitive advantage"; build your own model routing since nobody understands your business like you do; and keep a model-agnostic harness so your system never knows which model is behind the API call. [full thread](https://x.com/realmadhuguru/status/2077885624607228018)

Meta AI高级总监Madhu Guru认为,Kimi和GLM这类开源权重模型将迫使企业彻底重构AI技术栈,企业需要最大化模型可选性。他给出三条建议:第一,建立严谨的评估体系,既要有保障基础功能的回归评估,也要有更高难度的"爬坡"评估,因为"评估速度就是竞争优势";第二,自建模型路由,因为没人比你更懂自己的业务;第三,保持模型无关的harness,让系统永远不需要知道API背后是哪个模型。

He recalled the spark for the project came from watching author Steven Johnson break down how he writes books; today over 30 million people and 600,000 organizations use it. Google Labs chimed in with the origin story: it began as a little Labs experiment called Project Tailwind. [rename announcement](https://x.com/joshwoodward/status/2077811657385079045) · [Google Labs on the journey](https://x.com/GoogleLabs/status/2077832590132949268)

Google副总裁Josh Woodward宣布NotebookLM正式改名:团队内部早就叫它"Notebook",如今对外正式更名为Gemini Notebook。他回忆项目的火花来自看作家Steven Johnson拆解自己写书的方法;如今已有超过3000万用户和60万家组织在使用它。Google Labs也补充了这段历史:它最初只是一个叫Project Tailwind的小小实验项目。

### Sebastian Raschka, evangelist

ML researcher and author Sebastian Raschka shared architecture notes on the latest open model: it's 250B parameters bigger than GLM 5.2, less sparse than Kimi K2.5's 1T (32B active at 3.2% sparsity versus 41B active at 4.2%), and doesn't use a hybrid approach like Nemotron. He's curious to see a token/sec throughput comparison. [analysis](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2077750586095628749)

机器学习研究者、畅销书作者Sebastian Raschka分享了对最新开源模型的架构分析:它比GLM 5.2大2500亿参数;比Kimi K2.5的1T模型更稠密(激活参数32B、稀疏度3.2%,对比后者激活41B、稀疏度4.2%);也没有采用Nemotron那样的混合架构。他表示很想看到token/秒吞吐量的对比数据。

### Peter Yang, AI educator and creator

AI educator Peter Yang shared reflections on hitting 100K YouTube subscribers: packaging matters less than delivering value right away; viewers can tell when a podcast guest is promoting something, so practical workflows beat fame; view counts are unpredictable (300K+ on some videos, 1K on others) but consistency is controllable; and he's still wrestling with the tension between maximizing views and making content he actually likes. [reflections](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2077906158577459349)

AI内容创作者Peter Yang在YouTube订阅破10万之际分享了几点心得:封面标题这些包装固然重要,但不如开门见山地提供价值;观众能看出播客嘉宾是不是来带货的,分享实用工作流比嘉宾名气更重要;播放量无法预测(有的视频30万+,有的只有1千),但每周稳定更新是自己能控制的;他还在纠结"追求播放量"和"做自己喜欢的内容"之间的张力,坦言尚未想明白。
