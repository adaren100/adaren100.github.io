### Peter Yang, product manager at Roblox and creator of the Creatoreconomy.es newsletter

Peter Yang spent over an hour wrestling with OpenClaw + GPT for a simple agentic task — sending a weekly stats recap email — with frustrating results. He ended up saying "you completely messed up the previous template" and "you totally screwed it up," even switching the model to Sonnet mid-session. He's a fan of OpenClaw but concluded this model simply doesn't follow through on agentic tasks or cron jobs, and his hope is that "Spud" (GPT 5.5) will solve this. On a brighter note, he's also streamlined his workflow down to just two apps open. ([tweet 1](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2046036593199497615), [tweet 2](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2045909612315172936))

### Nan Yu, Head of Product at Linear

Nan posed a sharp question: if every major AI lab put out a press release simultaneously, which one would have the shortest distance between what they said and what they actually believe? He also reflected on how the best response to a loaded question is sometimes to reject its premise entirely — "like asking should I get a red Lambo or a green Lambo, and the correct answer is you shouldn't buy a Lambo." He added a wry observation on PR credibility: being perceived as bad at PR can paradoxically make you more believable. ([tweet 1](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2045910980597530836), [tweet 2](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2045910308611326166), [tweet 3](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2045903644298465422))

Nan 提出了一个犀利的问题：如果各大 AI 实验室同时发出一份新闻稿，哪家说的话和真实想法之间距离最短？他还思考了一种洞察：面对前提有问题的问题，最好的回答有时是否定这个前提本身 — "就像有人问你买红色兰博基尼还是绿色兰博基尼，而正确答案是你根本不该买兰博基尼。"他还观察到一个关于 PR 公信力的反直觉现象：被认为不擅长公关，反而会让人觉得你更诚实。([推文 1](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2045910980597530836), [推文 2](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2045910308611326166), [推文 3](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2045903644298465422))

### Guillermo Rauch, CEO of Vercel

Vercel disclosed a serious security incident: a Vercel employee's account was compromised through a breach at an AI platform he used. Through that foothold, an attacker gained access to Vercel's Google Workspace and then deeper Vercel environments. While customer environment variables are fully encrypted at rest, "non-sensitive" env vars were exposed through attacker enumeration. Rauch called the attacking group "highly sophisticated" and suspects they were significantly accelerated by AI, noting they moved with surprising velocity and in-depth knowledge of Vercel's internals. The company is working with Google Mandiant, elite cybersecurity firms, and law enforcement. New security features have already shipped — including a dashboard overview of environment variables and a better UI for managing sensitive env vars. Rauch committed to making this "the most formidable security response imaginable." ([tweet](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2045995362499076169))

Vercel 披露了一起严重安全事件：一名 Vercel 员工在其使用的 AI 平台遭遇泄露后，账户被攻击者入侵，进而被用作跳板渗透进 Vercel 的 Google Workspace 及更深层的内部环境。虽然客户环境变量已全量加密，但被标记为"非敏感"的变量被攻击者通过枚举手段获取。Rauch 称攻击者"技术极为精湛"，并怀疑其行动速度受到 AI 的显著加持，展现出对 Vercel 内部架构的深入了解。目前 Vercel 已联合 Google Mandiant、顶尖网络安全公司及执法机构展开调查，并已上线新的安全功能，包括环境变量总览页面和更清晰的敏感变量管理界面。Rauch 承诺将把这次事件处理成"有史以来最有力的安全响应"。([推文](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2045995362499076169))

### Garry Tan, President and CEO of Y Combinator

Garry Tan shipped GBrain v0.14, which introduces a jobs server for OpenClaw based on BullMQ patterns — significantly improving throughput and reducing gateway load. He's also working on getting native OpenClaw subagents to run as minions for parallel task execution, though a PR to the core project wasn't merged. His current advice to others struggling with OpenClaw limitations: tell it to implement jobs to replace crons and subagents wherever possible. ([tweet 1](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2046060505127895051), [tweet 2](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2046060507799658774), [tweet 3](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2046062819322610009))

Garry Tan 发布了 GBrain v0.14，引入基于 BullMQ 模式的 OpenClaw jobs server，显著提升吞吐量、降低网关负载。他还在尝试让原生 OpenClaw subagents 作为"小弟"并行执行任务，但相关 PR 尚未被 core 项目接受。对于正在为 OpenClaw 局限性头疼的用户，他目前的建议是：让它自己实现 jobs 系统来替代 cron 和 subagents。([推文 1](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2046060505127895051), [推文 2](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2046060507799658774), [推文 3](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2046062819322610009))
