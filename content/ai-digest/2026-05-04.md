### Latent Space's Swyx

Swyx flagged the on-stage shutdown of Vibe-kanban by tokengobbler at AIE Europe — 30,000 MAU, going open-source-only — with the sharpest line from the post-mortem: "Everyone who is making money is doing 2 things: selling to enterprise, and reselling tokens. We were doing neither." He said the 2021-2025 software engineering retrospective from that talk will stick with him, and noted this isn't even the first company to shutter live at AIE. Earlier in the day he riffed that "efficiency is back on the menu again, boys" while quoting a related piece. (https://x.com/swyx/status/2050753293601935777, https://x.com/swyx/status/2050879234382655581)

Latent Space 的 Swyx 转发了 tokengobbler 在 AIE Europe 现场宣布关停 Vibe-kanban 的消息——产品有 3 万 MAU，但决定只以开源项目形式继续存在。他引用了那场剖析里最扎心的一句话："现在还能赚到钱的公司都在做两件事：卖给企业，倒卖 token。我们一样都没做。"他说那场覆盖 2021 到 2025 的软件工程复盘会让他记很久，而且这已经不是第一家在 AIE 现场宣布关停的公司了。当天早些时候他还发了一句 "efficiency is back on the menu again, boys"，转的是类似话题。(https://x.com/swyx/status/2050753293601935777, https://x.com/swyx/status/2050879234382655581)

### Roblox Product Lead Peter Yang

Peter Yang shared a small but high-leverage tip: install Amphetamine from the Mac App Store, uncheck everything under Settings → Session Defaults, and your agents will keep running even when the MacBook lid is closed. He also dropped his latest podcast with ex-Tinder CPO Ravi Mehta on a 3-layer context system for building AI products — Functional (what the app does), Visual (what it looks like), Data (how the data structure works) — with Mehta live-building a music discovery app from scratch to demonstrate combining all three layers in a single prompt. (https://x.com/petergyang/status/2050963126234034387, https://x.com/petergyang/status/2050946115374321901)

Roblox 产品负责人 Peter Yang 分享了一个简单但很顶用的技巧：从 Mac App Store 装 Amphetamine，然后在 Settings → Session Defaults 里把下面的选项全部取消勾选，这样合上 MacBook 盖子也能让 agent 继续跑。他还发布了和前 Tinder CPO Ravi Mehta 的最新播客，主题是做 AI 产品的三层 context 体系——Functional（app 在做什么）、Visual（长什么样）、Data（数据结构怎么组织）——Mehta 在直播里现场从零搭了一个音乐发现 app，把这三层在同一个 prompt 里组合给观众看。(https://x.com/petergyang/status/2050963126234034387, https://x.com/petergyang/status/2050946115374321901)

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Aaron Levie laid out the most structured argument of the day for why implementing AI agents in enterprises is going to create more work than people realize — a massive opportunity for consulting firms, FDEs from agent vendors, and net-new internal agent engineering roles. His checklist of what large enterprises actually have to do: get agents talking to data securely across decades of legacy systems; implement the right access controls, scopes, monitoring and logging; document existing processes in a form agents can consume; redesign workflows for human-agent collaboration (just replicating the old workflow "will mute the gains"); build evals for the new end-state processes; and keep up with a rapidly shifting set of best practices and architectural shifts. In a separate post he pushed back on the "AI as being" framing: "we should treat AI like a utility, not like a being. The more we confuse what AI is the more we will make ourselves go crazy with analogies that will never fully hold true." (https://x.com/levie/status/2051057677984469277, https://x.com/levie/status/2051009208393589096)

Box CEO Aaron Levie 给出了今天最有结构感的一段论证：在企业里落地 AI agent 所产生的新工作量，会远超大多数人现在的想象——这对咨询公司、agent 供应商派驻的 FDE，以及企业内部新出现的 agent 工程岗位都是巨大机会。他给出的清单是大企业实际必须做的事：让 agent 安全地访问跨数十年遗留系统里的数据；做好访问控制、scope、监控和日志；用 agent 能消费的形式把现有流程文档化；为人和 agent 协作重新设计工作流（直接复刻旧流程"会把收益基本抹掉"）；为新的端到端流程建 eval；并跟上 agent 领域里在快速变化的最佳实践和架构选择。在另一条推文里，他反对把 AI 当成"存在物"来类比："我们应该把 AI 当作一种公共设施，而不是一种存在。越是去比附 AI 是什么，就越会被那些根本站不住脚的类比搞得越来越糊涂。"(https://x.com/levie/status/2051057677984469277, https://x.com/levie/status/2051009208393589096)

### Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan

Garry Tan rolled out GBrain v0.26.3 with a full MCP OAuth panel — you can now control which clients (Claude Code, Cowork, ChatGPT, Perplexity Computer) get access to your "brain", with full invalidation and logs. The release also adds support for multiple repos, multiple MCP endpoints, and both full OAuth and Bearer Tokens; the one-time admin login link comes from OpenClaw or Hermes. He also described his current daily-driver stack: Codex with goals is powerful in conjunction with gstack (his plans and office-hours runs) and gbrain (code search plus the broader context of what he's trying to build and what he cares about). (https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051089703030686050, https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051089704658010321, https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051065641118130269)

Y Combinator CEO Garry Tan 发布了 GBrain v0.26.3，新增了一个完整的 MCP OAuth 面板——你可以管理哪些客户端（Claude Code、Cowork、ChatGPT、Perplexity Computer）能访问你的"brain"，支持完整的吊销和日志。这一版还支持多 repo、多 MCP endpoint，以及 OAuth 和 Bearer Token 两种鉴权方式；一次性的管理员登录链接通过 OpenClaw 或 Hermes 获取。他也聊了自己当下的日常组合：Codex 跑 goal、gstack 存所有计划和 office hours 记录、gbrain 做代码搜索并提供他正在做什么、关心什么的更广 context，三者搭在一起非常顺手。(https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051089703030686050, https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051089704658010321, https://x.com/garrytan/status/2051065641118130269)

### FPV Ventures Partner Nikunj Kothari

Nikunj Kothari aired a real frustration with where VC sentiment has drifted: too many funds are "capping downside" instead of maximizing upside, and when he asks a GP why they did a deal, by the fifth "why" the real reason usually surfaces as some version of "well, big token factory will acquire them so our downside is capped." His take: that's not how you return a 5-10x fund — incentives have shifted to AUM-maxxing and "deploy baby deploy," which feels deeply short-sighted. His closing line: time is the only thing you don't get back, so use it on things that matter and people who won't treat you as an option call. (https://x.com/nikunj/status/2050779734116856137)

FPV Ventures 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 吐槽了当下 VC 圈的一种漂移：太多基金在"压缩下行风险"，而不是在追上行收益。他每次问 GP 当初为什么做某笔交易，问到第五个 why 时，真实理由往往是这种话："反正 big token factory 之类的大厂会把他们收掉，下行就兜住了。"他的判断很直接：靠这种打法不可能回出一支 5-10x 的基金——激励机制已经变成 AUM 最大化加 "deploy baby deploy"，短视得离谱。他最后那句话很硬：时间是你唯一拿不回来的东西，把它花在重要的事和不会把你当 option call 的人身上。(https://x.com/nikunj/status/2050779734116856137)

### OpenClaw's Peter Steinberger

Peter Steinberger shipped RepoBar 0.4.0, meaningfully sharpening his GitHub menubar app: persistent SQLite caching, fewer wasted API calls, visible rate limits, better Issues/PR loading, and archive fallback support. He also showcased Codex reproducing a macOS-only launchd issue he previously couldn't reliably hit on a non-fresh install — a clean win for the Crabbox sandbox approach. (https://x.com/steipete/status/2051088325100831046, https://x.com/steipete/status/2051026592764240204)

OpenClaw 的 Peter Steinberger 发布了 RepoBar 0.4.0，把这个 GitHub 菜单栏 app 明显做得更聪明了：持久化的 SQLite 缓存、更少的无效 API 调用、可见的 rate limit、更好的 Issues/PR 加载，以及 archive fallback。他还展示了 Codex 帮他复现了一个 macOS 才会出现、并且必须在非全新系统才会稳定触发的 launchd 问题——这种他自己本来很难复现的场景，被 Crabbox 这种沙箱方式干净利落地拿下。(https://x.com/steipete/status/2051088325100831046, https://x.com/steipete/status/2051026592764240204)
