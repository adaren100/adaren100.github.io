### Peter Yang, AI Educator & Newsletter Writer

Peter Yang shared how Anthropic product teams use agents internally, quoting Jess, the product lead for Claude Managed Agents, who said access to the company's codebase has been her biggest unlock. Rather than pinging engineers to find out what they're working on, she can track PRs directly and see what's merged and deployed, giving her a far deeper understanding of the product than before ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2071292628302434361)).

Peter Yang 分享了 Anthropic 产品团队如何在内部使用 agent，引用了 Claude Managed Agents 产品负责人 Jess 的话：能够访问公司代码库是她最大的解锁点。她不再需要去问工程师在做什么，而是可以直接追踪 PR，看哪些已经合并、哪些已经上线，这让她对产品的理解比以往任何时候都更深入（[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2071292628302434361)）。

### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch argued that builders don't need a LinkedIn, they need a page on their own website describing and linking to what they've actually shipped, summing up the idea with the line "You need a Link, not a LinkedIn" ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2071284129275285580), [link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2071287181650653372)).

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 认为，创业者不需要 LinkedIn，而需要在自己的网站上有一个页面，介绍并链接到自己真正做出来的东西，他用一句话总结了这个观点："你需要的是一个 Link，不是 LinkedIn"（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2071284129275285580)，[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2071287181650653372)）。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie predicted that frontier-level cybersecurity models will inevitably become open and available to anyone, which will spawn alternative tech stacks that pull economic value and control away from the US. He argued most AI regulatory thinking wrongly assumes China can't catch up, and that gatekeeping advanced models leaves the US no safer and strategically worse off; the only real choices are to stay at the frontier or fall asymmetrically behind ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2071253118252356001)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 预测，前沿级别的网络安全模型终将变得开放、人人可用，这将催生出替代性的技术栈，把经济价值和控制权从美国手中分流出去。他认为，目前大多数 AI 监管思路都错误地假设中国无法赶上，而对先进模型实施"看门人"式管控既不会让美国更安全，战略上反而会更不利；真正的选择只有两个：保持在前沿，或者被不对等地甩在后面（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2071253118252356001)）。
