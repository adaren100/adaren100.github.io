### Latent Space's Swyx

Latent Space host Swyx weighed in on Vercel's AI Gateway data dump — his read of the model-by-use-case split is delightfully counterintuitive: Gemini leads in education and personal assistants, Anthropic leads in vibecoding and back office, and OpenAI leads in recruiting outreach. He flags that this is just the slice routed through Vercel's gateway, so market share remains a mystery. He also took a swipe at the "haha OpenClaw bad, prompt injection is the #1 danger" crowd, hinting that anyone who's seen enough leaked AI API keys knows the real risks have more layers. (https://x.com/swyx/status/2054720201070190632, https://x.com/swyx/status/2054570997865718220)

Latent Space 主理人 Swyx 对 Vercel AI Gateway 公开的数据做了点评，里面"哪个模型在哪种 use case 上领先"的分布相当反直觉：Gemini 在教育和个人助理上领先，Anthropic 在 vibecoding 和后台业务上领先，OpenAI 反而是在招聘 outreach 上领先。他特别提醒这只是经过 Vercel gateway 的那一小部分流量，整体市场份额还是看不清。他还顺手怼了一下那批"哈哈 OpenClaw 不行、prompt injection 才是最大威胁"的人，言下之意是看过足够多被泄露的 AI API key 之后，就会知道真正的风险远不止这一层。(https://x.com/swyx/status/2054720201070190632, https://x.com/swyx/status/2054570997865718220)

### Roblox Product's Peter Yang

Roblox product lead and AI newsletter writer Peter Yang ranted on the AI-layoff cover story making the rounds: when CEOs say "AI changed how we work, so we had to cut 1,000+ employees," what's really happening is they overhired in the zero-interest era and AI is just a convenient scapegoat. Over 80,000 tech employees were laid off in Q1, the highest since 2022–23. In a separate post he made a craft-vs-slop prediction: AI gets people to average quickly, but anyone who actually knows their shit or cares enough to go the extra mile will be more in demand than ever. He also threw flowers at whoever's making Anthropic's product videos — no AI video tool can touch that quality yet. (https://x.com/petergyang/status/2054569893060809151, https://x.com/petergyang/status/2054401226343952869, https://x.com/petergyang/status/2054363065576309052)

Roblox 产品负责人、AI newsletter 作者 Peter Yang 吐槽了最近这一波"AI 害的"裁员公关稿：当 CEO 们说"AI 改变了工作方式，所以我们不得不裁掉 1000+ 人"，真实情况其实是零利率时代过度招人，现在拿 AI 当替罪羊很顺手。Q1 科技业被裁员超过 8 万人，是 2022–23 年以来最高。他另一条 post 抛出一个 craft vs. slop 的判断：AI 能让大多数人很快达到"平均水平"，但真正懂行、真正愿意多走一步的人会比以前更抢手，因为大家会越来越渴望好东西。他还点名表扬了做 Anthropic 产品视频的那个团队，说目前没有任何 AI 视频工具能做到那种质感。(https://x.com/petergyang/status/2054569893060809151, https://x.com/petergyang/status/2054401226343952869, https://x.com/petergyang/status/2054363065576309052)

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Box CEO Aaron Levie made a bold call: forward deployed engineers, or the equivalent, are about to become one of the most in-demand jobs in tech. His reasoning — deploying agents is far more technical than deploying software. Software has generally been an updated version of an existing concept, easy for enterprises to slot in; with agents, you're effectively shipping work output into the enterprise, and the customer expects the task to be solved nearly end-to-end. That means vendors have to deeply understand the business process: helping pick the right models, building extensive evals, doing change management, setting up data for the agents, and continuously tuning the system in production. He frames it as another example of the highly technical work AI is actually creating. (https://x.com/levie/status/2054398342852194386)

Box CEO Aaron Levie 抛了一个比较大胆的判断：forward deployed engineer（或者类似角色）马上会变成科技行业最抢手的工种之一。他的逻辑是 —— 部署 agent 比部署软件技术含量高得多。过去几十年的软件基本上是某个已有概念的升级版，企业很容易往现有流程里换；但 agent 不一样，你实际上是在把"工作产出"本身交付到企业里，客户期望的是任务被基本端到端解决。这意味着 vendor 必须真正深入理解客户的业务流程：帮他们选模型、搭建大量 eval、做变更管理、把数据接好，然后还要在生产环境里持续给 agent 系统调参。他觉得这是 AI 真正在创造的高技术含量工作的又一个例子。(https://x.com/levie/status/2054398342852194386)

### FPV Ventures Partner Nikunj Kothari

FPV Ventures partner Nikunj Kothari had a wild NYC coincidence: he met a friend for coffee who lamented that his old startup never reached its potential, they brainstormed what the same idea would look like in the agent era, and Nikunj said he'd prototype something over the weekend — then walked straight into another startup's office unannounced and found them pivoting to *exactly* that idea, but already 5x better. He also pushed back on two things: VC has become "consensus capital," so if you're a founder for the sake of being one, you'll need to play that game, but if you're truly obsessed, treat each rejection as learning, not a verdict. And: don't trust anyone who gives advice in absolutes right now — a lot of old priors (don't boil the ocean, find your wedge) are turning out to be garbage in this era. (https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054612777785045209, https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054599845214650442, https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054412709119099266)

FPV Ventures 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 在纽约碰到一件巧到不行的事：他和一个老朋友喝咖啡，对方感慨自己以前那家创业公司没真正起飞，两人就头脑风暴"如果在 agent 时代这个想法应该长什么样"，Nikunj 还说他周末来做个 prototype。结果聊完直接走进另一家他一直 respect 但从没见过面的创业公司，对方告诉他他们正在 pivot，然后展示的产品 prototype 几乎就是刚刚他俩聊的那个想法，而且做得已经强出 5 倍。他还顺手抛了两个判断：VC 现在已经变成了 consensus capital，如果你只是"为了当 founder 而创业"，那你确实得迎合这套游戏；但如果你是真的 obsessed，那每一次被拒只是 learning，不是判决。另外一条：现在不要相信任何把建议讲成绝对真理的人 —— 很多老的 prior，比如不要 boil the ocean、要先找到 wedge，在这一波里已经被证伪掉了。(https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054612777785045209, https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054599845214650442, https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054412709119099266)

### OpenAI CEO Sam Altman

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman shared two things worth catching. First, a product musing: he gets some anxiety when he's not using the smartest-available model, but sometimes he doesn't mind if it's really slow — and he's wondering aloud whether the industry should focus more on a price/speed tradeoff rather than just price/intelligence. Second, a concrete push: for the next 30 days, OpenAI is giving companies that want to try switching to Codex two months of free Codex usage. He's positioning Codex as the best AI coding product and making it as easy as possible to test the claim. (https://x.com/sama/status/2054627102922797323, https://x.com/sama/status/2054626219858293128)

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman 今天有两件值得看的事。一是产品层面的 musing：他说自己只要不是用"目前最聪明的那个模型"，就会有点焦虑；但有时候慢一点他其实并不介意 —— 他在想，整个行业是不是应该多关注 price/speed 这个 tradeoff，而不是只盯着 price/intelligence 这条轴。二是非常具体的一个 push：未来 30 天内，对于想从其他工具切换到 Codex 试用的公司，OpenAI 直接送两个月的 Codex 免费用量。他对 Codex 的定位是"目前最好的 AI 编码产品"，并希望让验证这个说法的成本降到最低。(https://x.com/sama/status/2054627102922797323, https://x.com/sama/status/2054626219858293128)
