### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie argued that the battle in AI is shaping up to be a battle for context: agent effectiveness comes down to domain expertise, access to the right context and tools, and fitting into workflows where users can review and incorporate the work. The platforms that capture and govern the most context — and route work between frontier models for planning and cheaper models for volume — will be where agents do their best work, which is why the applied AI layer is far more than an LLM wrapper ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2073138135014502777)). In a companion post he stressed that deploying AI in the enterprise takes real work: most workflows weren't designed for agents to drop into, so companies must clean up data, modernize IT systems, build evals, and design where humans stay in the loop — which is why applied AI companies are expanding forward-deployed engineering efforts and why the FDE role will be one of the most critical jobs in tech ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2072875685811716182)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 提出，AI 领域的竞争正在演变为一场关于 context 的竞争：agent 的效果取决于领域专业知识、能否获取正确的 context 和工具，以及能否嵌入用户可以审阅并采纳其成果的工作流。能够沉淀并治理最多 context 的平台，加上懂得让前沿模型负责规划、低成本模型负责跑量的调度能力，将成为 agent 发挥最大价值的地方，这也是应用层 AI 远不止是 LLM 套壳的原因（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2073138135014502777)）。在另一篇长文中他强调，企业落地 AI 需要下真功夫：大多数工作流并非为 agent 设计，企业必须清理数据、升级 IT 系统、建立评估体系，并设计好人类在环节中的位置。这正是应用层 AI 公司纷纷扩充驻场工程（FDE）团队的原因，FDE 也将成为科技行业最关键的岗位之一（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2072875685811716182)）。

### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch pitched "agentic self-improvement": give your agent the ability to introspect its past runs, spot inefficiencies, errors, and redundant tool calls, and produce new prompts and skills from what it learns. That, he says, is why agent observability is built in when you deploy agents to Vercel ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2073132174958841887)).

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 提出了 "agentic self-improvement"（agent 自我改进）的理念：让 agent 有能力回顾自己过去的运行记录，找出低效环节、错误和多余的工具调用，并据此生成新的 prompt 和技能。他表示，这正是在 Vercel 上部署 agent 时内置可观测性的原因（[链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2073132174958841887)）。

### Dan Shipper, Every CEO

Every CEO Dan Shipper pushed back on benchmark chatter claiming Fable 5 and Opus differ in quality: "it's the same model," he clarified, though it falls back to Opus 4.8 slightly more often, so benchmarks end up measuring a mix of Fable and Opus ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2073097796941484486)). He also shared Every's Fable 5 prompt library for the holiday weekend ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2073077325520838993)) and had fun with the economics of it all: an entire iOS app for 5M tokens, a cleared bug backlog for 20M — "Fable working for you while you're at the pool: Priceless" ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2073076447992746379)).

Every CEO Dan Shipper 反驳了关于 Fable 5 与 Opus 质量差异的基准测试传言，他澄清说"它们就是同一个模型"，只不过 Fable 回退到 Opus 4.8 的频率略高一些，因此基准测试实际测的是 Fable 和 Opus 的混合表现（[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2073097796941484486)）。他还分享了 Every 的 Fable 5 prompt 库，供大家假期使用（[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2073077325520838993)），并调侃了一番这背后的经济账：500 万 token 就能做出一个完整的 iOS 应用，2000 万 token 清空整个生产 bug 积压，"当你在泳池边度假时 Fable 还在为你干活：无价"（[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2073076447992746379)）。

### Nikunj Kothari, FPV Ventures Partner

FPV Ventures partner Nikunj Kothari, despite being one of the more vocal critics of Gemini's product experience, conceded it's still the only place where a single API key does it all: Flash for fast, cheap, long-context structured tasks, Nano Banana for world-class images, search with grounding, and strong realtime audio and video — so many of his side projects now simplify down to just bringing a Gemini key ([link](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2073151491557478883)). He also floated a theory that labs secretly time model releases to long weekends: Opus 4.5 landed right before Thanksgiving, and Fable 5 right before July 4th, giving the masses time to tinker ([link](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2073071325644816440)).

FPV Ventures 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 虽然一直是 Gemini 产品体验最直言不讳的批评者之一，但他也承认，Gemini 仍是唯一一个用单个 API key 就能搞定一切的平台：Flash 负责快速、低成本的长上下文结构化任务，Nano Banana 提供世界级的图像生成，还有带 grounding 的搜索以及出色的实时音视频能力。他的许多业余项目现在都简化成了只需要一个 Gemini key（[链接](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2073151491557478883)）。他还提出了一个有趣的猜想：各大实验室似乎故意把模型发布安排在长假之前，Opus 4.5 赶在感恩节前发布，Fable 5 则赶在 7 月 4 日国庆前上线，好让大家有时间尽情折腾（[链接](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2073071325644816440)）。

### Nan Yu, Linear Head of Product

Linear head of product Nan Yu highlighted a sharp observation: if your field doesn't produce good training data, it was probably full of crap to begin with ([link](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2073070255031615877)). On AI and medicine, he offered a balanced take — the best scenario is a doctor who spends significant time on your case and is equipped with LLMs ([link](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2073066919200956793)). He also savored the irony of Tailwind being adopted by the company that brought you Bootstrap, calling it a supreme victory for Tailwind ([link](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2073194274435317767)).

Linear 产品负责人 Nan Yu 转发并点赞了一个犀利的观察：如果你所在的领域产不出好的训练数据，那这个领域很可能从一开始就充斥着糟粕（[链接](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2073070255031615877)）。关于 AI 与医疗，他给出了一个平衡的观点：最理想的情形是医生既愿意在你的病例上投入大量时间，又配备了 LLM 作为辅助（[链接](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2073066919200956793)）。他还玩味了一个颇具讽刺意味的现象：当年做出 Bootstrap 的公司如今也采用了 Tailwind，堪称 Tailwind 的终极胜利（[链接](https://x.com/thenanyu/status/2073194274435317767)）。
