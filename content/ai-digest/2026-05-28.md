### AI Engineer organizer Swyx

Latent Space's Swyx (organizer of AI Engineer, also wearing the Cognition hat) made his most full-throated case yet for Cognition being the largest independent agent lab in the world — framing it as a perfect storm of "firsts": first koding agent (Devin), first cloud dev infra, the most-reviewed code-review/security work, the first LLM-wiki knowledge base, top-tier GTM, and a bench heavy with IOI gold medalists ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2059717021944926238)). He argues an agent lab is structurally long across model diversity, reasoning/tool-calling, harnesses, domain-specific RLFT, coding data and evals, the full agentic SDLC, and increasingly being the chief partner to CIOs combating tokenmaxxing slop from both humans and agents — his read on why this is Peter Thiel's biggest AI bet. Separately, he flagged Devin's new automations as the first non-annoying proactive agent implementation he's seen: smart triggers instead of dumb crons kicking off agents ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2059782589016310102)).

Latent Space 的 Swyx（AI Engineer 大会的组织者，同时也戴着 Cognition 的帽子）这次最完整地把"Cognition 已经是全世界最大的独立 agent lab"这套论述讲了一遍——他把它包装成一个由若干个"first"堆出来的完美风暴：first koding agent (Devin)、first cloud dev infra、被业内最广泛认可的 code review/security 团队、first LLM-wiki knowledge base、s-tier GTM，再加上一个塞满 IOI 金牌选手的板凳 ([链接](https://x.com/swyx/status/2059717021944926238))。他的核心论点是：agent lab 在很多维度上结构性地"long"——模型多样性、reasoning/tool-calling、harness、特定领域的 RLFT、coding 数据与 eval、完整的 agentic SDLC，以及越来越多地成为 CIO 在对抗人类和 agent 双向 tokenmaxx 出来的 slop 时的首席合作伙伴——这就是他眼中 Peter Thiel 把最大筹码押在这里的原因。另外他还点名了 Devin 新上的 automations，是他见过的第一个不烦人的 proactive agent 实现：smart trigger 取代了那种"crontab 定时拉一次"的 dumb 方式 ([链接](https://x.com/swyx/status/2059782589016310102))。

He also shared a starter prompt anyone can paste into Claude Code or Codex to spin up their own version, plus a written guide walking through all 12 formats ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2059648853197082909), [link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2059642264079716420)).

Roblox 的产品负责人 Peter Yang 做并开源了一个 `/slides` skill，能把粗略的大纲在几分钟内变成一份自包含的 HTML 幻灯片——12 种 layout（cover、stat grid、chart、quote、code、comparison）、3 套模板、live chart、每页一个不抢戏的 ambient 动画，还自带一个闭环：让 AI 自己给每页截图，然后定位并修掉布局 bug ([链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2059642246614647259))。他也甩出了一个 starter prompt，任何人粘到 Claude Code 或 Codex 里都能直接搭出自己的版本，另外配了一份覆盖全部 12 种 format 的文字版指南 ([链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2059648853197082909)、[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2059642264079716420))。

### Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch used GitHub's outage as a sharp reality check on the "coding AGI is here" narrative — Vercel's anomaly detection caught the dip 16 minutes before GitHub's own status page changed, and his takeaway is that despite no shortage of models and agents (this is the company that brought us Copilot), software infrastructure remains an extremely hard problem, especially under unprecedented demand ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2059612940307714540)). On the same theme, he joked that agents can plausibly find low-hanging fruit like inadequate concurrency control or missing database indices, but applying `/goal`-style prompts to moderately complex production systems "borders on AI psychosis" — you can't just tell a model "make my p99.9 10ms plz" ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2059614728129831389)). He also reflected on Vercel redesigning its Deployments list, one of the earliest acid-test views that proved Next.js could handle the dynamism of an app and the speed of a website in one framework ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2059683670609285188)).

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 借 GitHub 的 outage 给"coding AGI 已经到了"这套叙事浇了一盆冷水——Vercel 的 anomaly detection 比 GitHub 自己的 status page 早 16 分钟就抓到了那次下跌，他的结论很直接：哪怕模型和 agent 不缺（这家公司可是做出了 Copilot 这种 AI 编程的第一个重磅产品），software infrastructure 本身依然是一个极难的问题，尤其是当流量是前所未有规模的时候 ([链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2059612940307714540))。沿着同一条线，他开玩笑说：agent 确实能找到那些"低垂的果子"——比如并发控制做得不好、数据库 index 缺失之类——但你要把 `/goal` 这种 prompt 套到一个稍微复杂一点的生产系统上，那就开始"AI psychosis"了，你总不能直接跟模型说"帮我把 p99.9 干到 10ms 谢谢" ([链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2059614728129831389))。他也顺手回忆了一下 Vercel 这次重做 Deployments 列表的来历——这是当年 (zeit) 平台最早的几个视图之一，也是检验 Next.js 能不能"既扛一个 app 的动态，又跑出一个网站的速度"的最早 acid test ([链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2059683670609285188))。

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Box CEO Aaron Levie laid out a clean counter to the "AI replaces jobs" narrative: outside Silicon Valley, a meaningful share of enterprises are hiring AND adopting agents simultaneously, because agents automate tasks, not whole jobs. The tasks still need steering, the outputs still need reviewing, and as efficiency frees up dollars in one place, companies are redeploying them into client-facing roles, sales, and customer success — not dropping the savings to the bottom line ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2059482349977653619)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 把"AI 取代工作"这个叙事很干净地反驳了一遍：在硅谷之外，他接触到的大量企业是在同时招人和上 agent 的——因为 agent 自动化的是 task，不是整个 job。任务依然需要人来 steer，产出依然需要人来 review；而当 AI 在某些环节释放出预算时，公司更倾向于把这些钱投到面向客户的岗位、销售和 customer success 上，而不是直接把利润落到财报底下 ([链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2059482349977653619))。

### YC President Garry Tan

YC President Garry Tan dropped a short but pointed framing for the agent-software moment: it's time to make new software, not recreate old software — when the form factor changes, what you create has to change with it ([link](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2059771112737865805)). On the policy side, he called for bringing the SAT back at the UC system, noting they don't even look at SATs in admissions anymore ([link](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2059770818369065099)).

YC 总裁 Garry Tan 给当下这个 agent-driven 软件时代抛了一句很短但很尖的判断：现在该做的是"新软件"，不是把"旧软件"重做一遍——当 form factor 变了，被创造出来的东西也得跟着变 ([链接](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2059771112737865805))。在政策端，他喊话 UC 系统：把 SAT 加回来吧，他特别指出 UC 现在录取流程里连 SAT 都不看了 ([链接](https://x.com/garrytan/status/2059770818369065099))。

OpenClaw 的创始人 Peter Steinberger 给出了他目前最有价值的一项 skill 推荐：`autoreview`——在 PR merge 之前自动跑一遍 code review，能挖出大量 edge case，有时候一次 review 能跑好几个小时 ([链接](https://x.com/steipete/status/2059453909819654554))。

### Every CEO Dan Shipper

Every CEO Dan Shipper distilled his entire "thriving with AI" playbook into one line: ride the models. On this week's AI & I, he and COO Brandon Gell unpack the structural reason automation creates more human work, not less — AI commoditizes yesterday's expert competence, which floods the zone with close-but-not-quite-right code, writing, and design, which in turn drives up demand for experts who can use AI to produce distinctive, memorable work ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2059673326247625084)). They also push back hard on the "AI layoffs" framing — usually it's a cleaner cover story than admitting mismanagement, overhiring, or weak financials — and argue that as AI accelerates change, the value of human judgment goes up, not down, because models can only execute against stable frames and the world keeps breaking those frames ([link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2059683748493357383), [link](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2059682459499774029)).

Every 的 CEO Dan Shipper 把他全部的"如何在 AI 时代过得好"经验浓缩成一句话：ride the models。在本周的 AI & I 节目里，他和 COO Brandon Gell 解释了"自动化反而带来更多人类工作"这个悖论背后的结构性原因——AI 把昨天那些"专家级能力"打成了白菜价，结果整个市场充斥着"差一点点"的 code、文案和设计，反而拉高了对那些能借 AI 做出真正有辨识度作品的专家的需求 ([链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2059673326247625084))。他们也对"AI 裁员"这个说法做了很硬的反驳——大多数时候这只是比"我们管理不善 / 招人过多 / 财务不行"更好讲的封面故事；他们的观点是：AI 让世界变化得更快，反而推高了 human judgment 的价值，因为模型只能在稳定的 frame 里执行，而现实总是在打破这些 frame ([链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2059683748493357383)、[链接](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2059682459499774029))。
