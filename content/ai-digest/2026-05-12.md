AI Builders Digest — May 12, 2026

### Former Tesla AI Director Andrej Karpathy

Former Tesla AI director Andrej Karpathy dropped a useful hot tip plus a longer thought piece: at the end of your query, ask the LLM to "structure your response as HTML" and then open the file in your browser — slideshows work too. The bigger argument: audio is the human-preferred input into AIs, but vision is the preferred output from them (about a third of the brain is a parallel processor wired for vision). He sketches the progression he expects: raw text → markdown (today's default) → HTML (the emerging new default with layout and a bit of interactivity) → eventually interactive videos generated directly by a diffusion neural net. He also notes input still needs work, because audio/text/video alone can't capture things like pointing at the screen — we're a long way from needing Neuralink before that mind-meld is exhausted. (https://x.com/karpathy/status/2053872850101285137)

前特斯拉 AI 总监 Andrej Karpathy 抛了一个非常实用的小技巧外加一段更长的思考：在 prompt 末尾加一句"把回答 structure 成 HTML"，然后用浏览器打开生成的文件，他甚至会让 LLM 把内容直接做成 slideshow。他更大的论点是：对人来说，音频是最舒服的输入方式，但 vision 才是 AI 最适合的输出方式——人脑大约三分之一是为视觉专门搭出来的并行处理器，是信息进入大脑的"十车道高速"。他给出的演进路径是：纯文本 → markdown（现在的默认）→ HTML（layout 加一点交互性，正在变成新的默认）→ 最终走向由 diffusion 神经网络直接生成的 interactive video。他还顺手提了一句输入端也还远没做完：光是音频/文字/视频都不够，比如他想直接用手指着屏幕上的东西沟通，这种 mind-meld 的空间还大得很，远远轮不到 Neuralink。(https://x.com/karpathy/status/2053872850101285137)

### Claude Code's Thariq Shaikh

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Box CEO Aaron Levie made the case that the FDE / professional-services opportunity for deploying agents is going to dwarf prior tech waves. His argument: unlike the on-prem-to-cloud shift, which mostly swapped the medium of a service (cloud CRM for on-prem CRM), agents rewire the business process itself — and every industry, every department, and every firm has its own idiosyncrasies. On top of that, getting agents into production is genuinely hard: you have to modernize data, get access controls / entitlements / permissions right for both humans and agents, supply the right context, eval and maintain across model upgrades, and run the change management of redrawing where the human sits in the loop. He's putting it into practice at Box too — they've started hiring AI automation engineering roles, essentially internal FDEs that pair with the business to ship agents into mission-critical workflows. (https://x.com/levie/status/2053888702427115811, https://x.com/levie/status/2053672965125140915)

Box CEO Aaron Levie 抛了一个挺大的判断：agent 落地催生的 FDE / 专业服务机会，规模会远远超过之前任何一波技术浪潮。他的逻辑是这样：on-prem 到 cloud 那一轮，本质上只是换了服务的载体（on-prem CRM 变成 cloud CRM），但 agent 这一轮是直接重写企业内部的业务流程——而每个行业、每个部门、甚至每家公司，业务流程都长得不一样。再加上把 agent 真正推上生产线本身就是个硬活：数据要先升级、access control / entitlement / permission 得对人和 agent 同时讲得通、要给 agent 喂对 context、模型一升级就得重新评估和维护、还得做"流程里到底哪一步留给人哪一步交给 agent"的 change management。他自己在 Box 内部也已经开始落这件事——他们正在招 AI automation engineering 的角色，本质上就是内部 FDE，跟业务团队搭档把 agent 推进 mission critical 的工作流里。(https://x.com/levie/status/2053888702427115811, https://x.com/levie/status/2053672965125140915)

### FPV Ventures Partner Nikunj Kothari

FPV partner Nikunj Kothari shared an honest observation about the investor side of the table: every investor preaches "have no priors," but he has yet to meet one who actually doesn't. He uses real estate as an example — if you pitched him a real estate startup, he can speedrun fifteen reasons it fails, which is unhelpful to a founder who needs someone to believe in the one reason it works. His read: naivety (read: optimism) is a virtue on both sides of the aisle, and finding someone who believes in your conviction is worth its weight in gold. (https://x.com/nikunj/status/2053816543981551665)

FPV 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 今天对投资人这一侧做了一次很坦诚的自我吐槽：每个投资人嘴上都说"做投资不要有 prior"，但他还没遇到过一个真没有 prior 的人。他拿房地产举例：如果你跟他 pitch 一个房地产创业项目，他能 speedrun 一遍 15 个失败原因——但 founder 这边其实需要的是有人愿意相信"它会成的那一个理由"，前者一点用都没有。他的结论是：naivety（说人话就是乐观）在桌子两边都是一种美德，而能找到一个真的信你 conviction 的人，价值是真的值得用黄金称。(https://x.com/nikunj/status/2053816543981551665)
