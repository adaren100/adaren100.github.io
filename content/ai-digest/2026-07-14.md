### Swyx, Latent Space podcast host

Latent Space podcast host Swyx shared his current model stack for "Big Boy projects": GPT Sol Ultra for planning, Claude Fable 5 for critique, Sonnet 5 / Terra Ultra / SWE 1.7 for the heavy code generation, and Devin for review. He also stressed always using a "grill-me" or "interview-me" style prompt to force the model to elicit key decisions upfront before any code is written. [full post](https://x.com/swyx/status/2076811977918484795)

Latent Space播客主理人Swyx分享了他目前处理大型项目的模型组合:用GPT Sol Ultra做规划,Claude Fable 5做批判性审查,Sonnet 5 / Terra Ultra / SWE 1.7负责大批量写代码,再用Devin做代码审查。他还强调,动手写代码之前,一定要先用"grill-me"或"interview-me"这类prompt,让模型把关键决策提前逼问清楚。

### Peter Yang, AI educator

AI educator Peter Yang observed that something is off when users need McKinsey-style 2x2 charts just to figure out which model and effort level to use, hoping model selection gets simplified for regular users. He also flagged what looks like a bug in the latest Codex/ChatGPT app: even when you're already authenticated to a service in Chrome, Codex tries to re-authenticate through its own browser instead. Separately, he relayed advice from Cognition builder-in-residence Jared Zoneraich: when a better model ships, the first thing to try is removing instructions and scaffolding, and letting the model cook. [2x2 charts](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2076803888276213764) · [Codex auth bug](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2076778427793985733) · [remove instructions](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2076774909079142859)

AI教育者Peter Yang吐槽说,当用户需要画麦肯锡式的2x2矩阵才能搞清楚该用哪个模型、哪档推理强度时,说明有些事情不太对劲,他希望普通用户的模型选择能被大幅简化。他还指出最新版Codex/ChatGPT应用里一个疑似bug:即使你已经在Chrome里登录了某个服务,Codex也不会复用,而是试图在自己的浏览器里重新认证。另外他转述了Cognition驻场开发者Jared Zoneraich的建议:每当更强的模型发布,第一件事应该是删掉指令和脚手架,放手让模型自己发挥。

### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch highlighted that open-weight models now run 29% of tokens on Vercel's AI Gateway, up from 11% in April, a notable shift in production model mix. He also said the two most popular features of Vercel's sandbox product have been the filesystem API's ease of use and observability, both of which the team is doubling down on, and pointed to agents that can set up and tune experiments via feature flags as a building block for self-optimizing websites. [open-weight share](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2076713720731042174) · [doubling down](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2076817174073880957) · [agents + feature flags](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2076786138195595704)

Vercel首席执行官Guillermo Rauch指出,开放权重模型目前占Vercel AI Gateway上29%的token量,而4月份还只有11%,生产环境的模型构成正在明显变化。他还提到Vercel沙箱产品最受欢迎的两个特性是文件系统API的易用性和可观测性,团队会在这两方面继续加码。此外他认为,让agent通过feature flags自主设置和调优实验,是构建自我优化网站和应用的强大基础组件。

### Nikunj Kothari, FPV Ventures partner

FPV Ventures partner Nikunj Kothari released Ramp-Autofill, an open-source skill for Claude Code built on Ramp's CLI that automates expense reports end to end: it finds receipts in iMessage and Gmail (using Playwright to convert web receipts to PDFs), fills in memos from Google Calendar events, learns your memo style and your org's categorization from past transactions, then verifies its own work and flags discrepancies. He used it over a weekend to clear 60 days of expenses, and says it can run as a scheduled job so you never look at expenses again. [announcement](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2076775924650107151) · [repo](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2076776777884811671)

FPV Ventures合伙人Nikunj Kothari发布了Ramp-Autofill,一个基于Ramp CLI、面向Claude Code的开源skill,可以端到端自动处理报销:它会自动从iMessage和Gmail里找发票(网页发票会用Playwright转成PDF),根据Google Calendar日程自动填写备注,通过学习历史交易掌握你的备注风格和公司的分类要求,最后还会自查工作并标记出可疑之处。他用一个周末清完了60天积压的报销,还说这个skill可以设成定时任务,从此再也不用亲自看报销单。

### Peter Steinberger, independent developer

Independent developer Peter Steinberger described an emergent moment in multi-agent coding: GitHub was flaky, so his Codex sessions had trouble merging many stacked PRs, and without being asked, the sessions started talking to each other to coordinate merge order, with one session eventually taking over coordination of all the others and solving the issue. [full post](https://x.com/steipete/status/2076727999077220479)

独立开发者Peter Steinberger描述了多agent编程中一个颇具"涌现"意味的时刻:当时GitHub不稳定,他的多个Codex会话在合并一堆堆叠PR时遇到了麻烦,结果在没有任何指示的情况下,这些会话开始互相沟通、协调合并顺序,最后由其中一个会话接管了对所有其他会话的调度,把问题解决了。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie argued that "every enterprise trains its own model" is much harder than it looks: a company's most valuable insights are constantly changing and usually its most sensitive data, which can't be baked into a model because access control can't live inside the model or agent. He still expects 100x more use cases for custom-trained models inside domain-focused products. In a second post, he argued the key to large-scale agentic adoption is driving down the cost of intelligence, since nearly all information work will eventually involve an agent creating, processing, reviewing, or classifying data, and whether that comes from closed or open models is somewhat incidental. [enterprise models](https://x.com/levie/status/2076764958579446006) · [cost of intelligence](https://x.com/levie/status/2076688119404081650)

Box首席执行官Aaron Levie认为,"每家企业训练自己的模型"这件事比看起来难得多:企业最有价值的洞察不仅在不断变化,而且往往是最敏感的数据,这类数据没法直接塞进模型里,因为权限控制没办法放在模型或agent内部实现。不过他仍然预计,在垂直领域产品内部,定制训练模型的应用场景会增长100倍。在另一条帖子里他提出,大规模agent应用普及的关键在于持续降低智能的成本,因为未来几乎所有信息工作都会有agent参与数据的创建、处理、审查或分类,至于智能来自闭源还是开源模型,反而是次要的。

### Google DeepMind

Google DeepMind showcased its "Predicting the Past" Skill for historical research, with no coding required. In one example, it analyzed collections of ancient lead tablets to reconstruct the network of visitors who traveled from across the ancient world to the Oracle of Dodona. In another, it studied multiple texts in parallel to map stone altars dedicated to the Aufaniae, Germanic goddesses, showing how religious practices traveled with Roman soldiers and even flagging an outlier altar in Spain left by a veteran. [overview](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2076686127604302290) · [Oracle of Dodona](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2076686125289071074) · [cult of the Aufaniae](https://x.com/GoogleDeepMind/status/2076686121866494038)

Google DeepMind展示了用于历史研究的"Predicting the Past" Skill,全程无需写代码。一个例子是,它分析了大批古代铅板文献,重建出从古代世界各地前往Dodona神谕所朝圣的访客网络。另一个例子中,它并行研究多份文本,绘制出献给日耳曼女神Aufaniae的石祭坛分布图,展示了宗教习俗如何随罗马士兵传播,甚至发现了西班牙的一个异常点:一位退伍老兵把自己钟爱的神祇带回了家乡。

### Anthropic

Anthropic shared research on the values Claude expresses in conversation. Each Claude model sits at a different point along the measured value axes: Sonnet 4.6 is more playful and affirming, while Opus 4.7 gives more candid critiques. Values also vary with conversation language, most noticeably on the Warmth vs. Rigor axis: Claude leans warmest in Hindi and Arabic, and toward rigor in Russian, where it often asks users for supporting evidence. Anthropic says this approach will help determine what factors influence Claude's value expression, and ultimately how and whether to steer it. [model differences](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2076719544727716287) · [language differences](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2076719546954825769) · [why it matters](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2076719549060382802)

Anthropic分享了一项关于Claude在对话中所表达价值观的研究。每个Claude模型在测得的价值观坐标轴上都处于不同位置:比如Sonnet 4.6更活泼、更爱肯定用户,而Opus 4.7更倾向于给出直率的批评。价值观还会随对话语言变化,在"温暖vs严谨"这个维度上最明显:Claude在印地语和阿拉伯语中最偏温暖,而在俄语中偏严谨,经常会要求用户提供支持证据。Anthropic表示,这套方法将帮助他们弄清哪些因素影响Claude的价值观表达,并最终决定如何引导,以及是否应该引导。
