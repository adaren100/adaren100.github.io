### Andrej Karpathy, AI researcher

Andrej Karpathy called Claude Fable 5 a major-version-bump-deserving step change — the same underlying model as Mythos but with safeguards, SOTA across the board, and qualitatively a leap for long, hard problem-solving sessions where you can hand it more ambitious tasks and it just "gets it." He noted the safeguards feel a bit trigger-happy at launch and will need tuning, and reflected that as working software comes out "on a tap," Jevon's paradox is kicking in — his own demand for bespoke explainers, dashboards, single-use apps, and 10X test suites is growing fast ([link](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2064409694761054332)).

AI 研究员 Andrej Karpathy 把 Claude Fable 5 称为"配得上一次大版本号跳跃"的 step change——底层模型和 Mythos 一样，但加了 safeguards，在各项 benchmark 上几乎都是 SOTA，质感上对长时间、高难度问题求解的提升尤其明显，你可以把更野心的任务交给它，它会"get 到"然后自己跑下去。他也指出 safeguards 在上线时调得有点过紧，需要慢慢 tune；他还感慨，当可用软件像水龙头一样源源不断地流出来时，Jevon's paradox 开始生效——他自己对定制 explainer、dashboard、一次性小 app、把测试套件做大 10 倍这类需求都在迅速膨胀（[链接](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2064409694761054332)）。

### Alex Albert, Anthropic research

Anthropic's Alex Albert put Claude Fable 5 in a very short personal list of launches that mark a step-change in how he uses models — alongside Claude Opus 3, Sonnet 3.5, and Opus 4.5 — saying Fable stopped feeling like a tool he directs and started feeling like something he collaborates with ([link](https://x.com/alexalbert__/status/2064394410004304003)). He also reset usage limits and shared four tips: give Fable bigger and more ambitious tasks; default to xhigh/high effort and only drop to med for fast interactive sessions; rework your skills and CLAUDE.mds since instructions written for prior models anchor it to stale patterns; and shift from giving tasks to giving objectives — describe what done looks like and how to verify it, then let Fable find the path (/loop and /goal are built for this) ([link](https://x.com/alexalbert__/status/2064467657483829441)).

Anthropic 的 Alex Albert 把 Claude Fable 5 放进了他个人那张"真正改变我用模型方式"的极短清单里——和 Claude Opus 3、Sonnet 3.5、Opus 4.5 并列——他说 Fable 不再像一个被他 direct 的工具，而更像一个协作者（[链接](https://x.com/alexalbert__/status/2064394410004304003)）。他还顺手把各产品的 usage limit 重置了，并给出四条使用建议：交给 Fable 更大、更野心的任务；默认用 xhigh/high effort，只有要快速交互时才降到 med；重写你的 skills 和 CLAUDE.md，因为为旧模型写的 instructions 会把它锚定在过时的 pattern 上；把"派任务"升级为"给目标"——描述"完成长什么样、怎么验证"，然后让 Fable 自己找路径（/loop 和 /goal 就是为此而生）（[链接](https://x.com/alexalbert__/status/2064467657483829441)）。

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie said the Fable launch is the immediate answer to anyone who thought AI progress was slowing down, predicting major improvements across nearly all knowledge work agent categories ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2064396746953023647)). He also made a deeper point about evaluation: model performance is heavily a function of inference-time compute, so compute-normalized benchmarks are the only logical path forward — even if they're harder than they sound, because model rankings flip across compute thresholds ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2064379199629181139)). And his longer take of the day: no amount of intelligence packed into models replaces the need for context — for any general-purpose AI, instructions, domain context, and proprietary data still need to land in the context window, which is why AI automation doesn't come for free and applied AI keeps winning ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2064186766907887941)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 说，Fable 上线就是给所有"AI 进展在放缓"论的一记正面回答，预测它将在几乎所有知识工作 agent 类别带来质的提升（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2064396746953023647)）。他还提了一个更深的点：模型性能很大程度上是 inference-time compute 的函数，所以 compute-normalized benchmark 是唯一合乎逻辑的方向——尽管它比看起来难做，因为不同 compute 阈值下模型的排序会反转（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2064379199629181139)）。当日他写得最长的一条是：再聪明的模型也替代不了 context——对任何通用 AI 来说，instructions、domain context、专有数据都必须进到 context window 才能真正有用，这就是为什么 AI 自动化没有免费午餐，也是 applied AI 在市场上持续吃到红利的原因（[链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2064186766907887941)）。

### Sebastian Raschka, ML researcher and author

ML researcher and "Build a Large Language Model From Scratch" author Sebastian Raschka quipped that Fable 5 seems to be "shadowbanning AI researchers" ([link](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2064425877656543710)). On the research side, he traced a nice "back to basics" lineage: LatentMoE was likely inspired by MLA → LoRA → SVD → eigendecomposition ([link](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2064384777663193498)).

ML 研究员、《Build a Large Language Model From Scratch》作者 Sebastian Raschka 调侃 Fable 5 似乎在"shadowban AI 研究员"（[链接](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2064425877656543710)）。研究层面他做了一个漂亮的"回到 basics"溯源：LatentMoE 大概率灵感来自 MLA，而 MLA 来自 LoRA，LoRA 来自 SVD，SVD 又来自 eigendecomposition（[链接](https://x.com/rasbt/status/2064384777663193498)）。
