### Peter Yang, AI educator

AI educator Peter Yang argued that banning Chinese AI models would be just as much a self-own as banning Chinese EVs, a pointed jab at the growing wave of regulatory hawkishness. He also shared a clip from Anthropic's Thariq on why agent verification needs a separate judge: rubric-based review has to come from a second agent, not the one that produced the output, because models show "self-preferential bias" and go easy on their own work, a lesson he says extends well beyond video shorts. [Chinese models](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2079273815004303477) · [self-preferential bias](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2079257646939742542)

AI教育者Peter Yang提出,禁止中国AI模型将和禁止中国电动车一样,是一次彻头彻尾的"自伤",直指日益高涨的监管鹰派声浪。他还转发了Anthropic的Thariq的一段发言,解释了为什么agent的验证需要一个独立的裁判:基于rubric的审核必须由另一个agent来做,而不是产出内容的那个agent,因为模型存在"自我偏好偏差",会对自己的输出更宽松,他表示这个道理远不止适用于视频短片。[关于中国模型](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2079273815004303477) · [自我偏好偏差](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2079257646939742542)

### Amjad Masad, Replit CEO

Replit CEO Amjad Masad flagged what might be the first physical product ever shipped by a coding agent. He also argued that outside a handful of giants like Netflix and Spotify, huge consumer subscription businesses are rare, because consumers mostly spend their money on food, rent, entertainment, and shopping, while software is typically bought by their employer rather than themselves. [first physical product](https://x.com/amasad/status/2079282869063786541) · [why consumer subscriptions are rare](https://x.com/amasad/status/2079086360703680583)

Replit首席执行官Amjad Masad指出,这可能是史上第一个由coding agent交付的实体产品。他还认为,除了Netflix、Spotify这类少数巨头之外,大型消费级订阅业务其实很罕见,因为消费者的钱主要花在食物、房租、娱乐和购物上,而软件通常是由公司买单,不是个人。[史上第一个实体产品](https://x.com/amasad/status/2079282869063786541) · [为什么消费级订阅业务稀少](https://x.com/amasad/status/2079086360703680583)

### Nikunj Kothari, FPV Ventures partner

FPV Ventures partner Nikunj Kothari warned that many founders from the last 18 months will soon learn that just because AI has no "moats" left doesn't mean scale and capital become the new moat. Pointing to Webvan, Groupon, MySpace, Yahoo, AltaVista, Blockbuster, and Nokia, he noted each was structurally and financially strong yet still got beaten by a company with a sharper unique insight, or simply collapsed under the weight of its own scale; the real needle founders have to thread is finding an insight worth a 10+ year journey without letting capital and scale substitute for it. [moats and scale](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2079328912912355470)

FPV Ventures合伙人Nikunj Kothari提醒,过去18个月里的许多创始人很快会发现,AI已经没有"护城河"了,但这不代表规模和资本就能成为新的护城河。他列举了Webvan、Groupon、MySpace、Yahoo、AltaVista、Blockbuster和Nokia,指出这些公司当年在结构和资金上都很强势,却依然被拥有更独到洞见的公司击败,或者干脆被自身的规模压垮。创始人真正要做的,是找到一个值得投入十年以上的独特洞见,而不是让资本和规模成为洞见的替代品。[护城河与规模](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2079328912912355470)
