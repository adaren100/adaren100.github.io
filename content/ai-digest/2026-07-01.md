### Peter Yang, AI Educator & Content Creator

Peter Yang pushed back on the "software factory" framing, arguing that assembly-line metaphors badly miss what AI-human collaboration will actually look like. He sees the future as more conductor-orchestra, film director-crew, or chef-kitchen — creative work that can't be reduced to a production line. He also admitted he's not keen on the trend of making "(model) is INSANE" hype videos every time a new model drops ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2072051258882666620), [link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2072098874081296855)).

Peter Yang 反对"软件工厂"这一说法，认为流水线式的比喻根本没有抓住 AI 与人类协作的本质。他认为未来更像是指挥家与乐团、电影导演与剧组、主厨与厨师团队的关系——是需要创意的工作，无法简化为生产线。他也坦言自己不愿加入每次新模型发布就制作"（模型名）太强了"夸张视频的游戏（[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2072051258882666620)，[链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2072098874081296855)）。

### Andrew Ng, AI Evangelist & Educator

Andrew Ng published a detailed essay on "loop engineering," laying out three nested loops that now define how he builds 0-to-1 AI products. The innermost agentic coding loop runs every few minutes — a coding agent writes, tests, and iterates code autonomously, using a browser to check its own work without human intervention. The middle developer feedback loop runs over hours — the developer reviews the product, makes higher-level decisions, and steers the agent. The outer external feedback loop — alpha testers, A/B tests, user interviews — is still slow (days to weeks) and still requires human judgment, because the developer holds context about users that the AI system does not. Ng notes that engineers are increasingly playing a partial product management role, with the hardest new skill being shaping product vision and balancing building with user feedback ([link](https://x.com/AndrewYNg/status/2071988145667928442)).

吴恩达发布了一篇关于"loop engineering"（循环工程）的详细文章，梳理出他如今构建 0 到 1 的 AI 产品时所依赖的三个嵌套循环。最内层的 agent 编程循环每隔几分钟运行一次——coding agent 自主编写、测试和迭代代码，用浏览器检查自己的工作，无需人工介入。中间层的开发者反馈循环以小时为单位——开发者审查产品，做出更高层次的产品决策，引导 agent 方向。最外层的外部反馈循环——Alpha 测试、A/B 测试、用户访谈——依然缓慢（以天或周计），依然需要人类判断，因为开发者掌握着 AI 系统所不具备的用户背景知识。吴恩达指出，工程师正越来越多地扮演部分产品经理的角色，而最难培养的新能力是塑造产品愿景以及在动手构建与获取用户反馈之间找到平衡（[链接](https://x.com/AndrewYNg/status/2071988145667928442)）。
