### Latent Space's Swyx

Swyx made the case that company engineering blogs only stay alive when bylines belong to named individuals rather than "the ___ team," because real ownership brings real accountability ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2055079344632926603)). He also called out Raycast's recent tech stack writeup as one of the best he's ever read, arguing that devtools companies should publish these regularly because they signal product care, attract SOTA-minded hires, deter competitors, and help non-fit users understand why an "easy" request isn't actually easy ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2054938522034020816)). Separately, he flagged a new Latent Space podcast episode with Redpoint's Jacob Effron on AI x Healthcare, a vertical Swyx admits his team is underexposed to ([link](https://x.com/swyx/status/2055071896920879236)).

Latent Space 的 Swyx 提出，公司的工程博客只有在署名是具体个人、而不是"某某团队"时才有生命力，因为真正的归属感才会带来真正的责任感 ([链接](https://x.com/swyx/status/2055079344632926603))。他还称赞了 Raycast 最近发布的技术栈文章，说是他职业生涯里看过最好的之一，并建议 devtools 公司多写这类内容：既能向用户展示打磨产品的用心，又能吸引追求 SOTA 的人才，让竞争对手望而却步，同时也能向那些"用不上"的用户解释为什么他们觉得"简单"的需求其实并不简单 ([链接](https://x.com/swyx/status/2054938522034020816))。另外，他还预告了 Latent Space 最新一期播客，嘉宾是 Redpoint 的 Jacob Effron，主题是 AI x 医疗，Swyx 也坦承这是他团队覆盖较少的一个垂直领域 ([链接](https://x.com/swyx/status/2055071896920879236))。

Peter Yang opened a candid question to designers about how to make AI design tools like Claude Design and pencil.dev produce work with actual taste, asking whether there's a "design skill file" worth loading or whether the right move is to always have the agent create a design system first ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2055036366183113110)). He also reacted to today's OpenAI launch, half-joking that he'll let Codex drain his phone battery if it means talking to it on mobile ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2055029260470071606)), and noted that a long-awaited update he'd been chasing finally shipped ([link](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2055024711843950787)).

Roblox 产品负责人 Peter Yang 向设计师们抛出了一个真诚的问题：怎样才能让 Claude Design、pencil.dev 这类 AI 设计工具产出真正"有品味"的作品？是有一个"design skill file"可以加载，还是应该先让 agent 生成一套设计系统再开始 ([链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2055036366183113110))？他还对今天 OpenAI 的发布做出反应，半开玩笑说只要能在手机上跟 Codex 对话，他愿意让它把电池耗光 ([链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2055029260470071606))；另外他也提到，自己等了很久的一个更新终于上线了 ([链接](https://x.com/petergyang/status/2055024711843950787))。

### Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch

Guillermo Rauch showed off `ai-cli`, a new globally-installable npm package that renders images directly in the terminal and gives instant access to every image, video, and text model through Vercel's AI Gateway: `npm i -g ai-cli` then `npx ai-cli image '...'` ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2054989456189648918)). He also highlighted that Notion's new developer platform is built on Vercel Sandbox, pointing out that you can extend Notion natively or bring Notion into your own workflow via their MCP, and called the launch landing page "Next-level" ([link](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2054734851086155778)).

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch 展示了 `ai-cli`，一个可以全局安装的 npm 包，能直接在终端里渲染图片，并通过 Vercel AI Gateway 即时调用所有图像、视频和文本模型，使用方式是 `npm i -g ai-cli`，然后 `npx ai-cli image '...'` ([链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2054989456189648918))。他还提到，Notion 全新的开发者平台就跑在 Vercel Sandbox 上，你既可以原生扩展 Notion，也可以通过他们的 MCP 把 Notion 接入自己的工作流，他还称这次发布的 landing page 做得"Next 级别" ([链接](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2054734851086155778))。

### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Aaron Levie argued that college career services should be aggressively steering technical students toward forward deployed engineer (FDE) roles, which combine deep CS fundamentals, systems thinking, and business acumen, with mandatory fluency in coding agents, MCP, CLIs, and Skills. He predicts thousands of tech companies, consultancies, IT services firms, and large enterprises will all be hiring for this profile, making it one of the clearest career opportunities for highly technical talent right now ([link](https://x.com/levie/status/2054729966630441007)).

Box CEO Aaron Levie 认为，大学的就业指导部门应该积极引导技术方向的学生去争取 forward deployed engineer (FDE) 这类岗位。这种角色需要扎实的 CS 基础、systems thinking 和商业敏感度，同时必须熟练掌握 coding agents、MCP、CLI 和 Skills 等工具。他预测未来会有成千上万的科技公司、咨询公司、IT 服务公司，以及大量中大型企业都在抢这种人，对技术人才来说，这是当下最清晰的职业机会之一 ([链接](https://x.com/levie/status/2054729966630441007))。

### FPV Ventures Partner Nikunj Kothari

Nikunj Kothari delivered two pointed takes. First, any large company still gating its data behind human-only walls needs to go headless or risk irrelevance, because the agent market and usage will be 100x larger than the human one — keep gatekeeping and you'll be 100x less valuable ([link](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054924517135540320)). Second, he wrote a frank thread on venture loyalty: most large-AUM funds simply cannot afford to miss a category, so they will invest in competitors, sometimes at the same stage, and seed portfolios routinely pivot into each other's spaces — founders should treat capital partners as one tool among many and choose accordingly ([link](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054753629173940522)).

FPV Ventures 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 抛出了两个犀利的观点。第一，所有还把数据锁在"只服务人类"这堵墙后的大公司，要么转向 headless，要么就准备被边缘化，因为 agent 市场和使用量会是人类的 100 倍，如果还死守 gatekeeping，你的价值就只剩 1% ([链接](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054924517135540320))。第二，他写了一段关于 VC 忠诚度的真心话：大多数大 AUM 基金根本承受不起错过任何一个赛道，所以他们一定会投竞争对手，有时候甚至是同一轮，而 seed 阶段的 portfolio 公司也常常在 pivot 后撞进彼此的领域。创始人应该把资本伙伴当作工具之一，谨慎选择 ([链接](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2054753629173940522))。

### OpenClaw Founder Peter Steinberger

Peter Steinberger shipped a major OpenClaw release focused on performance, reliability, security, and stability, introducing new automation flows with "crabbox," automated video QA, and what he describes as insane amounts of CI CPU spend ([link](https://x.com/steipete/status/2055026017291370701)). He also flagged that OpenClaw with computer vision is "dangerous" — a tease tied to a new capability demo ([link](https://x.com/steipete/status/2055026331658621323)). Separately, he released mcporter 0.11.0, which he now uses primarily as a more stable browser automation CLI and as a way for agents to test MCPs without restarting harnesses, while noting that code mode adoption in harnesses is gradually reducing the need for it ([link](https://x.com/steipete/status/2054986075232199038)).

OpenClaw 创始人 Peter Steinberger 发布了一个重量级的 OpenClaw 版本，主打性能、可靠性、安全性和稳定性，新增了基于"crabbox"的自动化流程、自动化视频 QA，以及他自嘲"疯狂"的 CI CPU 投入 ([链接](https://x.com/steipete/status/2055026017291370701))。他还预告称，加上 computer vision 后的 OpenClaw"很危险"，是新能力的一次小型展示 ([链接](https://x.com/steipete/status/2055026331658621323))。另外，他还发布了 mcporter 0.11.0，他现在主要把它当作一个更稳定的浏览器自动化 CLI 来用，也方便 agent 在不重启 harness 的情况下测试 MCP，但他也承认随着越来越多 harness 支持 code mode，这个工具的必要性在慢慢下降 ([链接](https://x.com/steipete/status/2054986075232199038))。

### OpenAI

OpenAI launched Codex inside the ChatGPT mobile app in preview, letting users start new work, review outputs, steer execution, and approve next steps from their phone while Codex keeps running on their laptop, Mac mini, or devbox ([link](https://x.com/OpenAI/status/2055016850849993072)). The preview is live today on iOS and Android in all supported regions, with phone-to-Codex support for Windows coming soon ([link](https://x.com/OpenAI/status/2055016852133417389)).

OpenAI 正式将 Codex 接入 ChatGPT 手机端 App，进入预览阶段。用户现在可以在手机上发起新任务、查看输出、引导执行、批准下一步操作，而 Codex 本身仍在你的笔记本、Mac mini 或 devbox 上运行 ([链接](https://x.com/OpenAI/status/2055016850849993072))。预览版今天在所有支持地区的 iOS 和 Android 上线，Windows 上的手机端连接 Codex 功能也将很快上线 ([链接](https://x.com/OpenAI/status/2055016852133417389))。
