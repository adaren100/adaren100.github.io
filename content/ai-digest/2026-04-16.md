### Peter Steinberger (OpenClaw)

Peter Steinberger, the self-described "ClawFather" behind OpenClaw, pushed back hard on security concerns. Four months and thousands of engineering hours later, OpenClaw now has a layered security model: full yolo mode, Docker/OpenShell sandboxing, allow-lists, and per-access exec prompts — all pen-tested by hundreds of security researchers. He also warned that other open-source harnesses ignoring this security work are a real risk, especially as frontier models like GPT 5.4-Cyber gain reverse-engineering capabilities.

[https://x.com/steipete/status/2044423791405924562](https://x.com/steipete/status/2044423791405924562)

OpenClaw 的创始人 Peter Steinberger 驳斥了对安全性的质疑。经过四个月、数千小时的工程投入，OpenClaw 现已具备多层安全体系：完全开放模式、Docker/OpenShell 沙箱、允许名单、以及逐次执行确认弹窗，并经过数百名安全研究员的渗透测试。他还警告称，随着 GPT 5.4-Cyber 等前沿模型的逆向工程能力增强，那些忽视安全工作的开源 harness 项目将是真正的风险所在。

[https://x.com/swyx/status/2044234661644382581](https://x.com/swyx/status/2044234661644382581)

Cognition 顾问 Swyx 庆祝了今天 **Spaces** 的发布——这一概念他三个月前就参与设计。他的框架是：subagent（执行任务的 agent）本质上是优化问题，而能够组合、管理和查询这些 subagent 的"boss agent"才是能力挑战。他还分享了 AI 工程策略：先极致提升能力（capabilitymaxx），再进行蒸馏，称这是突破 AI 帕累托前沿的可靠公式。

### Dan Shipper (CEO, Every)

Every CEO Dan Shipper shared a deep conversation with Eve Bodnia, who is developing **energy-based models (EBMs)** as an alternative to LLMs. The core argument: LLMs process one token at a time and are inherently language-dependent, losing information when applied to problems that are fundamentally numerical or spatial. EBMs instead map all possible outcomes onto a mathematical energy landscape and find the lowest-energy (most probable) answer — more like a bird's-eye view than a step-by-step drive through an unfamiliar city. Bodnia's provocative claim: LLM progress is plateauing, and step-change improvements will require fundamentally different architectures.
[https://x.com/danshipper/status/2044431229504643413](https://x.com/danshipper/status/2044431229504643413)

Every CEO Dan Shipper 分享了与 Eve Bodnia 的深度对话，后者正在开发**能量模型（EBM）**作为 LLM 的替代方案。核心论点是：LLM 逐 token 处理，本质上依赖语言，在面对数字或空间类问题时会损失信息。而 EBM 则将所有可能的结果映射到一个数学能量地形图上，寻找能量最低点（即最可能的答案），更像是鸟瞰全图而非在陌生城市逐步导航。Bodnia 提出了一个挑衅性观点：LLM 的进步正在触顶，要实现跨越式突破需要全然不同的架构。

### Nikunj Kothari (Partner, FPV Ventures)

FPV Ventures partner Nikunj Kothari clarified his earlier take on AI and human clarity: yes, you can give system diagrams to Claude Code and one-shot many tasks, but the *depth of thinking, clarity, and persistence* it takes to get to that point of clarity is still a uniquely human contribution. He acknowledged the "Bitter Lesson" counterargument but held firm — AI hasn't yet learned the lessons that a thoughtful human brings from years of experience.
[https://x.com/nikunj/status/2044489137542332717](https://x.com/nikunj/status/2044489137542332717)

FPV Ventures 合伙人 Nikunj Kothari 澄清了他此前关于 AI 与人类清晰度的观点：是的，你可以把系统架构图交给 Claude Code 并一次性完成很多任务，但达到这种清晰度所需的**深度思考、表达清晰和持续坚持**，仍然是人类独有的贡献。他承认"苦涩教训"这一反驳论点，但仍坚持己见——AI 还没有学到一个有经验的人类经过多年积累才能带来的洞察。
