### Guillermo Rauch, Vercel CEO

Vercel CEO Guillermo Rauch argued that cybersecurity is one of the best benchmarks for superintelligence, calling it the "IQ test" of software engineering. In his view, it's easy for a model to "one-shot an XYZ clone" and impress people, but finding, patching, reversing, and exploiting vulnerabilities demand true reasoning power and "corner thinking" that transcends languages and frameworks — a skill very few humans excel at. He added that seeing Kimi K3 do well here bodes well for open models. [cybersecurity as benchmark](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2078912929714356698)

Vercel首席执行官Guillermo Rauch提出,网络安全是检验超级智能的最佳benchmark之一,堪称软件工程的"智商测试"。在他看来,模型"一把梭生成一个XYZ克隆"去惊艳众人很容易,但漏洞的发现、修补、逆向和利用需要真正的推理能力和"边角思维",这种能力超越了编程语言和框架本身,连人类中都极少有人擅长。他还补充说,Kimi K3在这方面表现出色,对开源模型来说是个好兆头。[网络安全作为benchmark](https://x.com/rauchg/status/2078912929714356698)

### Aaron Levie, Box CEO

Box CEO Aaron Levie made three arguments about the AI landscape. On regulation: with strong open source models just behind the frontier, gatekeeping access to frontier capabilities makes you less secure and less competitive — even if America banned open models, other ecosystems wouldn't, and they'd gain the advantage. On economics: when AI costs drop, spend usually goes up, not down, because cheaper tokens make a wider range of tasks affordable — more code written, more security review, agents running over datasets you couldn't process before. And on diffusion: AI progress is rate-limited by its interaction with the real world — coding was adopted fast because one person can make something work end-to-end from a computer, but drug development, sales, and contracts all need reality to answer back, which is why the applied AI layer holds so much opportunity. [open source regulation](https://x.com/levie/status/2078992778449850769) · [cost and demand](https://x.com/levie/status/2078968158006939716) · [AI diffusion](https://x.com/levie/status/2078864191683969212)

Box首席执行官Aaron Levie就AI行业发表了三个观点。关于监管:在开源模型紧追前沿水平的今天,对前沿模型能力设卡只会让自己更不安全、更没竞争力,就算美国全面禁止开源模型,其他生态也不会跟着禁,反而会因此占据优势。关于经济账:AI成本下降时,总支出通常不降反升,因为token更便宜后,更多任务变得划算了:写更多代码、做更多安全审查、让agent跑以前处理不起的大数据集。关于扩散速度:AI带来的进步终究受制于它与现实世界的交互。编程之所以普及得快,是因为一个人在电脑前就能端到端把事情跑通;而新药研发、销售、合同谈判都需要现实世界的反馈,这也正是应用层AI机会巨大的原因。[开源与监管](https://x.com/levie/status/2078992778449850769) · [成本与需求](https://x.com/levie/status/2078968158006939716) · [AI扩散](https://x.com/levie/status/2078864191683969212)
