### Box CEO Aaron Levie

Box CEO Aaron Levie wrote a long-form post on why unstructured data — contracts, financial documents, medical research, engineering docs — has historically been unusable by machines and rate-limited by manual human review, unlike structured data that could be queried in a database. He argued that today's frontier models (GPT-5.6, Fable 5, Grok 4.5, Muse Spark 1.1, and leading open-weight models), combined with deep domain expertise, now let agents run for minutes or hours across these datasets, unlocking entirely new classes of questions — like full contract risk analysis, deeper investment due diligence, or mining clinical trial data for new insights — not just automating the manual work we already do. https://x.com/levie/status/2075655202724815315

Box CEO Aaron Levie 发表了一篇长文,阐述为什么合同、财务文件、医学研究、工程文档这类非结构化数据,过去一直无法被机器处理,只能依赖人工逐一审阅,这一点与可以直接在数据库里查询的结构化数据形成鲜明对比。他认为,如今的前沿模型(GPT-5.6、Fable 5、Grok 4.5、Muse Spark 1.1 以及一批领先的开源权重模型)叠加深厚的行业专业知识,已经能让 agent 在这些数据集上连续运行几分钟甚至几个小时,从而解锁一整类全新的问题——比如对合同做全面风险分析、对投资标的做更深入的尽职调查、或者从临床试验数据中挖掘新洞见——而不仅仅是把已有的人工工作自动化。

### ML/AI Research Engineer Sebastian Raschka

Sebastian Raschka laid out practical, cost-efficiency guidance for choosing coding models: unless you need Terra Ultra-level performance, a Luna model at a higher effort setting matches or beats it for less; skip everything below Sol High in favor of higher-effort Luna; skip Sol Extra High for Terra Ultra; and the extra cost of Sol Ultra probably isn't worth it over Max. https://x.com/rasbt/status/2075573860796436626

机器学习/AI 研究工程师、作家 Sebastian Raschka 给出了一份实用的编程模型选型性价比指南:除非你确实需要 Terra Ultra 级别的性能,否则把 Luna 模型调到更高的 effort 档位,效果相当甚至更好,价格却更便宜;Sol High 以下的档位都可以放弃,直接用更高 effort 的 Luna;Sol Extra High 可以跳过,直接上 Terra Ultra;而 Sol Ultra 相对 Max 多花的钱,大概率并不值得。
